VLSU: Mapping the Limits of Joint Multimodal
Understanding for AI Safety
VLSU：绘制联合多模态理解在 AI 安全中的局限

Larry Lindsey   Rutika Moharir   Gunnar Lund   Yang Xu   Navid Shiee

Abstract  摘要

Safety evaluation of multimodal foundation models often treats vision and language inputs separately, missing risks from joint interpretation where benign content becomes harmful in combination. Existing approaches also fail to distinguish clearly unsafe content from borderline cases, leading to problematic over-blocking or under-refusal of genuinely harmful content. We present Vision Language Safety Understanding (VLSU), a comprehensive framework to systematically evaluate multimodal safety through fine-grained severity classification and combinatorial analysis across 17 distinct safety patterns. Using a multi-stage pipeline with real-world images and human annotation, we construct a large-scale benchmark of 8,187 samples spanning 15 harm categories. Our evaluation of eleven state-of-the-art models reveals systematic joint understanding failures: while models achieve 90%+ accuracy on clear unimodal safety signals, performance degrades substantially to 20-55% when joint image-text reasoning is required to determine the safety label. Most critically, 34% of errors in joint image-text safety classification occur despite correct classification of the individual modalities, further demonstrating absent compositional reasoning capabilities. Additionally, we find that models struggle to balance refusing unsafe content while still responding to borderline cases that deserve engagement. For example, we find that instruction framing can reduce the over-blocking rate on borderline content from 62.4% to 10.4% in Gemini-1.5, but only at the cost of under-refusing on unsafe content with refusal rate dropping from 90.8% to 53.9%. Overall, our framework exposes weaknesses in joint image-text understanding and alignment gaps in current models, and provides a critical test bed to enable the next milestones in research on robust vision–language safety. 1 Introduction  1 引言

Large vision-language models (VLMs) have achieved remarkable capabilities across applications from fine-grained chart understanding to emerging agentic behavior. As these models are increasingly deployed, ensuring their safety and alignment is critical. While text safety mechanisms are relatively mature (zhang2024safetybench; hartvigsen2022toxigen; gehman2020realtoxicityprompts; ghosh2025aegis2; parrish2022bbq; rottger2024xstest) and image-oriented safety measures exist (helff2025llavaguard), joint vision-language safety remains critically underexplored—representing a significant gap in our understanding of multimodal AI safety.
大型视觉语言模型（VLMs）在从细粒度图表理解到新兴代理行为等应用中取得了显著能力。随着这些模型越来越多地部署，确保其安全性和一致性至关重要。虽然文本安全机制相对成熟（zhang2024safetybench; hartvigsen2022toxigen; gehman2020realtoxicityprompts; ghosh2025aegis2; parrish2022bbq; rottger2024xstest），并且存在面向图像的安全措施（helff2025llavaguard），但联合视觉语言安全仍严重未得到探索——这代表了我们理解多模态 AI 安全中的一个重大空白。

Most current evaluation approaches fail to address a primary challenge introduced by multimodal data: content that is safe in isolation may become harmful when interpreted jointly (wang2025siuo; rottger2025msts). Consider an innocuous image of a rooftop paired with text “I’ve always wondered what falling feels like”—individually benign components that together suggest self-harm intent. Figure 1 (left) shows such examples where models respond helpfully to unsafe queries when the image and text appear safe individually. This failure mode reflects a deeper challenge: unlike unimodal safety where harmful signals are directly observable within a single modality, multimodal safety requires understanding emergent intent that arises through cross-modal interactions.
当前大多数评估方法未能解决多模态数据引入的一个主要挑战：在孤立状态下安全的内容，在联合解释时可能变得有害（wang2025siuo; rottger2025msts）。考虑一个无害的屋顶图像与文本“我一直想知道坠落是什么感觉”配对——这些单独的良性组件组合在一起却暗示了自残意图。图 1（左）展示了这类例子，其中模型在图像和文本单独看起来安全时，会对不安全的查询做出有益的回应。这种失效模式反映了一个更深层次的挑战：与单模态安全（有害信号可直接在单一模态中观察到）不同，多模态安全需要理解通过跨模态交互产生的涌现意图。

The challenge extends beyond detecting harmful combinations. Real-world deployment demands nuanced judgment to distinguish educational content about sensitive topics from genuinely harmful material. A chemistry textbook image paired with “what could we create with these mixtures?” should receive a helpful educational response, while the same image with “how to make poison?” should trigger appropriate safety measures. Existing safety framing lacks the mechanism to distinguish between such educational or informative requests and those with malicious intent. Figure 1 (right) shows such examples where models over-refuse borderline queries that should ideally have received a helpful response.
挑战不仅在于检测有害组合。实际部署需要细致的判断，以区分关于敏感话题的教育内容与真正有害的材料。一张化学教科书图片配上“我们能用这些混合物创造什么？”应该得到有益的教育回应，而同一张图片配上“如何制作毒药？”则应触发适当的安全措施。现有的安全框架缺乏区分此类教育或信息请求与恶意意图的机制。图 1（右）展示了模型过度拒绝本应得到有益回应的边缘查询的例子。

To tackle these challenges, we present VLSU (Vision Language Safety Understanding), a comprehensive benchmark constructed through a new framework that systematically breaks down these two challenges for multimodal safety. Our contributions include:
为应对这些挑战，我们提出了 VLSU（视觉语言安全理解），这是一个通过新框架构建的全面基准，该框架系统地分解了多模态安全中的这两个挑战。我们的贡献包括：

  • •

    VLSU benchmark and framework: 8,187 human-graded samples of image-text pairs that systematically cover 15 harm categories across 17 safety patterns constructed following the proposed framework and including a novel borderline safety severity class. Our dataset proves more challenging than existing benchmarks, leading to 25% lower F1 and exposing failures points invisible to existing evaluations (Section 4.1).


    • VLSU 基准和框架：包含 8,187 个人类评分的图像-文本对样本，系统性地覆盖了 15 个危害类别，这些类别是根据提出的框架构建的 17 种安全模式，并包括一个新颖的边界安全严重性类别。我们的数据集比现有基准更具挑战性，导致 F1 值降低了 25%，并暴露了现有评估中无法发现的失效点（第 4.1 节）。
  • •

    Scalable methodology: Scalable pipeline with systematic multi-stage parameterization for creating image-text pairs with real-world images, avoiding synthetic artifacts while ensuring comprehensive coverage of the multimodal safety space.


    • 可扩展方法：具有系统多阶段参数化的可扩展流程，用于创建包含真实世界图像的图像-文本对，避免合成伪影，同时确保全面覆盖多模态安全空间。
  • •

    Evaluation insights: Our evaluation reveals critical weaknesses across eleven state-of-the-art VLMs across open and closed source, ranging from 4B to 72B (open) model sizes and including latest multimodal reasoning model. Most models achieve strong performance on clearly unsafe data (>>90%+) but suffer substantial degradation when image-text pairs require genuine cross-modal reasoning (20-55%; Section 4.2). Most concerning, 34% of errors occur despite correct individual modality understanding (Section 5) further showing absent compositional reasoning capabilities.


    • 评估见解：我们的评估揭示了十一种最先进的视觉语言模型（VLMs）在开源和闭源方面存在的关键弱点，模型规模从 4B 到 72B（开源）不等，并包括最新的多模态推理模型。大多数模型在明显不安全的数据上表现出色（ >> 90%+），但在图像-文本对需要真正的跨模态推理时性能大幅下降（20-55%；第 4.2 节）。最令人担忧的是，34%的错误发生在单个模态理解正确的情况下（第 5 节），这进一步表明缺乏组合推理能力。

2 Vision-Language Safety Framework
2 视觉-语言安全框架

We design a vision-language safety framework to systematically generate data around the different failure modes in VLMs introduced above. We do this in two key steps: (1) by introducing a new borderline severity class that captures content related to harm categories but does not contain any malicious intent, and (2) combinatorial safety mapping that systematically characterizes how individual modality severity ratings combine into joint safety assessments.
我们设计了一个视觉-语言安全框架，用于系统地生成上述 VLMs 中不同失效模式的数据。我们通过两个关键步骤来实现这一点：(1) 引入一个新的边缘严重性类别，该类别捕获与危害类别相关的内容，但不包含任何恶意意图，以及(2) 组合式安全映射，系统地描述单个模态严重性评级如何组合成联合安全评估。

2.1 Safety Severity Levels: Introducing the Borderline Class
2.1 安全严重性级别：引入边缘类别

Not all safe (or unsafe) queries are equally safe (or unsafe). Safety is a spectrum and should be handled accordingly. VLMs need to be trained to understand the intent of user queries, being able to differentiate the purely unsafe with malicious intent from the cases that refer to harmful topics but in an educational or informative context without the intent of harm amplification. For joint vision-language data, safety assessment must consider: (i) individual modality safety severity, (ii) cross-modal interactions, and (iii) contextual intent. We introduce a new borderline severity class to capture and formally represent these characteristics. This operationalizes our safety framework and guides construction of the VLSU dataset that inherently embeds such distinctions. Based on this, we now have three safety severity levels, defined as:
并非所有安全（或不安全）的查询都是同等安全（或不安全）。安全性是一个连续谱，应相应处理。视觉语言模型需要被训练以理解用户查询的意图，能够区分纯粹具有恶意意图的不安全情况与指涉有害话题但处于教育或信息性语境且无放大伤害意图的情况。对于联合视觉语言数据，安全性评估必须考虑：(i) 单一模态的安全性严重程度，(ii) 跨模态交互，以及 (iii) 语境意图。我们引入一个新的边界严重程度类别来捕捉和正式表示这些特征。这使我们的安全性框架得以操作化，并指导构建 VLSU 数据集，该数据集内嵌了这些区分。基于此，我们现在有三个安全性严重程度级别，定义为：

Unsafe   Following established safety taxonomies (helff2025llavaguard; rottger2025msts; chi2024llama), we use a taxonomy of 15 typical harm categories: violence-related (interpersonal, graphic, animal), weapons, terrorism, self-harm, discrimination, exploitation, fraud, drug abuse, hate speech, jailbreaks, and explicit content. Content is unsafe if it promotes, encourages, or expresses harmful actions or beliefs from any of these categories. Models should refuse to engage with such content.
不安全遵循既定的安全分类法（helff2025llavaguard; rottger2025msts; chi2024llama），我们使用一个包含 15 个典型危害类别的分类法：与暴力相关的（人际、暴力画面、动物）、武器、恐怖主义、自残、歧视、剥削、欺诈、药物滥用、仇恨言论、越狱和露骨内容。如果内容推广、鼓励或表达上述任何类别中的有害行为或信念，则被视为不安全。模型应拒绝与这类内容互动。
Borderline   Borderline content references content from harm categories in the taxonomy, but it doesn’t aim to amplify harm or produce explicit content with malicious intent. Borderline content often occurs in educational, informative, or discussion contexts. Such data should be approached with caution and should receive a helpful response from models.
边缘边缘内容引用了分类体系中的有害类别内容，但它旨在放大伤害或以恶意意图产生明确的有害内容。边缘内容通常出现在教育、信息或讨论环境中。此类数据应谨慎处理，模型应对其作出有益的回应。
Safe   Safe content falls outside harm categories and poses no risk of promoting dangerous behaviors. Models should respond helpfully to such content.
安全安全内容不属于有害类别，且不会促进危险行为。模型应对此类内容作出有益的回应。

2.2 Multimodal Safety Combinatorics
2.2 多模态安全组合器

We formalize joint vision-language safety as a function of individual modality safety severity levels, systematically mapping how image-only and text-only safety assessments combine. For each multimodal query, we define a safety tuple sis_{i}-sts_{t}-sjs_{j} where sis_{i}, sts_{t}, sjs_{j} ∈\in {S, B, U} represent the safe / borderline / unsafe ratings for image-only, text-only, and combined modalities respectively (e.g. S-U-U indicates safe image, unsafe text, unsafe combined). This theoretical space yields 33=273^{3}=27 combinations but during the manual annotation process we find that certain combinations are practically impossible. For example, if the text modality is clearly unsafe the joint label cannot be safe or borderline. After eliminating these non-occurring patterns, we are left with 17 combinations that consistently emerge (Figure 3).
我们将联合视觉-语言安全形式化为个体模态安全严重程度水平的函数，系统地映射了仅图像和仅文本安全评估如何结合。对于每个多模态查询，我们定义一个安全元组 sis_{i} - sts_{t} - sjs_{j} ，其中 sis_{i} 、 sts_{t} 、 sjs_{j} ∈\in {S, B, U}分别代表仅图像、仅文本和组合模态的安全/边缘/不安全评级（例如 S-U-U 表示安全图像、不安全文本、不安全组合）。这个理论空间产生了 33=273^{3}=27 种组合，但在手动标注过程中我们发现某些组合在实际中不可能出现。例如，如果文本模态明显不安全，联合标签不能是安全或边缘。在排除这些不出现的模式后，我们剩下 17 种组合始终出现（图 3）。

The combinations span a critical spectrum, from cases where both unimodal safety signals clearly determine the combined safety rating (e.g. U-U-U), over cases where one modality dominates the determination of the combined safety rating (e.g. S-U-U) to combinations requiring joint multimodal understanding (e.g. S-S-U) where individually safe components become unsafe in combination, as illustrated in Figure 1 (left). This systematic approach enables identification of failure modes invisible to traditional safety evaluation. Unimodal-dominated combinations test whether models detect obvious safety signals, while joint-reasoning combinations assess genuine multimodal understanding. Borderline combinations evaluate fine-grained calibration—preventing both over-blocking of legitimate content (Figure 1 (right)) and under-refusal of harmful content.
这些组合涵盖了关键的范围，从单模态安全信号都明确决定组合安全评级的情况（例如 U-U-U），到单模态主导决定组合安全评级的情况（例如 S-U-U），再到需要联合多模态理解的情况（例如 S-S-U），其中单独安全的组件组合起来会变得不安全，如图 1（左）所示。这种系统方法能够识别传统安全评估无法察觉的失效模式。单模态主导的组合测试模型是否检测到明显的安全信号，而联合推理的组合评估真正的多模态理解能力。边界组合评估细粒度校准——防止合法内容被过度屏蔽（图 1（右））和有害内容被拒绝不足。

3 VLSU Dataset  3 VLSU 数据集

Dataset Generation Pipeline
数据集生成流程

We develop a systematic four-stage pipeline (Figure 2) for VLSU construction that prioritizes realistic multimodal generation while ensuring comprehensive coverage of safety scenarios. Our approach deliberately integrates real-world images to ground the evaluation in authentic visual contexts, moving beyond the limitations of purely synthetic datasets.
我们开发了一个系统化的四阶段流程（图 2）用于构建 VLSU，该流程优先考虑逼真的多模态生成，同时确保全面覆盖安全场景。我们的方法有意整合真实世界图像，将评估置于真实的视觉环境中，超越了纯合成数据集的限制。

Stage 1: Parameterized Image-Concept Generation   We generate diverse image concepts across all TT harm categories, and all three severity levels SiS_{i} through systematic parameterization. Each concept (e.g.,“rooftop of high-rise building”) serves as a semantic anchor for subsequent image retrieval. We employ Gemini-1.5-Pro-002 to generate these concepts conditioned on specific safety categories, their textual description and intended severity requirements, ensuring broad coverage while maintaining semantic coherence.
第一阶段：参数化图像-概念生成 我们通过系统化的参数化方法，在所有 TT 危害类别中生成多样化的图像概念，涵盖所有三种严重程度 SiS_{i} 。每个概念（例如，“高层建筑屋顶”）作为后续图像检索的语义锚点。我们使用 Gemini-1.5-Pro-002 根据特定的安全类别、其文本描述和预期的严重程度要求生成这些概念，确保广泛覆盖的同时保持语义一致性。

Stage 2: Real Image Retrieval   Rather than relying on synthetic image generation, we retrieve authentic images from a large-scale image repository using the generated concepts as search queries. This design choice ensures visual realism (geng2024unmet) and prevents models from exploiting artifacts common in synthetic images. Each retrieved image undergoes de-duplication via perceptual hashing to guarantee uniqueness across the benchmark—no image appears twice in VLSU.
阶段 2：真实图像检索与合成图像生成不同，我们使用生成的概念作为搜索查询，从大规模图像库中检索真实图像。这一设计选择确保了视觉真实性（geng2024unmet），并防止模型利用合成图像中常见的伪影。每个检索到的图像都通过感知哈希进行去重，以保证在基准测试中的唯一性——VLSU 中没有任何图像会重复出现。

Stage 3: Context-Driven Query Synthesis   The combination pipeline synthesizes queries by jointly conditioning on: (i) the retrieved image, (ii) target text severity StS_{t}, (iii) intended joint severity SjS_{j}, (iv) stylistic variations YY, and (v) token length constraints LL. This multi-dimensional parameterization enables systematic exploration across the entire intended safety spectrum while maintaining natural language diversity. Crucially, the synthesis process considers the image content to generate contextually grounded queries that expose joint understanding requirements.
第三阶段：基于上下文的查询合成 组合管道通过联合调节以下参数合成查询：(i)检索到的图像，(ii)目标文本严重性 StS_{t} ，(iii)预期联合严重性 SjS_{j} ，(iv)风格变化 YY ，以及(v)标记长度约束 LL 。这种多维参数化能够在保持自然语言多样性的同时，系统地探索整个预期安全范围。关键在于，合成过程考虑图像内容以生成基于上下文的查询，从而揭示联合理解需求。

Stage 4: Multi-Stage Human Annotation   A rigorous annotation protocol grounds each sample in policy-driven labels. Human annotators independently assess: (i) image-only safety, (ii) text-only safety, and (iii) joint image-text safety. This triple annotation enables fine-grained analysis of where safety signals originate and how they combine. We have three expert annotations for each sample verified through additional manual review. Additional details on the annotation process and guidelines is provided in Appendix B

Dataset Statistics and Composition

VLSU comprises 8,187 total samples of image-text pairs distributed across our framework’s 17 severity combinations and 15 harm categories. The dataset achieves balanced representation across severity levels: 2,186 (26%) safe combinations, 3,312 (41%) borderline combinations, and 2,689 (33%) unsafe combinations. To ensure balanced evaluation, we include substantial safe content, addressing a critical gap in existing benchmarks that focus exclusively on unsafe scenarios. This distribution enables robust evaluation across the full safety spectrum rather than focusing solely on extreme cases. Each sample employs a unique real image ensuring diverse contexts. The systematic parameterization yields comprehensive coverage across harm categories and combinatorial patterns, with queries spanning multiple stylistic variations (formal, casual, indirect), token lengths (concise to verbose), and contextual framings (educational, malicious, ambiguous). More details on dataset composition and statistics are provided in Appendix A.3.

6 Related Work  6 相关工作

Unimodal Safety Benchmarks
单模态安全基准

Most of the early work in safety benchmarks focused on text-only models’ safety. Naturally, text safety benchmarks have matured over recent years across several safety aspects such as toxicity (zhang2024safetybench; hartvigsen2022toxigen; gehman2020realtoxicityprompts; ghosh2025aegis2), bias (parrish2022bbq) and over-blocking (rottger2024xstest). Recently, image-safety benchmarks have also been introduced covering specific aspects of image safety like violence constantin2022affect, hate kiela2021hateful, harmful object detection ha2023hod. qu2025unsafebench recently explored generation of unsafe synthetic images to offset cost of data collection.
早期关于安全基准的研究大多集中在纯文本模型的安全性上。自然而然，近年来文本安全基准在毒性（zhang2024safetybench; hartvigsen2022toxigen; gehman2020realtoxicityprompts; ghosh2025aegis2）、偏见（parrish2022bbq）和过度屏蔽（rottger2024xstest）等多个安全方面逐渐成熟。最近，图像安全基准也被引入，涵盖了图像安全的特定方面，如暴力（constantin2022affect）、仇恨（kiela2021hateful）、有害物体检测（ha2023hod）。qu2025unsafebench 最近探索了生成不安全的合成图像，以抵消数据收集的成本。

Multimodal Safety Benchmarks
多模态安全基准

Safety benchmarks for multimodal models remain relatively nascent. LlavaGuard helff2025llavaguard approaches image safety as a natural unimodal safety extension by not incorporating explicit query context. Rather, they pair images with text-based policy that is used to build an image guardrail model. MMSafetyBench (liu2024mmsafetybench) is one of the early works that focuses on safety of images along with textual queries. However, the images are synthetically generated and the text queries are templated drastically constraining the diversity of potential multimodal queries. VLSBench hu2025vlsbench constructed a challenging image-text safety benchmark by removing any unsafe-looking text from the pair, requiring models to explicitly understand harm in the image content to do well. Even in this data, 67% of images still remain synthetic in VLSBench and the changes to text queries are templated. In contrast, in our work, we develop a scalable data generation pipeline that sources all real-world images and pairs them with grounded, contextual and natural-sounding text queries. Our dataset is more than 5×\times and 4×\times larger than MMSafetyBench and VLSBench respectively.

MOSSBench li2025mossbench studies over-sensitivity but focused on a narrow aspect within multimodal safety where models tend to block safe looking queries because of specific unsafe attributes added to the image. SIUO wang2025siuo and MSTS rottger2025msts look at another specific aspect where inputs are safe but the joint meaning could be unsafe. These datasets due to their limited focus are much smaller in size: 300, 167 and 400 samples respectively. While these works focused on some particular cases within multimodal safety, we develop a formal vision-language safety framework that allows us to map all such potential combinations and understand model’s behavior in a more fine-grained manner across them.
MOSSBench li2025mossbench 研究了过度敏感问题，但专注于多模态安全中的一个狭窄方面，即模型由于图像中添加了特定的不安全属性而倾向于阻止安全的查询。SIUO wang2025siuo 和 MSTS rottger2025msts 则考察了另一个特定方面，即输入是安全的，但联合意义可能是不安全的。由于这些数据集的聚焦范围有限，它们的大小也相对较小：分别为 300、167 和 400 个样本。虽然这些研究关注了多模态安全中的一些特定案例，我们开发了一个正式的视觉-语言安全框架，该框架使我们能够映射所有此类潜在组合，并在更细粒度的层面上理解模型在这些组合中的行为。

7 Conclusion  7 结论

We introduce VLSU, a comprehensive multimodal safety benchmark and framework that exposes critical gaps in current vision-language models. Our systematic framework, along with newly introduced borderline severity level, reveals that models excel at unimodal-dominated safety signals but fail dramatically when joint reasoning is required. Furthermore, we observe models either over-refuse borderline data or under-refuse unsafe content, pointing to multimodal safety alignment gaps. The performance ceiling observed even with structured prompting suggests that inference-time interventions cannot compensate for inherent model deficiencies. This demonstrates that current models lack genuine multimodal safety understanding, relying instead on superficial cues either in unimodal safety signals or in instructional prompts. VLSU enables systematic evaluation of these previously hidden vulnerabilities, providing the research community with a principled benchmark for developing robust multimodal safety in VLMs.
我们介绍了 VLSU，这是一个全面的多模态安全基准和框架，揭示了当前视觉语言模型中的关键缺陷。我们的系统框架，连同新引入的边界严重程度级别，表明模型在单模态主导的安全信号上表现出色，但在需要联合推理时却表现糟糕。此外，我们观察到模型要么过度拒绝边界数据，要么不足够拒绝不安全内容，这指向了多模态安全对齐的缺陷。即使使用结构化提示观察到的性能上限表明，推理时的干预措施无法弥补模型固有的缺陷。这表明当前模型缺乏真正的多模态安全理解，而是依赖于单模态安全信号或指令提示中的表面线索。VLSU 能够系统地评估这些之前隐藏的漏洞，为研究界提供了一个原则性的基准，用于开发 VLMs 中的鲁棒多模态安全。

C.1.1 Overview and Methodology
C.1.1 概述和方法论

Stage 1 generates diverse image concepts across all safety categories and severity levels through a two-step process: (1) generating basic concepts, and (2) augmenting these concepts to increase variety. To maximize the diversity of retrieved images suitable for multimodal safety evaluation, we employ a counter-intuitive generation strategy: when generating safe image concepts, we prompt the model to simultaneously produce unsafe questions that could be asked about those images (and vice versa). This ensures that safe images can still serve as contexts for harmful queries. Only the image concepts are retained for downstream retrieval; the generated questions are discarded.
第一阶段通过两步过程在所有安全类别和严重程度级别上生成多样化的图像概念：（1）生成基本概念；（2）增强这些概念以增加多样性。为了最大化适合多模态安全评估的检索图像的多样性，我们采用了一种反直觉的生成策略：在生成安全图像概念时，我们提示模型同时生成关于这些图像可能提出的不安全问题（反之亦然）。这确保了安全图像仍然可以作为有害查询的上下文。仅保留图像概念用于下游检索；生成的问​​题被丢弃。

C.4 Post-processing  C.4 后处理

All the data is graded by an autograder, a GPT-4o model, optimized using DSPy khattab2023dspy for unimodal and multimodal severity grading. The optimizer is run on a gold set of 170 samples, evenly distributed across harm categories, and annotated by 7 expert annotators. We use autograding to filter down to a 10,000 sample set maintaining an even distribution of severity levels. This set is then human annotated using detailed guidelines and annotation instructions. Details on the human annotation process and guidelines is provided in section B.
所有数据均由一个自动评分器进行评分，该评分器是一个 GPT-4o 模型，使用 DSPy khattab2023dspy 进行单模态和多模态严重程度评分的优化。优化器在 170 个样本的金数据集上运行，这些样本均匀分布在伤害类别中，并由 7 位专家标注者进行标注。我们使用自动评分来筛选出 10,000 个样本集，保持严重程度级别的均匀分布。然后使用详细的指南和标注说明对这组数据进行人工标注。关于人工标注过程和指南的详细信息，请参见 B 部分。
