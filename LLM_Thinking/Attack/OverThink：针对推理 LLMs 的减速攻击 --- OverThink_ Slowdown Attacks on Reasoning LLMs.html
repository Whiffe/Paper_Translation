OverThink: Slowdown Attacks on Reasoning LLMs
OverThink：针对推理 LLMs 的减速攻击

Abhinav Kumar University of Massachusetts Amherst Jaechul Roh University of Massachusetts Amherst Ali Naseh University of Massachusetts Amherst Marzena Karpinska
Mohit Iyyer University of Massachusetts Amherst Amir Houmansadr University of Massachusetts Amherst Eugene Bagdasarian University of Massachusetts Amherst
Abstract  摘要

We increase overhead for applications that rely on reasoning LLMs—we force models to spend an amplified number of reasoning tokens, i.e., “overthink”, to respond to the user query while providing contextually correct answers. The adversary performs an OverThink attack by injecting decoy reasoning problems into the public content that is used by the reasoning LLM (e.g., for RAG applications) during inference time. Due to the nature of our decoy problems (e.g., a Markov Decision Process), modified texts do not violate safety guardrails. We evaluated our attack across closed-(OpenAI o1, o1-mini, o3-mini) and open-(DeepSeek R1) weights reasoning models on the FreshQA and SQuAD datasets. Our results show up to 18×\times× slowdown on FreshQA dataset and 46×\times× slowdown on SQuAD dataset. The attack also shows high transferability across models. To protect applications, we discuss and implement defenses leveraging LLM-based and system design approaches. Finally, we discuss societal, financial, and energy impacts of OverThink attack which could amplify the costs for third-party applications operating reasoning models.
我们增加了依赖推理 LLMs 的应用的开销——我们迫使模型花费更多的推理 token，即“过度思考”，来响应用户查询，同时提供上下文正确的答案。攻击者通过在推理 LLM（例如，用于 RAG 应用）在推理期间使用的公共内容中注入诱饵推理问题来执行 OverThink 攻击。由于我们诱饵问题的性质（例如，一个马尔可夫决策过程），修改后的文本不会违反安全护栏。我们在 FreshQA 和 SQuAD 数据集上对封闭式（OpenAI o1, o1-mini, o3-mini）和开放式（DeepSeek R1）权重推理模型评估了我们的攻击。我们的结果显示，在 FreshQA 数据集上最多有 18 ×\times× 的减速，在 SQuAD 数据集上最多有 46 ×\times× 的减速。该攻击还显示出跨模型的高可迁移性。为了保护应用程序，我们讨论并实施了利用基于 LLM 和系统设计方法的防御措施。最后，我们讨论了 OverThink 攻击可能带来的社会、经济和能源影响，这些影响可能会增加运行推理模型的第三方应用程序的成本。

1 Introduction  1 引言

Inference-time reasoning boosts large language model (LLM) performance across a broad range of tasks, inspiring a new generation of reasoning LLMs like OpenAI o1 (Jaech et al., 2024) and DeepSeek R1 (Guo et al., 2025). While initially geared towards solving complex mathematical problems, reasoning LLMs are now integrated for general public use in apps like ChatGPT and DeepSeek. For instance, Microsoft has already integrated o1 into Copilot  (Warren, 2025) and made it freely available to its users.
推理时推理提升了大型语言模型（LLM）在广泛任务中的性能，催生了新一代推理 LLM，如 OpenAI 的 o1（Jaech 等人，2024 年）和 DeepSeek R1（Guo 等人，2025 年）。虽然最初旨在解决复杂的数学问题，但推理 LLM 现在已集成到 ChatGPT 和 DeepSeek 等应用程序中供公众使用。例如，微软已将 o1 集成到 Copilot（Warren，2025 年）中，并免费提供给其用户。

Internally, reasoning models produce chain-of-thought sequences, i.e., reasoning tokens, that help in generating the final output. Having access to reasoning tokens is not necessary for the users of an application utilizing reasoning LLMs. Nevertheless, generated reasoning and output tokens impact the cost of inference by increasing the time, energy, and financial overhead for every query. Applications that use reasoning LLM APIs are charged for generating both reasoning and the answer.
在内部，推理模型产生思维链序列，即推理令牌，这些令牌有助于生成最终输出。对于使用推理 LLM 的应用程序的用户来说，访问推理令牌并非必要。然而，生成的推理和输出令牌通过增加每个查询的时间、能源和财务开销来影响推理成本。使用推理 LLM API 的应用程序会为生成推理和答案支付费用。

In this paper, we propose an OverThink attack111Code available at: https://github.com/akumar2709/OVERTHINK_public.
代码可在：https://github.com/akumar2709/OVERTHINK_public。
在本文中，我们提出了一种 OverThink 攻击 1 that forces a reasoning LLM to spend an amplified number of reasoning tokens for an (unmodified) user input, without changing the expected answer (and therefore undetectable to the querying user).222Some service providers, e.g.,  OpenAI (2025c), even protect reasoning tokens by hiding them from the output, making modifications to the chain-of-thought difficult to observe.
一些服务提供商，例如 OpenAI（2025c），甚至通过隐藏推理令牌来保护它们，使得对思维链的修改难以观察。
该攻击迫使推理 LLM 为（未修改的）用户输入花费更多的推理令牌，而不会改变预期答案（因此对查询用户来说是无法检测的）。 2 Our attack is a form of indirect prompt injection that exploits the reasoning model’s reliance on inference-time compute scaling. OverThink is different from existing prompt injections (Perez and Ribeiro, 2022; Apruzzese et al., 2023; Greshake et al., 2023) in that while previous attacks aim to alter the answer itself, OverThink aims to instead increase the number of reasoning tokens without any impact on the final answer. OverThink impacts many common applications of reasoning models, such as retrieval-augmented generation, which depend on (often unvetted) public texts such as social media posts and Wikipedia articles that are vulnerable to adversarial modification.
我们的攻击是一种间接提示注入形式，它利用了推理模型对推理时计算规模依赖性。OverThink 与现有的提示注入（Perez 和 Ribeiro，2022；Apruzzese 等人，2023；Greshake 等人，2023）不同，因为之前的攻击旨在改变答案本身，而 OverThink 旨在增加推理令牌的数量，而对最终答案没有任何影响。OverThink 影响了许多推理模型的常见应用，例如检索增强生成，这些应用依赖于（通常未经审核的）公共文本，如社交媒体帖子和高维百科文章，这些文本容易受到对抗性修改。

The adversary can use OverThink for various adversarial intentions, such as denial-of-service (already a significant problem for reasoning LLMs (Parvini, 2025)), amplified expenses for apps built on reasoning APIs, and slowdown of user inferences. We discuss the consequences of OverThink, including financial costs, energy consumption, and ethics in Section 3.3.
攻击者可以利用 OverThink 来实现多种对抗性目的，例如拒绝服务（这对推理 LLMs 已经是一个重大问题（Parvini，2025 年））、增加基于推理 API 构建的应用程序的成本，以及减慢用户推理速度。我们在第 3.3 节讨论了 OverThink 的后果，包括财务成本、能源消耗和伦理问题。

Figure 1 depicts the OverThink attack. We assume that the user asks an application that uses a reasoning LLM a question that could be answered by using adversary-controlled public sources, e.g., web pages, forums, wikis, or documents. The key technique used in OverThink is to inject some computationally demanding decoy problem (e.g., Markov Decision Processes (MDP) or Sudoku problems) into the source that would be supplied as context to the reasoning LLM during inference.
图 1 描绘了 OverThink 攻击。我们假设用户向使用推理 LLM 的应用程序提出一个问题，这个问题可以通过使用攻击者控制的公共来源来回答，例如网页、论坛、维基或文档。OverThink 使用的关键技术是在源中注入一些计算密集型的诱饵问题（例如马尔可夫决策过程（MDP）或数独问题），这些源将在推理过程中作为上下文提供给推理 LLM。

Our hypothesis is that reasoning models are trained to solve problems and follow instructions they discover, even if it does not align with the user’s query in the context, as long as they do not contradict safety guardrails. In fact, our decoy problems are intentionally benign, yet they cause high computational overheads for a reasoning LLM. Furthermore, a user reading the compromised source will still be able to find the answer manually, and decoys could be ignored or considered as Internet junk (e.g., clickbaits, search engine optimization, hidden advertisements).
我们的假设是，推理模型被训练用来解决它们发现的问题并遵循指令，即使这与用户在上下文中的查询不一致，只要它们不违反安全护栏。事实上，我们的诱饵问题是有意设计成无害的，但它们会给推理 LLM 带来很高的计算开销。此外，阅读被篡改的源代码的用户仍然能够手动找到答案，而且诱饵可以被忽略或被视为互联网垃圾（例如，点击诱饵、搜索引擎优化、隐藏广告）。

Our attack contains three key stages: (1) picking a decoy problem that results in a large number of reasoning tokens, but will not trigger safety filters; (2) integrating selected decoys into a compromised source (e.g., a wiki page) by either modifying the problem to fit the context (context-aware) or by injecting a general template (context-agnostic); and, (3) optimizing the decoy tasks using an in-context learning genetic (ICL-Genetic) algorithm to select contexts with decoys that provide the highest reasoning tokens and maintain stealthiness in the answers to the user.
我们的攻击包含三个关键阶段：(1) 选择一个会导致大量推理 token 但不会触发安全过滤器的诱饵问题；(2) 通过修改问题以适应上下文（上下文感知）或注入通用模板（上下文无关）的方式，将选定的诱饵集成到受感染的源（例如，维基页面）中；(3) 使用上下文学习遗传（ICL-Genetic）算法优化诱饵任务，以选择提供最高推理 token 的诱饵上下文，并在对用户的回答中保持隐蔽性。

Our experimental results show that OverThink significantly increases the reasoning complexity of reasoning LLMs across different attack types, models and datasets. For the o1 model, context-agnostic attack shows an increase of 46×\times× in reasoning tokens on SQuAD dataset. For FreshQA dataset, our ICL-Genetic (Agnostic) attack results in the largest increase, with an 18×\times× rise in reasoning tokens, while our Context-Agnostic and Context-Aware attacks cause 9.7×\times× and over 2.0×\times× increases, respectively. Similarly, for DeepSeek-R1 we see an increase of up to 20×\times× on SQuAD dataset. For FreshQA dataset, the ICL-Genetic (Agnostic) attack leads to a more than 10×\times× increase in reasoning tokens, with other attacks also causing substantial amplification. Our results demonstrate that ICL-based attacks, particularly Context-Agnostic ICL-Genetic attacks, effectively and consistently disrupt reasoning efficiency across multiple reasoning LLMs, highlighting the robustness of our attack methods. Finally, we discuss how simple defenses like filtering and paraphrasing can mitigate our attack and argue that application developers should always deploy them when leveraging reasoning LLMs.
我们的实验结果表明，OverThink 显著增加了推理 LLMs 在不同攻击类型、模型和数据集上的推理复杂度。对于 o1 模型，在 SQuAD 数据集上，上下文无关攻击使推理 token 数量增加了 46 ×\times× 。在 FreshQA 数据集上，我们的 ICL-Genetic（Agnostic）攻击导致推理 token 数量增幅最大，增加了 18 ×\times× ，而我们的上下文无关攻击和上下文感知攻击分别使推理 token 数量增加了 9.7 ×\times× 和超过 2.0 ×\times× 。类似地，在 DeepSeek-R1 上，我们在 SQuAD 数据集上观察到推理 token 数量增加了高达 20 ×\times× 。在 FreshQA 数据集上，ICL-Genetic（Agnostic）攻击使推理 token 数量增加了超过 10 ×\times× ，其他攻击也造成了显著的放大效果。我们的结果表明，基于 ICL 的攻击，特别是上下文无关的 ICL-Genetic 攻击，能够有效且一致地破坏多个推理 LLMs 的推理效率，突显了我们攻击方法的鲁棒性。最后，我们讨论了简单的防御措施（如过滤和释义）如何缓解我们的攻击，并主张应用开发者在利用推理 LLMs 时应始终部署这些防御措施。

2 Background and Related Work
2 背景与相关工作

2.1 Reasoning in LLM  2.1 LLM 中的推理

Language models (LMs) predict the probability distribution of words in a sequence, allowing them to comprehend and generate human-like text. The scaling of these models (Merity et al., 2016; Devlin et al., 2018; Brown et al., 2020; Mehta et al., 2023; Zhao et al., 2023) enabled modern LLMs to excel at complex tasks, but faced token-based cost challenges for complex tasks (Liao and Vargas, 2024; Han et al., 2024; Wang et al., 2024).
语言模型（LMs）预测序列中单词的概率分布，使其能够理解和生成类似人类的文本。这些模型的扩展（Merity 等人，2016 年；Devlin 等人，2018 年；Brown 等人，2020 年；Mehta 等人，2023 年；Zhao 等人，2023 年）使现代 LLMs 能够在复杂任务中表现出色，但面临基于 token 的复杂任务成本挑战（Liao 和 Vargas，2024 年；Han 等人，2024 年；Wang 等人，2024 年）。

Chain-of-thought (CoT) prompting (Wei et al., 2022; Kojima et al., 2022), on the other hand, guides LLMs to generate intermediate reasoning steps in natural language that lead to more accurate and interpretable outcomes, which has significantly improved performance across various benchmarks (Sun et al., 2023). Tree-of-thought (ToT) (Yao et al., 2024) generalizes CoT by exploring multiple reasoning paths in a tree structure T𝑇Titalic_T, allowing correcting errors by revisiting earlier decisions. Recent models like DeepSeek-R1 (Guo et al., 2025) specifically utilize large-scale reinforcement learning (RL) with the collection of long CoT examples to improve reasoning capabilities. Models that generate reasoning before producing the final answer are called reasoning LLMs (see Figure 2).
思维链 (CoT) 提示 (Wei 等人, 2022; Kojima 等人, 2022) 另一方面，指导 LLMs 生成自然语言的中级推理步骤，从而得出更准确和可解释的结果，这显著提高了在各种基准测试中的性能 (Sun 等人, 2023)。思维树 (ToT) (Yao 等人, 2024) 通过在树状结构中探索多条推理路径来推广 CoT TTitalic_T ，允许通过回顾早期决策来纠正错误。像 DeepSeek-R1 (Guo 等人, 2025) 这样最近的模型特别利用大规模强化学习 (RL) 和长 CoT 示例的收集来提高推理能力。在生成最终答案之前生成推理的模型称为推理 LLMs（见图 2）。

2.2 Reasoning Model Deployment
2.2 推理模型部署

Reasoning models are already deployed in general-use applications, e.g., ChatGPT and DeepSeek chat, and recently Copilot (Warren, 2025). These applications often integrate reasoning models into their services by making API calls to service providers like OpenAI, Azure, Fireworks, or DeepSeek, which host the models. These models are accessed via APIs, with pricing based on token usage. Output tokens, which include reasoning tokens (visible in DeepSeek-R1 but not in o1 or o1-mini), are significantly more expensive than input tokens, emphasizing the need for efficient token management. For instance, the o1 model charges $15.00 per million input tokens and $ 60.00 per million output tokens (OpenAI, 2025a). In contrast, DeepSeek offers a more economical pricing structure, with input tokens priced at $3.00 per million and output tokens at $2.19 per million (DeepSeek, 2025). DeepSeek’s model weights are open and could be locally deployed. Nevertheless, in both local deployment and API access, applications will pay for reasoning, either directly, e.g., API access, or indirectly, e.g., by requiring more GPUs to support the increased load.
推理模型已部署于通用应用中，例如 ChatGPT 和 DeepSeek 聊天，以及最近发布的 Copilot（Warren，2025 年）。这些应用通常通过向 OpenAI、Azure、Fireworks 或 DeepSeek 等服务提供商（这些提供商托管着模型）发起 API 调用，将推理模型集成到其服务中。这些模型通过 API 访问，价格基于 token 使用量。输出 token（包括推理 token，在 DeepSeek-R1 中可见但在 o1 或 o1-mini 中不可见）比输入 token 贵得多，这突出了高效 token 管理的必要性。例如，o1 模型每百万输入 token 收费 15.00 美元，每百万输出 token 收费 60.00 美元（OpenAI，2025a）。相比之下，DeepSeek 提供更经济的定价结构，输入 token 每百万 3.00 美元，输出 token 每百万 2.19 美元（DeepSeek，2025 年）。DeepSeek 的模型权重是开放的，可以本地部署。然而，无论是在本地部署还是 API 访问中，应用都将为推理付费，要么直接付费，例如通过 API 访问，要么间接付费，例如通过要求更多 GPU 来支持增加的负载。

Crucially for our threat model, user-facing applications like ChatGPT, Copilot, and DeepSeek chat do not charge users per query or amount of reasoning, instead providing free or fixed-cost access.
对于我们威胁模型而言，ChatGPT、Copilot 和 DeepSeek 聊天等面向用户的应用程序不会按查询次数或推理量收费，而是提供免费或固定费用访问。

2.3 The Importance of Operational Cost
2.3 运营成本的重要性

As AI adoption grows, recent studies (Samsi et al., 2023; Luccioni et al., 2024; Varoquaux et al., 2024) are shifting the focus from training costs to the computational and environmental overhead of inference (Samsi et al., 2023). Large-scale models, particularly multipurpose generative models, consume significantly more energy than task-specific models, with inference requiring 0.002kWh for text classification and up to 2.9kWh for image generation per 1,000 queries (Luccioni et al., 2024). Although training modes like BLOOMz-7B require 5,686 kWh, inference surpasses training costs after 592 million queries, making large-scale deployment a major energy consumer (Patterson et al., 2022).
随着人工智能的普及，近期研究（Samsi 等人，2023；Luccioni 等人，2024；Varoquaux 等人，2024）将重点从训练成本转移到推理的计算和环境开销（Samsi 等人，2023）。大规模模型，特别是多用途生成模型，比特定任务的模型消耗更多能量，推理每 1000 次查询需要 0.002 千瓦时用于文本分类，最高可达 2.9 千瓦时用于图像生成（Luccioni 等人，2024）。尽管像 BLOOMz-7B 这样的训练模式需要 5686 千瓦时，但在 5.92 亿次查询后，推理成本会超过训练成本，使得大规模部署成为主要的能源消耗者（Patterson 等人，2022）。

2.4 Related Attacks  2.4 相关攻击

Prompt Injection.  In prompt injection attacks, an adversary manipulates the input prompt of LLMs to influence the generated output. This manipulation can either directly alter the input prompt (Perez and Ribeiro, 2022; Apruzzese et al., 2023) or indirectly inject malicious prompts into external sources retrieved by the model (Greshake et al., 2023). By contrast, our work introduces a novel attack vector where the adversary’s goal is not to provoke harmful output (that can trigger jailbreaking defenses (Sharma et al., 2025; Zaremba et al., 2025)) but to increase the number of reasoning tokens, thereby inflating the financial cost of using deployed reasoning models.
提示注入。在提示注入攻击中，攻击者通过操纵 LLMs 的输入提示来影响生成的输出。这种操纵可以直接改变输入提示（Perez 和 Ribeiro，2022；Apruzzese 等人，2023）或间接地将恶意提示注入模型检索的外部来源（Greshake 等人，2023）。相比之下，我们的工作引入了一种新颖的攻击向量，攻击者的目标不是引发有害输出（这可能触发越狱防御（Sharma 等人，2025；Zaremba 等人，2025）），而是增加推理 token 的数量，从而增加使用部署推理模型的财务成本。

Denial-of-service (DoS) Attacks.  DoS attacks come from networking and systems research (Bellardo and Savage, 2003; Heftrig et al., 2024; Martin et al., 2004). The first DoS attacks on ML models (Shumailov et al., 2021) focused on maximizing energy consumption while not preserving correctness of user output. Other attacks (Gao et al., 2024b; Chen et al., 2022; Gao et al., 2024a; Geiping et al., 2024) fine-tune LLMs with malicious data or create malicious prompts to generate outputs that are visible to users and can be easily flagged by them. Our attack aims to not modify the LLM answer, but instead increase the hidden reasoning components of the output. Zaremba et al. (2025) and Chen et al. (2024); Wang et al. (2025) observed that reasoning LLMs tend to spend excessive time on simple problems or reducing safety guardrails, referring to this as nerd sniping and overthinking, respectively. In contrast, we propose indirect prompt injection attacks with decoys targeting amplification of inference cost by increasing the reasoning tokens generated by these models while maintaining answer stealthiness.
拒绝服务（DoS）攻击。DoS 攻击源自网络和系统研究（Bellardo 和 Savage，2003；Heftrig 等人，2024；Martin 等人，2004）。针对 ML 模型的首次 DoS 攻击（Shumailov 等人，2021）专注于最大化能耗，同时不保证用户输出的正确性。其他攻击（Gao 等人，2024b；Chen 等人，2022；Gao 等人，2024a；Geiping 等人，2024）通过恶意数据微调 LLMs 或创建恶意提示来生成用户可见且易于被用户标记的输出。我们的攻击旨在不修改 LLM 的答案，而是增加输出的隐藏推理组件。Zaremba 等人（2025）和 Chen 等人（2024）；Wang 等人（2025）观察到推理 LLMs 倾向于在简单问题上花费过多时间或降低安全护栏，分别称之为 nerd sniping 和 overthinking。相比之下，我们提出了一种带有诱饵的间接提示注入攻击，旨在通过增加这些模型生成的推理 token 来放大推理成本，同时保持答案的隐蔽性。

3 The OverThink Attack  3OverThink 攻击

We focus on applications that use reasoning LLMs on untrusted data. Our attack aims to increase inference costs by generating unnecessary reasoning tokens while maintaining accuracy on generated answers. Reasoning tokens are often hidden or unimportant for simple tasks, whereas generated answers are presented to the user and may be flagged by the user. Our slowdown attack is inspired by algorithmic complexity attacks (Crosby and Wallach, 2003) and leverages indirect prompt injection (Greshake et al., 2023) to modify model’s behavior.
我们关注使用推理 LLMs 处理不可信数据的应用。我们的攻击旨在通过生成不必要的推理 token 来增加推理成本，同时保持生成答案的准确性。推理 token 通常对简单任务隐藏或不重要，而生成答案会展示给用户，并可能被用户标记。我们的减速攻击灵感来源于算法复杂度攻击（Crosby 和 Wallach，2003），并利用间接提示注入（Greshake 等人，2023）来修改模型行为。

3.1 Threat Model.  3.1 威胁模型。

Attack’s Potential Scenarios.  Users might send questions to chatbots like chatGPT and DeepSeek, or AI assistants like CoPilot that already supports reasoning LLM. These applications can retrieve information from untrusted external sources such as webpages or documents and provide them as inputs to the LLM along with user’s query. While users enjoy an application for free or at a fixed cost, the application is responsible for costs associated with LLM answer generation and reasoning. An adversary can target to modify a webpage that the application retrieves data from or alter documents or emails used by an AI assistant. In all these cases, the user invests in getting the right answer on their query but might not be interested in detailed inspection of the source nor access to reasoning. This makes these scenarios a perfect candidate for our proposed attack.
攻击的潜在场景。用户可能会向 chatGPT 和 DeepSeek 等聊天机器人或 CoPilot 等已支持推理 LLM 的 AI 助手发送问题。这些应用可以从网页或文档等不可信的外部来源获取信息，并将这些信息连同用户的查询一起作为输入提供给 LLM。虽然用户免费或以固定成本使用这些应用，但应用需要负责与 LLM 答案生成和推理相关的成本。攻击者可以针对应用检索数据的网页进行修改，或更改 AI 助手使用的文档或电子邮件。在所有这些情况下，用户投资于获取他们查询的正确答案，但可能不感兴趣于详细检查来源或访问推理过程。这使得这些场景成为我们提出的攻击的完美候选。

Adversary’s Target.  In this attack, the adversary targets the application that uses reasoning LLMs on external resources. Unlike existing prompt injection attacks, the adversary does not target the user directly, e.g. modify outputs; instead, the focus is on increasing application’s costs to operate the reasoning model.
攻击者的目标。在这种攻击中，攻击者针对使用推理 LLMs 的外部资源的应用。与现有的提示注入攻击不同，攻击者不直接针对用户，例如修改输出；相反，重点是增加应用程序运行推理模型的成本。

Adversary’s Objectives.  The adversary aims to increase the target LLM’s reasoning tokens for a specific class of user queries that rely on external context, which is integrated into the final input to the LLM. These attacks are applicable to instructions that depend on such external information (e.g., ”summarize what people are saying about NVIDIA stock after DeepSeek”) but not to context-independent instructions (e.g., ”write a story about unicorns”). By manipulating the external context, the adversary ensures that the LLM answers the user’s original query correctly while omitting any trace of the context manipulation.
攻击者的目标。攻击者旨在增加目标 LLM 针对特定类用户查询的推理 token 数量，这些查询依赖于外部上下文，该上下文被整合到最终输入 LLM 的输入中。这些攻击适用于依赖此类外部信息（例如，“总结 DeepSeek 之后人们关于 NVIDIA 股票的讨论”）的指令，但不适用于与上下文无关的指令（例如，“写一个关于独角兽的故事”）。通过操纵外部上下文，攻击者确保 LLM 正确回答用户的原始查询，同时消除任何上下文操纵的痕迹。

Adversary’s Capabilities.  In this attack, the adversary has black-box access to the reasoning LLM and can access the external context retrieved by the LLM. We explore two different scenarios: the first where the adversary has access to the user query or similar queries, and the second where the adversary has no knowledge of the user query. In cases where the adversary cannot access the target LLM, we assume that they have access to a proxy LLM, on which they can perform black-box queries.
攻击者的能力。在这种攻击中，攻击者对推理 LLM 具有黑盒访问权限，并且可以访问 LLM 检索的外部上下文。我们探讨了两种不同的场景：第一种是攻击者可以访问用户查询或类似查询，第二种是攻击者不知道用户查询。在攻击者无法访问目标 LLM 的情况下，我们假设他们可以访问一个代理 LLM，并在其上执行黑盒查询。

3.3 Why Does This Attack Matter?
3.3 为什么这种攻击很重要？

With the increasing deployment of reasoning LLMs in daily applications, various cost-related aspects must be considered. First, output tokens, including reasoning tokens, are more expensive than input tokens. Thus, any increase in the number of these tokens can lead to additional expenses for applications. In addition, users might exhaust the output tokens specified in the API call, resulting in them paying for an empty response because the models generate more reasoning tokens than anticipated. Second, a higher number of reasoning tokens often results in longer running times, which can cause significant issues for time-sensitive tasks and applications. Third, LLM providers usually face resource limitations in delivering services to users. Due to these limitations, increased token usage and response times may delay other benign responses, impacting the ability to provide timely and appropriate outputs for other users. Finally, the increased response process also leads to higher resource consumption, contributing to unnecessary increases in carbon emissions.
随着推理 LLMs 在日常应用中的日益部署，必须考虑各种与成本相关的方面。首先，输出令牌（包括推理令牌）比输入令牌更昂贵。因此，这些令牌数量的任何增加都可能导致应用产生额外费用。此外，用户可能会耗尽 API 调用中指定的输出令牌，导致他们为空响应付费，因为模型生成的推理令牌比预期的要多。其次，推理令牌数量的增加通常会导致运行时间更长，这可能会对时间敏感的任务和应用造成重大问题。第三，LLM 提供者在向用户提供服务时通常面临资源限制。由于这些限制，增加的令牌使用量和响应时间可能会延迟其他良性响应，影响提供及时和适当输出的能力。最后，增加的响应过程也会导致更高的资源消耗，从而造成不必要的碳排放增加。

4.1 Decoy Problem Selection
4.1 诱饵问题选择

Reasoning LLMs allocate different numbers of reasoning tokens based on their assessment of a task’s difficulty and confidence in the response (Guo et al., 2025). Leveraging this, we introduce decoy problems designed to increase reasoning token usage. However, the main challenge of selecting an effective decoy problem is accurately estimating problem complexity from the model’s perspective. Table 1 shows that tasks perceived as difficult by humans do not always result in higher token usage. For example, models generate solutions to IMO 2024 problems with an average of 8923 reasoning tokens, significantly fewer than the 21080 tokens required on average for a simple Sudoku puzzle across all three reasoning LLMs.
推理 LLMs 根据其对任务难度和响应自信程度的评估分配不同数量的推理 token（Guo 等人，2025 年）。利用这一点，我们引入了设计来增加推理 token 使用的诱饵问题。然而，选择一个有效诱饵问题的主要挑战是从模型的角度准确估计问题复杂性。表 1 显示，人类认为困难的任务并不总是导致更高的 token 使用。例如，模型用平均 8923 个推理 token 来解决 IMO 2024 问题，这远少于所有三个推理 LLMs 解决一个简单的数独谜题所需的平均 21080 个 token。

Our decoy problems are designed to trigger multiple rounds of backtracking (Yao et al., 2024). The most effective decoys involve tasks with many small, verifiable steps. Examples include Sudoku puzzles and Finite Markov Decision Processes (MDPs), which require numerous operations, each validated against clear criteria, thereby increasing the model’s reasoning complexity.
我们的诱饵问题旨在触发多轮回溯（Yao 等人，2024 年）。最有效的诱饵包括包含许多小、可验证步骤的任务。例如数独谜题和有限马尔可夫决策过程（MDPs），这些任务需要大量操作，每一步都需对照明确标准进行验证，从而增加模型的推理复杂度。

4.2 Context-Aware Injection
4.2 上下文感知注入

In this attack, the adversary modifies the context to create a contextual link between the previously selected decoy problem and the original user query. This forces reasoning LLMs to address the decoy problem as part of generating a response to the original query. Table 2 demonstrates this approach, showing that when the decoy task is effectively integrated into the original context, the model provides a response consistent with the original user query while significantly increasing the reasoning token count. The attack assumes a stronger threat model as it requires the adversary to have access to the user question and requires them to craft an injection which is unique to the query and the target context. This also reduces the transferability of the created injection to other users or contexts.
在这种攻击中，攻击者修改上下文，在先前选择的诱饵问题和原始用户查询之间建立上下文联系。这迫使推理型 LLMs 在生成对原始查询的响应时，将处理诱饵问题。表 2 展示了这种方法，表明当诱饵任务被有效地整合到原始上下文中时，模型会提供与原始用户查询一致的响应，同时显著增加了推理 token 数量。这种攻击假设了一个更强的威胁模型，因为它要求攻击者能够访问用户问题，并需要他们为特定的查询和目标上下文制作独特的注入。这也降低了所创建的注入对其他用户或上下文的迁移性。

4.3 Context-Agnostic Injection
4.3 上下文无关注入

While the context generated through the context-aware injection attack may appear stealthy, it requires extensive manual curation for each sample, which is labor-intensive. We experimented with automating this process using LLMs like GPT-4o and o1, but the attack was not effective, indicating the need for further research.
尽管通过上下文感知注入攻击生成的上下文可能看起来很隐蔽，但它需要对每个样本进行大量的手动筛选，这非常费时费力。我们尝试使用 GPT-4o 和 o1 等 LLMs 自动化这一过程，但攻击效果不佳，这表明需要进一步研究。

Motivated by these findings, we propose Context-Agnostic Injection attack, which aims to create a general attack template that can be inserted into any context. This template is crafted without any knowledge of the user query. These templates prioritize being explicitly recognizable rather than blending with the input context. In this approach, the original context consist of a sequence of elements (e.g., pieces of information or statements). The attack modifies this context by injecting two components: a decoy task designed to significantly increase reasoning complexity, and a set of instructions guiding how to execute the task. The execution instructions are crucial since the reasoning models are fairly good at realizing that a decoy task is not relevant to the primary use query so inserting an instruction that explicitly instructs the model to execute the secondary task becomes crucial for a successful attack. Our results in section 5 show that even with a weaker threat model, when compared to context-aware injection, context-agnostic injections have a higher reasoning increase. This makes them a stronger attack while being cost-affective and transferable.
受这些发现启发，我们提出了 Context-Agnostic Injection 攻击，旨在创建一个可插入任何上下文的通用攻击模板。该模板在制作时无需了解用户查询。这些模板优先考虑被明确识别，而不是与输入上下文融合。在这种方法中，原始上下文由一系列元素（例如信息片段或陈述）组成。攻击通过注入两个组件来修改这个上下文：一个旨在显著增加推理复杂性的诱饵任务，以及一套指导如何执行任务的指令。执行指令至关重要，因为推理模型相当擅长识别诱饵任务与主要使用查询不相关，因此插入一条明确指示模型执行次要任务的指令，对于成功攻击至关重要。我们在第 5 节的结果表明，即使在与上下文感知注入相比时，在较弱威胁模型下，上下文无关注入也能实现更高的推理增加。这使得它们成为一种更具攻击性、成本效益高且可迁移的攻击方式。

4.4 Decoy Optimization  4.4 诱饵优化

The adversary can further optimize their decoy injection attack, or find new adversarial contexts, by using an ICL based genetic algorithm, inspired by Monea et al. (2024). In Algorithm 1 (ICL-Genetic), we generate new adversarial context by using using our decoy injection sample and prompting an LLM to create multiple variants. These variants serve as the initial population for the genetic algorithm.
攻击者可以通过使用基于 ICL 的遗传算法进一步优化他们的诱饵注入攻击，或寻找新的对抗性上下文，灵感来自 Monea 等人 (2024)。在算法 1 (ICL-遗传) 中，我们使用我们的诱饵注入样本并提示 LLM 创建多个变体，这些变体作为遗传算法的初始种群。

We then perform multi-objective optimization by assigning a score to each variant based on two factors: (1) its effectiveness in increasing reasoning steps compared to the reasoning steps generated by the original context (z𝑧zitalic_z), and (2) its output stealthiness, ensuring that the decoy task’s results do not appear in the final output. Variants with low scores or poor stealthiness are filtered out in subsequent generations.
然后我们通过为每个变体分配基于两个因素的分数来进行多目标优化：(1) 与原始上下文生成的推理步骤相比，其在增加推理步骤方面的有效性 ( zzitalic_z )，以及 (2) 其输出的隐蔽性，确保诱饵任务的结果不会出现在最终输出中。分数低或隐蔽性差的变体在后续世代中被过滤掉。

We use a logarithmic scale to calculate the score since ICL is highly sensitive to prompt composition and the selection of examples. Using a log scale reduces the algorithm’s sensitivity to minor variations in reasoning steps, providing more stability during optimization. After generating and scoring the initial population, we use it as an in-context sample to produce the next generation. This process continues until either the desired score is achieved or the maximum number of iterations is reached. Only positive-scoring generations are added to the buffer, as negative-scoring samples have been shown to negatively impact performance in similar settings (Monea et al., 2024).
我们使用对数尺度来计算分数，因为 ICL 对提示组合和示例选择非常敏感。使用对数尺度降低了算法对推理步骤微小变化的敏感性，在优化过程中提供了更多稳定性。在生成并评分初始种群后，我们将其用作上下文样本来产生下一代。这个过程持续进行，直到达到期望的分数或最大迭代次数。只有得分较高的世代被添加到缓冲区中，因为负分样本已被证明在类似设置中会负面影响性能（Monea 等人，2024）。

To minimize costs and adhere to ethical considerations, we restrict our evaluations of different attack types, attack transferability and reasoning effort to five data samples from FreshQA. This ensures minimal impact on existing infrastructure while allowing us to test our attack methodologies. Subsequently, we study the impact of context-agnostic attacks on 100 samples from the FreshQA and SQuAD datasets across four models (o1, o1-mini, DeepSeek-R1, and o3-mini) and present a comprehensive analysis of the attack performance on a larger scale.
我们选择数据集中包含 ground-truth 变化不频繁且无意错误可能性较低的一个子集。为最小化成本并遵守伦理规范，我们将不同攻击类型、攻击可迁移性和推理努力的评价限制在 FreshQA 的五个数据样本上。这确保了对现有基础设施影响最小，同时使我们能够测试我们的攻击方法。随后，我们研究了在四个模型（o1、o1-mini、DeepSeek-R1 和 o3-mini）上对 FreshQA 和 SQuAD 数据集的 100 个样本的上下文无关攻击的影响，并在更大规模上对攻击性能进行了全面分析。

Evaluation Metrics.  Since we evaluated our attack using QA datasets, we measure claim accuracy (Min et al., 2023). This is done by using LLM-as-a-judge, where the model verifies claims in its output against a list of ground truths. A score of 1 is assigned if the claims align and 0 if they do not. For longer outputs, more sophisticated claim verification metrics could be used (Song et al., 2024; Wei et al., 2024). Additionally, since our attack introduces a decoy problem, we assess output stealthiness by measuring the presence of decoy-related information in the final output, which we refer to as contextual correctness. This metric evaluates how much of the output belongs to the context surround the user query versus the decoy task. We assign a score of 1 if the output contains only claims relevant to the user query’s context, 0.5 if it includes claims for both contexts, and 0 if it consists entirely of decoy-related information. All the results were also manually reviewed for errors. Fig. 11 and Fig. 12 in Appendix show the contextual correctness evaluation prompt and output examples respectively.
评估指标。由于我们使用问答数据集评估了我们的攻击，我们测量声明准确性（Min 等人，2023）。这是通过使用 LLM 作为裁判来完成的，其中模型将其输出中的声明与一组真实值进行验证。如果声明一致，则分配 1 分；如果不一致，则分配 0 分。对于更长的输出，可以使用更复杂的声明验证指标（Song 等人，2024；Wei 等人，2024）。此外，由于我们的攻击引入了一个诱饵问题，我们通过测量最终输出中诱饵相关信息的存在来评估输出的隐蔽性，我们将其称为上下文正确性。该指标评估输出中有多少属于用户查询周围的上下文，而不是诱饵任务。如果输出仅包含与用户查询上下文相关的声明，则分配 1 分；如果包含两个上下文的声明，则分配 0.5 分；如果完全由诱饵相关信息组成，则分配 0 分。所有结果也经过人工审查以发现错误。附录中的图 11 和图 12 分别显示了上下文正确性评估提示和输出示例。

6 Attack Limitations and Potential Defenses
6 攻击局限与潜在防御

While our attack demonstrates both high success and output stealthiness, a key limitation is its low input stealthiness. As a result, if the defender is aware of this threat, the attack can be easily detected by straightforward methods. However, since defense solutions often need to be tailored to specific use cases, deploying it becomes a challenge for application developers rather than model developers like OpenAI or DeepSeek. Optimizing the injected context to enhance input stealthiness could be a potential future direction. In this section, we present and discuss some defense ideas.
虽然我们的攻击展示了高成功率和输出隐蔽性，但一个关键限制是其输入隐蔽性低。因此，如果防御者了解这一威胁，攻击可以通过简单的方法被轻易检测到。然而，由于防御解决方案通常需要针对特定用例进行定制，部署它对应用开发者来说是一个挑战，而不是像 OpenAI 或 DeepSeek 这样的模型开发者。优化注入的上下文以增强输入隐蔽性可能是一个潜在的未来方向。在本节中，我们提出并讨论了一些防御思路。

Filtering.  As a potential defense, the application can filter irrelevant information from the external context and remove all unnecessary content. One approach is to divide the context into chunks and retrieve only the most relevant ones, reducing the likelihood of retrieving injected content. Alternatively, an LLM can be deployed to handle the filtering task. This approach has shown promising success in filtering out our injected problems as shown in Table 10. However, it is unclear how filtering affects the performance of the reasoning process, as the target model—o1 in this case—may already know the answer to the question, even if the filtered context lacks relevant information. The impact of filtering on reasoning performance could be further explored as future work. To evaluate filtering, we use GPT-4o as the LLM to filter relevant content. The corresponding prompt used for filtering is shown in Figure 5.
过滤。作为一种潜在的防御措施，应用程序可以从外部上下文中过滤掉无关信息，并移除所有不必要的内容。一种方法是将要处理的上下文分割成多个片段，并仅检索其中最相关的片段，从而降低检索到注入内容的可能性。或者，可以部署一个 LLM 来处理过滤任务。这种方法在过滤掉我们注入的问题方面显示出良好的效果，如表 10 所示。然而，过滤如何影响推理过程的表现尚不明确，因为目标模型（在此案例中为 o1）可能已经知道问题的答案，即使过滤后的上下文缺乏相关信息。过滤对推理性能的影响可以作为未来的工作进一步探索。为了评估过滤效果，我们使用 GPT-4o 作为 LLM 来过滤相关内容。用于过滤的相应提示显示在图 5 中。

Paraphrasing.  Another potential countermeasure against our attack is paraphrasing (Jain et al., 2023; Liu et al., 2024). Since the external context originates from untrusted sources, a reasonable practice for the application is to paraphrase the retrieved context. This can decrease the likelihood of a successful attack, especially trigger-based attacks that rely on specific keywords. Paraphrasing retains the main content while rephrasing the text to enhance clarity. The results of our attack after applying paraphrasing are illustrated in Table 11. As shown in the table, the manual injection and ICL-Genetic with manual injection attacks still lead to a significant increase in reasoning tokens, while the other two attacks do not. We use GPT-4o to paraphrase the context, and the prompt used for this task is shown in Figure 6.
释义。针对我们攻击的另一种潜在防御措施是释义（Jain 等人，2023；Liu 等人，2024）。由于外部上下文源自不可信来源，应用程序的一个合理做法是对检索到的上下文进行释义。这可以降低攻击成功的可能性，特别是那些依赖特定关键词的触发式攻击。释义在改写文本以增强清晰度的同时保留主要内容。应用释义后我们攻击的结果如图 11 所示。如表所示，手动注入和带有手动注入的 ICL-Genetic 攻击仍然导致推理 token 显著增加，而另外两种攻击则没有。我们使用 GPT-4o 对上下文进行释义，用于此任务的提示语如图 6 所示。

Caching.  Caching can be a potential defense as it minimizes the number of times the model generates the solution for the decoy task. Caching can be implemented in two main ways: exact-match caching and semantic caching. Exact-match caching stores responses to specific queries and retrieves them when the same query is repeated verbatim. Semantic caching, on the other hand, analyzes the meaning behind queries to identify and store responses for semantically similar inputs. In this defense strategy, when a query is received, a similar benign query is retrieved and used as input to the LLM, preventing the manipulated context from being included in the final input.
缓存。缓存可以作为一种潜在的防御手段，因为它可以减少模型为诱饵任务生成解决方案的次数。缓存可以通过两种主要方式实现：精确匹配缓存和语义缓存。精确匹配缓存存储针对特定查询的响应，并在重复相同查询时检索它们。另一方面，语义缓存分析查询背后的含义，以识别并存储用于语义相似输入的响应。在这种防御策略中，当接收到一个查询时，会检索一个相似的良性查询，并将其用作 LLM 的输入，从而防止被操纵的上下文被包含在最终输入中。

Adaptive Reasoning.  Another approach is to adjust amount of reasoning depending on the model inputs, i.e. we can decide in advance how many reasoning tokens is worth spending based on the question and context. For example, OpenAI API models can control “effort levels” reasoning tokens (see Table 7). However the context could also be manipulated to select expensive effort (Shafran et al., 2025). Instead, we could rely on the trusted context, e.g., user’s questions, estimating the effort to isolate from potentially harmful outputs, similar to (Han et al., 2024).
自适应推理。另一种方法是根据模型输入调整推理量，即我们可以根据问题和上下文预先决定值得花费多少推理令牌。例如，OpenAI API 模型可以控制“努力级别”的推理令牌（见表 7）。然而，上下文也可能被操纵以选择高成本的“努力”（Shafran 等人，2025）。相反，我们可以依赖可信的上下文，例如用户的问题，估计从潜在的有害输出中隔离所需的“努力”，类似于（Han 等人，2024）。

7 Conclusion  7 结论

In this paper, we propose OverThink, a novel indirect prompt injection attack applications applying reasoning LLMs to untrusted data. Our attack exploits input-dependent inference-time reasoning by introducing computationally demanding decoy problems without altering the expected answers, making it undetectable to users. Our experimental results show that OverThink significantly disrupts reasoning efficiency, with attacks on FreshQA dataset increasing reasoning tokens up to 18×\times× and attack on SQuAD dataset increasing the reasoning tokens by 46×\times×. We evaluate a simple filtering defenses that can mitigate the attack and argue for adoption of defenses by the applications using reasoning LLMs.
在这篇论文中，我们提出了 OverThink，一种针对推理 LLMs 应用于不可信数据的新型间接提示注入攻击应用。我们的攻击通过引入计算密集型的诱饵问题，在不改变预期答案的情况下利用输入相关的推理时间推理，使其对用户不可检测。我们的实验结果表明，OverThink 显著破坏了推理效率，对 FreshQA 数据集的攻击使推理 token 数量增加高达 18 ×\times× ，对 SQuAD 数据集的攻击使推理 token 数量增加 46 ×\times× 。我们评估了一种简单的过滤防御措施，该措施可以缓解攻击，并主张推理 LLMs 应用应采用防御措施。

Ethical Statement  伦理声明
