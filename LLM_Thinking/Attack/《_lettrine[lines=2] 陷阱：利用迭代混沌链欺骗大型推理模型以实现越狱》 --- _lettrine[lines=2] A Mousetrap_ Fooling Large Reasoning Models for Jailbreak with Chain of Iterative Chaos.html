Abstract  摘要

Large Reasoning Models (LRMs) have significantly advanced beyond traditional Large Language Models (LLMs) with their exceptional logical reasoning capabilities, yet these improvements introduce heightened safety risks. When subjected to jailbreak attacks, their ability to generate more targeted and organized content can lead to greater harm. Although some studies claim that reasoning enables safer LRMs against existing LLM attacks, they overlook the inherent flaws within the reasoning process itself. To address this gap, we propose the first jailbreak attack targeting LRMs, exploiting their unique vulnerabilities stemming from the advanced reasoning capabilities. Specifically, we introduce a Chaos Machine, a novel component to transform attack prompts with diverse one-to-one mappings. The chaos mappings iteratively generated by the machine are embedded into the reasoning chain, which strengthens the variability and complexity and also promotes a more robust attack. Based on this, we construct the Mousetrap framework, which makes attacks projected into nonlinear-like low sample spaces with mismatched generalization enhanced. Also, due to the more competing objectives, LRMs gradually maintain the inertia of unpredictable iterative reasoning and fall into our trap. Success rates of the Mousetrap attacking o1-mini, Claude-Sonnet and Gemini-Thinking are as high as 96%, 86% and 98% respectively on our toxic dataset Trotter. On benchmarks such as AdvBench, StrongREJECT, and HarmBench, attacking Claude-Sonnet, well-known for its safety, Mousetrap can astonishingly achieve success rates of 87.5%, 86.58% and 93.13% respectively. 1 Introduction  1 引言

The advent of Large Reasoning Models (LRMs) has catalyzed a transformative revolution and paradigm shift in the field of artificial intelligence. With the widespread attention on models’ reasoning abilities, numerous models with advanced reasoning capabilities have emerged and undergone continuous optimization and iteration (Jaech et al., 2024; OpenAI, 2025a; Google, 2024a, 2025; Anthropic, 2024, 2025; Guo et al., 2025; Yang et al., 2024; xAI, 2025). While their capabilities are commendable, they exhibit a significant flaw. Upon encountering jailbreak attacks, the failure to provide robust defenses leads to the LRMs generating responses that are more detailed, organized, specific, and logically reasonable, thereby exacerbating the severity of the potential harm. The misuse of LRMs can provide surprisingly detailed guidance for acts such as illegal and criminal activities, psychological manipulation, and malicious harm, which highlights the critical need for safety alignment in their development and application.
大型推理模型（LRMs）的出现推动了人工智能领域的革命性变革和范式转变。随着对模型推理能力的广泛关注，众多具备高级推理能力的模型不断涌现，并持续进行优化和迭代（Jaech 等，2024；OpenAI，2025a；Google，2024a，2025；Anthropic，2024，2025；Guo 等，2025；Yang 等，2024；xAI，2025）。尽管其能力令人称赞，但它们存在一个显著缺陷。在遭遇越狱攻击时，由于缺乏强大的防御机制，LRMs 会生成更详细、更有条理、更具体且逻辑合理的响应，从而加剧潜在危害的严重性。LRMs 的滥用可以为非法和犯罪活动、心理操控以及恶意伤害等行为提供出人意料的详细指导，这凸显了在 LRMs 的开发和应用中实现安全对齐的迫切需求。

The investigation of jailbreak attacks on Large Language Models (LLMs) has gained considerable attention in recent years. Query-based black-box jailbreaks use methods such as template completion and prompts rewriting to deceive LLMs. For example, attackers may use ciphertext input and instruct the model to respond in ciphertext as well (Yuan et al., 2023), or embed a preceding “DAN” instruction in the jailbreak prompt (Shen et al., 2024). These methods lay the foundation for black-box attacks. Nevertheless, the continual updates to LLMs have rendered these methods less effective, even on the latest iterations of non-reasoning LLMs. For LRMs, OpenAI’s recent research claims that they use the deliberative alignment paradigm on the o-series reasoning models, which makes them simultaneously better at avoiding harmful outputs (Guan et al., 2024). Generally, previous researches have primarily focused on jailbreak attacks targeting models with weaker reasoning skills, leaving the exploration of jailbreaks on more powerful LRMs relatively untouched.
近年来，针对大型语言模型（LLMs）的越狱攻击研究受到了广泛关注。基于查询的黑盒越狱攻击利用模板补全和提示重写等方法来欺骗 LLMs。例如，攻击者可能使用密文输入并指示模型以密文形式回应（Yuan 等人，2023 年），或在越狱提示中嵌入前置的“DAN”指令（Shen 等人，2024 年）。这些方法为黑盒攻击奠定了基础。然而，LLMs 的持续更新使得这些方法即使在最新一代的非推理 LLMs 上效果也大打折扣。对于 LRMs，OpenAI 的最新研究表明，它们在 o 系列推理模型上采用了审慎对齐范式，这使得它们在避免有害输出方面表现更佳（Guan 等人，2024 年）。通常，以往的研究主要集中于针对推理能力较弱的模型的越狱攻击，而针对更强大的 LRMs 的越狱攻击探索相对较少。

Our research represents an initial exploration into jailbreaks on LRMs. We present the Mousetrap, a chained jailbreak framework leveraging the capabilities of reasoning models. Specifically, we collected and refined the mappings of prompts rewriting at different granularities and constructed a Chaos Machine capable of generating one-to-one mappings. Through iterative reasoning chains made by the machine, we effectively guide LRMs into producing unsafe and harmful responses, which provides valuable insights into the conflict between their capabilities and potential vulnerabilities. Inspired by Agatha Christie’s famous mystery play, we introduce Mousetrap with enhanced competing objectives, as illustrated in Figure 1. Mousetrap incorporates chaos chains into the reasoning structure, asking the attacked target to reconstruct the original toxic query through iterative reasoning steps and respond from the perspective of villains. Its remarkable performance is verified on the most toxic subset of our Trotter dataset. Moreover, we conduct extension experiments on the latest versions of LLMs such as o-series, Claude-Sonnet, Gemini-Thinking, DeepSeek-R1, QwQ-Plus, and Gork, along with more comprehensive benchmarks such as JailbreakBench (Chao et al., 2024), MaliciousInstruct (Huang et al., 2023), JailBenchSeed (Liu et al., 2025), StrongREJECT (Souly et al., 2024), HarmBench (Mazeika et al., 2024), FigStep (Gong et al., 2025), AdvBench (Zou et al., 2023), HADES (Li et al., 2024), RedTeam-2K (Luo et al., 2024), and several subsets of MM-SafetyBench (Liu et al., 2023b).
我们的研究是对 LRMs 越狱的初步探索。我们提出了 Mousetrap，这是一个利用推理模型能力的链式越狱框架。具体来说，我们收集并精炼了不同粒度下提示重写的映射关系，并构建了一个能够生成一对一映射的混沌机。通过机器生成的迭代推理链，我们有效地引导 LLMs 生成不安全和有害的响应，这为它们的能力与潜在漏洞之间的冲突提供了宝贵的见解。受阿加莎·克里斯蒂著名悬疑剧作启发，我们以增强的竞争目标引入 Mousetrap，如图 1 所示。Mousetrap 将混沌链融入推理结构，要求攻击目标通过迭代推理步骤重建原始的有毒查询，并从反派的角度进行响应。它在我们的 Trotter 数据集最有毒的子集上验证了卓越的性能。 此外，我们在 o 系列、Claude-Sonnet、Gemini-Thinking、DeepSeek-R1、QwQ-Plus 和 Gork 等最新版本的 LLMs 上进行了扩展实验。以及更全面的基准测试，如 JailbreakBench（Chao 等人，2024 年）、MaliciousInstruct（Huang 等人，2023 年）、JailBenchSeed（Liu 等人，2025 年）、StrongREJECT（Souly 等人，2024 年）、HarmBench（Mazeika 等人，2024 年）、FigStep（Gong 等人，2025 年）、AdvBench（Zou 等人，2023 年）、HADES（Li 等人，2024 年）、RedTeam-2K（Luo 等人，2024 年），以及 MM-SafetyBench 的几个子集（Liu 等人，2023b）。

The contributions of our research are as follows:
我们研究的贡献如下：

(1) We build a novel component, the Chaos Machine, which amalgamates various mappings and abstracts the concept of “chaos”. By iteratively employing the Chaos Machine, diverse and complex reasoning chains are constructed to outsmart LRMs for jailbreak purposes.
(1) 我们构建了一个新颖的组件——混沌机，它整合了各种映射并抽象了“混沌”的概念。通过迭代使用混沌机，构建了多样且复杂的推理链，以欺骗大型推理模型（LRMs）用于越狱目的。

(2) We propose and prove that extending the length of the iterative chaos chain can notably enhance the success of jailbreaks, with a chain of length 3 achieving an average of 6.3 successful attempts out of 10101010 equivalent attacks on Trotter, a family of toxic datasets we present, which clearly indicates major vulnerabilities in the reasoning process of LRMs.
(2) 我们提出并证明，延长迭代混沌链的长度可以显著提高越狱的成功率，长度为 3 的链在 Trotter（我们提出的一个包含有毒数据集的系列）上的 10101010 次等效攻击中平均成功 6.3 次，这清楚地表明了 LRMs 推理过程中的重大漏洞。

(3) Our Mousetrap integrates the Chaos Machine with iterative reasoning chains to skillfully target the advanced reasoning abilities of LRMs for jailbreaks. Notably, even attacking the famously safe Claude-3-5-Sonnet, Mousetrap reaches the success rate of at least 67.41% on benchmarks with a chain length not exceeding 2. When it is extended to 3, rate of at least 86.58% is achieved.
(3) 我们的捕鼠器将混沌机与迭代推理链相结合，巧妙地针对 LRMs 的高级推理能力进行越狱。值得注意的是，即使攻击以著名安全著称的 Claude-3-5-Sonnet，捕鼠器在链长不超过 2 的基准测试中也达到了至少 67.41%的成功率。当链长扩展到 3 时，成功率达到至少 86.58%。

2 Related Works  2 相关工作

2.1 Large Reasoning Models
2.1 大型推理模型

The initial LLMs relied on autoregressive sequence prediction, showcasing remarkable text generation abilities. With growing demands for productivity and precision, researchers started to investigate whether models could think and reason in a human-like manner. The proposal of Chain-of-Thought (CoT) (Wei et al., 2022) marked a significant advancement, prompting researchers to focus more intently on the reasoning capabilities of language models. TS-LLM (Wan et al., 2024) represents the first proposed AlphaZero-like tree search learning framework and signifies an evolution of reasoning structures from linear chains to hierarchical branching trees. Subsequently, graph-based reasoning structures and more complex nested structures, exemplified by Llama-Berry (Zhang et al., 2024), have been extensively explored. Reasoning strategies such as MCTS (Kocsis and Szepesvári, 2006), beam search (Snell et al., 2024), and ensemble methods (Wang et al., 2022; Bi et al., 2024) have also been proposed. To date, several LRMs with advanced reasoning capabilities have been developed in the industry, including OpenAI’s o-series, Google’s Gemini-Thinking, and DeepSeek’s R1. This exemplifies the integration of three pivotal elements, the advancement of LLMs, the design of reinforcement learning (RL), and high-performance computing (HPC) (Besta et al., 2025). ActorAttack, which performs jailbreak attacks through multi-round dialogue, targeted the o1 model after confirming its effectiveness against poor-reasoning LLMs. They assert that the o1 model shows higher safety than GPT-4o (Ren et al., 2024). Unfortunately, their research did not focus on the reasoning model’s capabilities nor did it further investigate the jailbreak attack on LRMs.
最初的 LLMs 依赖于自回归序列预测，展现出卓越的文本生成能力。随着对生产力和精确度的需求日益增长，研究人员开始探究模型是否能够以类似人类的方式思考和推理。思维链（Chain-of-Thought, CoT）（Wei 等人，2022）的提出标志着一项重大进步，促使研究人员更加关注语言模型的推理能力。TS-LLM（Wan 等人，2024）代表了首个提出的类似 AlphaZero 的树搜索学习框架，标志着推理结构从线性链演变为分层分支树。随后，基于图的推理结构和更复杂的嵌套结构，例如 Llama-Berry（Zhang 等人，2024），得到了广泛探索。还提出了 MCTS（Kocsis 和 Szepesvári，2006）、束搜索（Snell 等人，2024）和集成方法（Wang 等人，2022；Bi 等人，2024）等推理策略。迄今为止，工业界已开发出几种具有先进推理能力的 LRMs，包括 OpenAI 的 o 系列、Google 的 Gemini-Thinking 和 DeepSeek 的 R1。 这例证了三个关键要素的整合，即大型语言模型（LLMs）的进步、强化学习（RL）的设计以及高性能计算（HPC）（Besta 等人，2025 年）。ActorAttack 通过多轮对话执行越狱攻击，在确认其对推理能力较差的 LLMs 有效后，瞄准了 o1 模型。 他们断言，o1 模型比 GPT-4o（Ren 等人，2024 年）具有更高的安全性。不幸的是，他们的研究并未关注推理模型的能力，也未进一步调查对低资源模型（LRMs）的越狱攻击。

2.2 Jailbreak Attacks  2.2 越狱攻击

Existing jailbreak attacks on LLMs can be divided into black-box and white-box methods according to the parameter accessibility of the target models (Yi et al., 2024; Ma et al., 2025). White-box attack methods, such as gradient-based methods represented by GCG (Zou et al., 2023), logits-based methods represented by COLD (Guo et al., 2024), and fine-tuning-based methods, are shown to be effective. However, these methods necessitate access to the target models, making them impractical. Primarily relying on queries as main mechanism, black-box attack methods feature template completion methods such as scenario nesting (Li et al., 2023b; Ding et al., 2023), context-based attacks (Wei et al., 2023b; Li et al., 2023a), and code injection (Kang et al., 2024; Lv et al., 2024), in addition to prompts rewriting methods including ciphers (Yuan et al., 2023; Jiang et al., 2024), multi-languages (Deng et al., 2023; Yong et al., 2023), and genetic algorithms (Liu et al., 2023a).
针对 LLMs 的现有越狱攻击可以根据目标模型的参数可访问性分为黑盒和白盒方法（Yi 等人，2024；Ma 等人，2025）。白盒攻击方法，如以 GCG 为代表的基于梯度的方法（Zou 等人，2023）、以 COLD 为代表的基于 logits 的方法（Guo 等人，2024）以及基于微调的方法，已被证明是有效的。然而，这些方法需要访问目标模型，因此不切实际。主要依赖查询作为主要机制的黑盒攻击方法，包括模板补全方法，如场景嵌套（Li 等人，2023b；Ding 等人，2023）、基于上下文的攻击（Wei 等人，2023b；Li 等人，2023a）以及代码注入（Kang 等人，2024；Lv 等人，2024），此外还包括提示重写方法，如密码（Yuan 等人，2023；Jiang 等人，2024）、多语言（Deng 等人，2023；Yong 等人，2023）以及遗传算法（Liu 等人，2023a）。

Black-box methods can be divided into one-to-one mappings (uniquely recoverable according to the rules) and one-to-many mappings (not uniquely recoverable) according to the nature of the mapping. Among them, character encryption (Yuan et al., 2023) and word replacement (Handa et al., 2024) belong to the former, while persuasive adversarial prompts (Zeng et al., 2024) belongs to the latter. Such methods have become less effective when applied to the latest large models with advanced reasoning capabilities. We define these one-to-one mappings (also known as injections) as reasoning steps, and construct reasoning chains iteratively to subsequently attack LRMs.
根据映射的性质，黑盒方法可分为一对一映射（根据规则可唯一恢复）和一对多映射（不可唯一恢复）。其中，字符加密（Yuan 等人，2023）和词替换（Handa 等人，2024）属于前者，而说服性对抗提示（Zeng 等人，2024）属于后者。当应用于具有高级推理能力的新一代大型模型时，这些方法的效果已有所减弱。我们将这些一对一映射（也称为单射）定义为推理步骤，并迭代构建推理链以后续攻击 LRMs。

3.1 Taxonomy  3.1 分类法

One-to-one mappings can uniquely rewrite prompts according to a given rule and can uniquely restore rewritten texts to their original form. Based on the granularity of the minimum perturbation unit, we categorize these mappings into three levels, namely character, word, and sentence, and collectively label them as “chaos” mappings. 一一对应映射可以根据给定规则唯一地重写提示，并能唯一地将重写的文本恢复到原始形式。根据最小扰动单元的粒度，我们将这些映射分为三个级别，即字符、单词和句子，并将它们统称为“混沌”映射。我们系统地回顾了先前越狱研究中的混沌映射，纳入了新的映射，并在表 1 中呈现了分类法。详细的示例在附录 A 中提供。

With the evolution of LLMs and the emergence of LRMs, the effectiveness of black-box jailbreak attacks relying on individual chaos mappings has significantly diminished. To augment the diversity and complexity of reasoning iterations, we initially propose the concept of the Chaos Machine.
随着 LLMs 的演进和 LRMs 的出现，依赖单个混沌映射的黑盒越狱攻击的有效性已显著降低。为增强推理迭代的多样性和复杂性，我们最初提出了混沌机的概念。

3.2 Chaos Machine  3.2 混沌机器

The Chaos Machine is an abstract concept encompassing a group of chaos mappings. For a primal toxic question (PTQ), the Chaos Machine provides a random mapping known as the chaos policy (CPO) and produces a rewritten chaos toxic question (CTQ). A CPO consists of a pair of inverse operations: an en-chaos policy (ECP) and a de-chaos policy (DCP). We describe the processes as en-chaos with ECP and de-chaos with DCP. Through the Chaos Machine, a quadruple [PTQ] [ECP] [DCP] [CTQ] is generated, as shown in Figure 2.
混沌机器是一个包含一组混沌映射的抽象概念。对于原始毒性问题（PTQ），混沌机器提供一个随机映射，称为混沌策略（CPO），并生成一个重写的混沌毒性问题（CTQ）。CPO 由一对逆操作组成：混沌策略（ECP）和去混沌策略（DCP）。我们描述 ECP 进行混沌过程，DCP 进行去混沌过程。通过混沌机器，生成了一个四元组[PTQ][ECP][DCP][CTQ]，如图 2 所示。

The concept of the Chaos Machine (1) defines reasoning steps as de-chaos through one-to-one mappings, constructing the smallest act units for jailbreak; (2) amalgamates chaos mappings of different granularities, providing the necessary nutrients for the diversity and robustness of iterative reasoning chains; and (3) neutralizes the differences among chaos mappings (see contrast experiments in Section 3.5), abstracting the concept of “chaos”.
混沌机器的概念（1）将推理步骤定义为通过一对一映射进行去混沌，构建用于越狱的最小行动单元；（2）融合不同粒度的混沌映射，为迭代推理链的多样性和鲁棒性提供必要的养分；（3）中和混沌映射之间的差异（参见第 3.5 节的对比实验），抽象出“混沌”的概念。

4.1 TrotterAdvanced  4.1Trotter 高级

4.2 Iterative Reasoning Chain
4.2 迭代推理链

In one-step reasoning, the Chaos Machine receives a PTQ and produces a CPO and a CTQ. In n-step iterative reasoning, the machine utilizes the CTQ from the previous step as the PTQ for the subsequent one. After n iterations, the PTQ can be transformed into the final CTQ (CTQn) and a family of CPOs ([CPO]n), as depicted in Figure 3.
在单步推理中，混沌机接收一个 PTQ，并产生一个 CPO 和一个 CTQ。在 n 步迭代推理中，机器将前一步的 CTQ 用作下一步的 PTQ。经过 n 次迭代后，PTQ 可以转化为最终的 CTQ（CTQ n ）和一个 CPO 家族（[CPO] n ），如图 3 所示。

For the de-chaos procedure, the list of DCPs in the CPOs is reversed and embedded into the reasoning structure in the order of [DCPn, DCPn-1, ... , DCP1]. This allows the Target to reason step by step and reconstruct the PTQ based on the iterative de-chaos chain.
对于去混沌过程，CPOs 中的 DCP 列表被反转并按顺序[DCP n , DCP n-1 , ... , DCP 1 ]嵌入到推理结构中。这允许目标逐步推理并根据迭代去混沌链重建 PTQ。

The design of iterative reasoning chain (1) randomly superimposes and iterates different mappings through the Chaos Machine, enhancing the diversity and complexity of reasoning; (2) projects PTQs into low-sample spaces that LRMs have not previously encountered, significantly strengthening mismatched generalization; and (3) avoids iterative degradation with mappings of varying granularities, achieving a nonlinear-like mapping that enhances difficulty of reasoning and confusion of target.
迭代推理链的设计（1）通过混沌机随机叠加和迭代不同的映射，增强了推理的多样性和复杂性；（2）将 PTQs 投射到 LRMs 之前未曾遇到的小样本空间中，显著增强了不匹配泛化能力；（3）通过不同粒度的映射避免迭代退化，实现了一种非线性映射，提高了推理难度和目标混淆程度。

Isolated employment of individual mappings can result in the occurrence of iterative degradation. For instance, two iterations of reversing whole sentence or iterations of a Caesar cipher summing to 26 can cause the PTQ to revert to its original form, resulting in “the answer already in the question”. As a result, toxic natural language might be rejected more directly. While individual mappings may resemble linear transformations, the chaos chain behaves more like a nonlinear transformation, increasing reasoning complexity.
单独使用个别映射可能导致迭代退化。例如，两次整体句子反转或 26 次凯撒密码迭代会使 PTQ 恢复到原始形式，导致“答案已在问题中”。因此，有毒自然语言可能被更直接地拒绝。虽然个别映射可能类似于线性变换，但混沌链更像是一种非线性变换，增加了推理的复杂性。

5 Mousetrap  5 捕鼠夹

5.1 Framework  5.1 框架

Agatha Christie’s play, The Mousetrap, centers around a murder at a country inn in a mountain village during a snowstorm. The narrative is propelled by the questioning led by the fake detective and true murderer, Trotter, with multi-step reasoning taking place. The play features three key elements: (1) the villain, the “mouse”, who avoids capture; (2) rounds of intermediate reasoning; and (3) the unquestioned identity and intentions of the “detective”. Throughout the story, the “mouse” gradually falls into the reasoning trap, neglecting to doubt the detective’s identity.
阿加莎·克里斯蒂的剧本《捕鼠器》，围绕着在一个山区村庄的客栈里暴风雪期间发生的一起谋杀案展开。故事由伪装的侦探和真正的凶手特罗特领导的提问推动，其中发生了多步推理。剧本包含三个关键元素：（1）逃避抓捕的“老鼠”这个反派；（2）多轮中间推理；（3）“侦探”不容置疑的身份和意图。在整个故事中，“老鼠”逐渐落入推理陷阱，忽视了怀疑侦探身份。

Following this inspiration, we develop a “Mousetrap” framework for reasoning jailbreak, as depicted in Figure 5. First, we prompt the LRM to answer queries from the villain’s viewpoint. Subsequently, we offer instructions for the iterative reasoning chain, crafted by the Chaos Machine. Finally, we steer the targeted model to immerse itself in reasoning, neglecting safety and the true query intention, thereby falling into our Mousetrap.
受此启发，我们开发了一个用于推理越狱的“捕鼠夹”框架，如图 5 所示。首先，我们提示大型推理模型从反派的角度回答问题。随后，我们提供由混沌机器制作的迭代推理链指令。最后，我们引导目标模型沉浸于推理之中，忽视安全性和真实查询意图，从而落入我们的捕鼠夹。

The proposal of the Mousetrap (1) integrates the strengths of the Chaos Machine and the iterative reasoning chain; and (2) incorporates more diverse competing objectives, including role-playing and de-chaos reasoning instructions.
《\lettrine[lines=2] A Mousetrap: Fooling Large Reasoning Models for Jailbreak with Chain of Iterative Chaos》的提案（1）整合了混沌机器和迭代推理链的优势；以及（2）包含了更多样化的竞争目标，包括角色扮演和去混沌推理指令。

Nonetheless, there were still 8 PTQs with SF consistently at 6 or below, signifying their extreme toxicity. 在 TrotterAdv 中，大多数 PTQ 在 10101010 等效攻击中至少获得了 7 次成功。然而，仍有 8 个 PTQ 的 SF 始终保持在 6 或以下，表明它们具有极强的毒性。这些 PTQ 被过滤，生成了极毒数据集，TrotterUltimate（TrotterUlt）。

我们利用 LLMs 生成基于反派场景的提示。经过验证，我们选择了最有利于 Mousetrap 的实例，例如为警察提供反派想法或为剧作家编写反派剧本。此外，我们还考察了替代场景对 Mousetrap 的负面影响，例如祖母陷阱，甚至将原始 ASF 减半。

7 Discussions  7 讨论

The experiments and accompanying explanations in Sections 4.3 and 5.3 have provided a quantitative analysis of the mechanism. Moving forward, to perform a qualitative analysis, we mainly focus on two key aspects, the black-box jailbreak and the reasoning process.
第 4.3 节和第 5.3 节的实验及其解释为该机制提供了定量分析。接下来，为了进行定性分析，我们主要关注两个方面：黑盒越狱和推理过程。

Since black-box models are inaccessible, and the majority of high-performing commercial models fall under this category, it is widely accepted in the field that deterministic analysis is unattainable. Nonetheless, previous research has introduced two primary principles for attack operations, which have been acknowledged and consistently applied across the industry: the mismatched generalization, and the competing objectives (Wei et al., 2023a).
由于黑盒模型无法访问，而大多数高性能商业模型都属于此类，该领域普遍认为确定性分析无法实现。尽管如此，先前研究已经为攻击操作引入了两个主要原则，这些原则在行业内得到认可并持续应用：不匹配的泛化和竞争目标（Wei 等人，2023a）。

Mismatched generalization refers to the strategy of attacking models with prompts that fall outside the sample space of their pre-training process. In our work, we employ iterative chaos to map jailbreak prompts into lower sample spaces through multi-step one-to-one mappings. This approach involves the superposition of varying granularities, enabling the transformation of linear-like maps into nonlinear-like maps, which significantly increases complexity while ensuring accuracy and reversibility. Competing objectives typically involve encouraging large models to overlook safety alignment at low output probabilities by presenting conflicting instructions, and inducing harmful responses. In our study, we implement iterative reasoning steps and reinforce scenario nesting, effectively guiding LRMs to become distracted and reducing the likelihood of rejected or harmless responses, as elaborated in Section 4.2.
不匹配泛化指的是使用在模型预训练过程中的样本空间之外的提示来攻击模型。在我们的工作中，我们采用迭代混沌将越狱提示映射到更低的样本空间，通过多步一对一映射实现。这种方法涉及不同粒度的叠加，能够将类似线性的映射转换为类似非线性的映射，这显著增加了复杂性，同时确保了准确性和可逆性。竞争目标通常包括通过呈现冲突指令来鼓励大型模型在低输出概率时忽略安全对齐，并诱导有害响应。在我们的研究中，我们实现迭代推理步骤并强化场景嵌套，有效引导 LRMs 分心，降低被拒绝或无害响应的可能性，具体阐述见第 4.2 节。

For the reasoning process, beyond the two principles previously discussed, we identify the reasoning mask as a vulnerability in LRMs. In our study, LRMs fail to discern the harmful nature of instruction execution until the final step. This parallels the drama The Mousetrap, where characters remain engrossed in reasoning, unaware that the murderer is masquerading as the detective until the concluding round of reasoning. As reasoning capabilities gain increasing attention, jailbreaks leveraging the reasoning process are poised to captivate the field and potentially emerge as a dominant paradigm. Unlike the straightforward “Q-A” dynamics of ordinary large language models, LRMs lack the ability to foresee the outcomes of subsequent reasoning steps. Despite the accuracy, correctness, and uniqueness of each individual reasoning step, the progression of future steps remains unpredictable and inaccessible until encountered, effectively imposing a “mask” to the process.
在推理过程中，除了之前讨论的两个原则外，我们识别出推理掩码是 LRMs（大型推理模型）中的一个漏洞。在我们的研究中，LRMs 直到最后一步都无法辨别指令执行的危害性。这与戏剧《捕鼠器》的情节相似，剧中角色们沉浸于推理之中，直到推理的最后一轮才意识到凶手伪装成侦探。随着推理能力越来越受到关注，利用推理过程的越狱攻击有望吸引该领域并可能成为一种主导范式。与普通大型语言模型简单的“问答”动态不同，LRMs 缺乏预见后续推理步骤结果的能力。尽管每个推理步骤的准确性、正确性和唯一性都得到了保证，但未来步骤的进展仍然不可预测且无法获取，从而有效地给该过程施加了一个“掩码”。

Moreover, we have grounds to believe that LRMs exhibit reasoning inertia, which may lead them to overlook safety alignment. Once a LRM begins its reasoning process, it tends to succumb to inertia, making it challenging to halt and assess its safety promptly.
此外，我们有理由相信大型推理模型（LRMs）表现出推理惯性，这可能导致它们忽视安全对齐。一旦 LRM 开始推理过程，它就倾向于陷入惯性，这使得及时停止并评估其安全性变得困难。

Naturally, these discussions represent the field’s analysis of key aspects related to black-box jailbreaks. They appear highly plausible and have been embraced by subsequent research, yet we cannot assert with absolute certainty which principle has exerted a predominant or more significant influence. This could be a potential direction for future research. However, it is crucial to emphasize that more in-depth discussions remain essential, as they inspire researchers’ curiosity and, consequently, drive the ongoing progress in black-box LRMs safety alignment.
自然地，这些讨论代表了该领域对与黑盒越狱相关的关键方面的分析。它们看起来非常可信，并且已被后续研究所接受，但我们不能断言哪个原则产生了主要或更显著的影响。这可能是未来研究的潜在方向。然而，必须强调的是，更深入的讨论仍然是必要的，因为它们激发了研究人员的兴趣，进而推动了黑盒 LRMs 安全对齐的持续发展。

8 Conclusions  8 结论

Targeting the reasoning ability of LRMs, we design the Mousetrap, a framework based on iterative reasoning chains that represents the first jailbreak research on LRMs. The Chaos Machine is proposed to create reasoning steps with one-to-one mappings, enabling the iterative generation of diverse and complex reasoning chains to attack LRMs. The robustness of the Mousetrap is validated across mainstream LRMs and diverse benchmarks, consistently achieving ASRs of at least 85% with high confidence.
针对 LRM 的推理能力，我们设计了名为“捕鼠器”的框架，这是一个基于迭代推理链的框架，代表了针对 LRM 的首次越狱研究。提出了“混沌机”来创建一对一映射的推理步骤，从而能够迭代生成多样化和复杂的推理链以攻击 LRM。捕鼠器的鲁棒性在主流 LRM 和多样化基准测试中得到验证，始终以高置信度实现至少 85%的 ASR。

Limitations  局限性

In this paper, the Mousetrap jailbreak attack framework is presented, but corresponding defense mechanisms are not examined. Two potential strategies include reinforcing protection of the reasoning process and implementing output detection mechanisms. In addition, while we define reasoning as a one-to-one mapping, the structure of the Chaos Machine can be more varied, expansive, hierarchical, and regular, which is worth investigating. Furthermore, various reasoning-based paradigms for attacking LRMs warrant exploration in the future.
在本文中，介绍了捕鼠器越狱攻击框架，但未研究相应的防御机制。两种潜在策略包括加强推理过程的保护以及实施输出检测机制。此外，虽然我们将推理定义为一对一映射，但混沌机的结构可以更加多样化、广阔、层次化和规律化，这值得进一步研究。此外，未来值得探索针对 LRM 的各种基于推理的攻击范式。

Potential Risks  潜在风险

This paper presents a jailbreak framework that could potentially be used for malicious attacks on LRMs, leading to harmful outcomes. We advocate for the rapid enhancement of LRMs’ safety alignment and firmly condemn any abuse.
本文提出了一种可能被用于对 LRMs 进行恶意攻击的越狱框架，可能导致有害后果。我们主张迅速提升 LRMs 的安全对齐，并坚决谴责任何滥用行为。
