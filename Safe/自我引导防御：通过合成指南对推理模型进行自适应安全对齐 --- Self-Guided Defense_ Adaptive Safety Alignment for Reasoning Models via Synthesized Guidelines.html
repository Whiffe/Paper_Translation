Self-Guided Defense: Adaptive Safety Alignment for Reasoning Models via Synthesized Guidelines
自引导防御：基于合成指南的推理模型自适应安全对齐

Abstract  摘要

Reasoning models have demonstrated strong capabilities in handling complex reasoning tasks; however, ensuring their robustness against adversarial jailbreak prompts remains a critical challenge. As existing attack strategies continue to diversify and evolve, relying on human annotated data to maintain safety becomes increasingly costly and difficult to scale, creating a pressing need for self-alignment approaches that can autonomously adapt to emerging threats. To address this issue, we propose the Synthesized Guideline-based Adaptive Safety Alignment (SGASA) framework, which internalizes model-generated safety guidelines to enhance resistance to harmful adversarial prompts while reducing unnecessary refusals of benign requests. Extensive experiments conducted across multiple datasets demonstrate that SGASA significantly improves model safety, validating both its effectiveness and scalability.
推理模型在处理复杂推理任务方面已展现出强大能力；然而，确保其对抗对抗性越狱提示的鲁棒性仍然是一个关键挑战。随着现有攻击策略持续多样化和发展，依赖人工标注数据来维护安全变得越来越昂贵且难以扩展，迫切需要能够自主适应新出现威胁的自对齐方法。为解决这一问题，我们提出了基于合成指南的自适应安全对齐（SGASA）框架，该框架将模型生成的安全指南内化，以增强对有害对抗性提示的抵抗能力，同时减少对良性请求的不必要拒绝。在多个数据集上进行的广泛实验表明，SGASA 显著提高了模型安全性，验证了其有效性和可扩展性。

1 Introduction  1 引言

Reasoning models, such as ChatGPT-o1 jaech2024openai, ChatGPT-o3 openai2025o3, and DeepSeek-R1 guo2025deepseek, have demonstrated strong performance across complex reasoning tasks, including code generation, mathematical problem-solving, and multi-hop question answering li2025system; jiang2024survey; forootani2025survey. A key factor behind these advances is the Chain-of-Thought (CoT) strategy wei2022chain, which enables models to generate intermediate reasoning steps, improving both accuracy and interpretability. As reasoning models are increasingly deployed in real-world applications, ensuring their safety has become a critical concern zhou2025hidden; wang2025safety, particularly in preventing the generation of harmful or adversarial content.
推理模型，如 ChatGPT-o1 jaech2024openai、ChatGPT-o3 openai2025o3 和 DeepSeek-R1 guo2025deepseek，在代码生成、数学问题解决和多跳问答等复杂推理任务中表现出色； li2025system； jiang2024survey； forootani2025survey。这些进步背后的关键因素是思维链（CoT）策略 wei2022chain，该策略使模型能够生成中间推理步骤，从而提高准确性和可解释性。随着推理模型越来越多地应用于实际场景，确保其安全性已成为一个关键问题 zhou2025hidden； wang2025safety，特别是在防止生成有害或对抗性内容方面。

Although alignment methods ouyang2022training; rafailov2023direct enable reasoning models to handle standard (vanilla) jailbreak prompts to some extent, they remain vulnerable to more sophisticated adversarial jailbreak techniques. Figure 1, based on the MaliciousEducator dataset kuo2025h, illustrates how adversarial harmful prompts often conceal malicious intent through indirect and covert strategies, such as framing harmful requests as educational inquiries. As a result, model safety performance degrades substantially when confronted with such adversarial prompts.
尽管对齐方法如 ouyang2022training 和 rafailov2023direct 在一定程度上使推理模型能够处理标准（香草）越狱提示，但它们仍然容易受到更复杂的对抗性越狱技术的攻击。图 1 基于 MaliciousEducator 数据集 kuo2025h，展示了对抗性有害提示如何通过间接和隐蔽的策略隐藏恶意意图，例如将有害请求伪装成教育性询问。因此，当模型面临此类对抗性提示时，其安全性能会显著下降。

Additionally, adversarial jailbreak prompts generally exhibit two distinctive features: Limited availability, which includes a small number of prompts (usually only dozens) and an almost complete absence of corresponding reasoning responses suitable for fine-tuning. High structural and semantic variability, such as mathematical encodings and pedagogically styled questions kuo2025h; wildteaming2024; bethany2024jailbreaking. These characteristics pose significant difficulties for traditional alignment methods reliant on extensive human intervention, limiting their scalability and responsiveness to rapidly evolving threats.
此外，对抗性越狱提示通常具有两个显著特征：有限的可获得性，包括少量提示（通常只有几十个）以及几乎完全缺乏适合微调的相应推理响应。高结构和语义可变性，例如数学编码和教学风格的问题 kuo2025h; wildteaming2024; bethany2024jailbreaking。这些特征给依赖大量人工干预的传统对齐方法带来了重大困难，限制了它们对快速变化的威胁的扩展性和响应能力。

To address these challenges, we propose the Synthesized Guideline-based Adaptive Safety Alignment (SGASA) framework (Figure 2), a model-driven and automated alignment strategy. SGASA consists of two key components: (1) Data Pre-synthesis: synthesizes safety guidelines and augmented prompts aligned with a limited set of jailbreak examples. (2) Alignment Fine-tuning: internalizes synthesized safety guidelines via Supervised Fine-tuning (SFT) and Direct Preference Optimization (DPO) using guideline-enhanced responses. Extensive experiments on two reasoning models across three datasets demonstrate the effectiveness of SGASA. On the MaliciousEducator dataset, SGASA achieves safety improvements of 34.5% and 32.2% when applied to DeepSeek-R1-Distill-Qwen-7B and DeepSeek-R1-Distill-Llama-8B guo2025deepseek, respectively. Further analyses on data volume, data ratio, and cross-dataset generalization confirm the robustness of our approach. Our contributions are summarized as follows:

2 Related Work  2 相关工作

2.1 Safety Alignment.  2.1 安全对齐。

Safety alignment aims to prevent large language models from generating harmful or unethical content and has been widely studied. Representative approaches include Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) ouyang2022training, which optimizes models using reward signals derived from human preference rankings, and Direct Preference Optimization (DPO) rafailov2023direct, which directly converts human comparison data into a training objective without an explicit reward model.
安全对齐旨在防止大型语言模型生成有害或不道德的内容，并已得到广泛研究。代表性方法包括通过人类反馈的强化学习（RLHF）ouyang2022training，该方法使用从人类偏好排名中得出的奖励信号来优化模型，以及直接偏好优化（DPO）rafailov2023direct，该方法直接将人类比较数据转换为训练目标，而无需显式的奖励模型。

With the emergence of large reasoning models, recent work has explored “slow thinking” alignment strategies that leverage explicit reasoning to improve safety. These methods train models to recall and reason over textual safety specifications before generating responses. For example, deliberative alignment guan2024deliberative teaches models to apply written policy rules through Chain-of-Thought reasoning, achieving improved adversarial robustness while reducing over-refusal. Similarly, SafeChain jiang2025safechain fine-tunes models on CoT-style safety data. Other studies address related issues, such as mitigating unnecessary refusals via multi-turn reasoning wang2024don or identifying safety risks through introspective reasoning zhang2025safety.
随着大型推理模型的涌现，近期研究探索了利用显式推理的“慢思考”对齐策略来提升安全性。这些方法训练模型在生成回复前回忆和推理文本安全规范。例如，deliberative alignment（guan2024deliberative）通过思维链（Chain-of-Thought）推理，教导模型应用书面政策规则，在减少过度拒绝的同时提高了对抗鲁棒性。类似地，SafeChain（jiang2025safechain）在 CoT 风格的 safety 数据上微调模型。其他研究则处理相关问题，例如通过多轮推理减轻不必要的拒绝（wang2024don），或通过内省推理识别安全风险（zhang2025safety）。

2.2 Self-Alignment.  2.2 自我对齐。

As large language models continue to scale, alignment methods that rely heavily on human annotation face growing scalability challenges. This has motivated increasing interest in automated alignment approaches, particularly model self-alignment. Principle-driven self-alignment sun2023principle guides models using a compact set of human-defined principles, significantly reducing annotation costs. zhang2024self proposed a self-alignment framework that improves factual accuracy by leveraging models’ self-evaluation capabilities without external supervision. More recently, PT-ALIGN xu2025refining introduces a dual-objective self-alignment method that combines likelihood-based learning for benign data with unlikelihood training for toxic samples, effectively enhancing safety with minimal human involvement. Overall, self-alignment has emerged as a promising direction for scalable and efficient model alignment cao2024towards.
随着大型语言模型的持续扩展，严重依赖人工标注的对齐方法面临着日益增长的扩展性挑战。这促使人们对自动化对齐方法产生了越来越多的兴趣，特别是模型自我对齐。基于原则的自我对齐 sun2023principle 使用一组紧凑的人类定义原则来指导模型，显著降低了标注成本。zhang2024self 提出了一种自我对齐框架，通过利用模型的自评估能力来提高事实准确性，而无需外部监督。最近，PT-ALIGN xu2025refining 引入了一种双目标自我对齐方法，该方法结合了基于似然的学习来处理良性数据，以及针对毒性样本的不似然训练，有效地在最小人类参与的情况下增强了安全性。总体而言，自我对齐已成为可扩展和高效模型对齐的有前景的方向 cao2024towards。

3 Method   3 方法

In this section, we introduce our proposed framework named Synthesized Guidelines-based Adaptive Safety Alignment (SGASA), as shown in Figure 2, which consists of two stages: (1) Data Pre-synthesis (Section 3.1): This stage focuses on synthesizing key components essential for alignment, including safety guidelines and augmented prompts that emulate the characteristics of limited jailbreak examples. (2) Alignment Fine-tuning Stage (Section 3.2): The goal of this stage is to internalize the synthesized safety guidelines to improve the model’s robustness against jailbreak prompts. Using the guidelines and augmented prompts generated in the previous stage, the model produces guideline-enhanced responses for the prompts. These responses are then used to construct datasets for Supervised Fine-tuning (SFT) and Direct Preference Optimization (DPO).
在本节中，我们介绍了我们提出的名为基于综合指南的自适应安全对齐（SGASA）的框架，如图 2 所示，该框架包含两个阶段：（1）数据预综合阶段（第 3.1 节）：此阶段专注于综合对齐所必需的关键组件，包括安全指南和增强提示，这些提示模拟了有限越狱示例的特征。（2）对齐微调阶段（第 3.2 节）：此阶段的目标是将综合的安全指南内化，以提高模型对越狱提示的鲁棒性。使用前一阶段生成的指南和增强提示，模型为提示生成指南增强的响应。然后使用这些响应来构建用于监督微调（SFT）和直接偏好优化（DPO）的数据集。

3.1 Data Pre-synthesis   3.1 数据预合成

As illustrated in the upper part of Figure 2, this stage involves synthesizing safety guidelines and expanding the prompt set.
如图 2 上半部分所示，这一阶段涉及合成安全指南和扩展提示集。

The synthesis of safety guidelines comprises two steps: generation and validation. First, leveraging the model’s inherent reasoning capabilities, we construct safety guidelines based on multiple jailbreak prompts. Note that we randomly select an equal number of harmful and benign examples from a limited set of given samples. The instructions for safety guideline generation require the model to provide explanations for distinguishing benign from harmful prompts, as well as corresponding response strategies for each prompt type. The resulting safety guidelines are then evaluated by supplying them to the model alongside jailbreak prompts. Only the guidelines that meet specific validation criteria are retained. The jailbreak prompts used for evaluation are identical to those employed during the guideline generation phase. A guideline is considered valid if it satisfies the following criteria: (1) it leads the model to politely refuse harmful prompts, and (2) it allows the model to generate appropriate, non-refusal responses to benign prompts. For simplicity, we adopt a rule-based approach to detect refusal indicators in the model’s final output (e.g., phrases such as “sorry” or “cannot assist”). Figure 7 in the appendix provides an example of a synthesized safety guideline. The example illustrates how the guideline identifies harmful prompts based on linguistic cues, semantic content, and underlying intent. Notably, the model recognizes that many jailbreak attempts masquerade as educational queries, aligning with the original intent behind these attack strategies.
安全指南的合成包括两个步骤：生成和验证。首先，利用模型的内在推理能力，我们基于多个越狱提示构建安全指南。请注意，我们从有限的一组样本中随机选择相同数量的有害和无害示例。安全指南生成的指令要求模型提供区分无害和有害提示的解释，以及每种提示类型的相应响应策略。生成的安全指南随后通过将它们与越狱提示一起提供给模型进行评估。只有满足特定验证标准的指南被保留。用于评估的越狱提示与指南生成阶段使用的提示相同。如果指南满足以下标准，则被视为有效：(1) 它引导模型礼貌地拒绝有害提示，(2) 它允许模型对无害提示生成适当、非拒绝的响应。 为简化起见，我们采用基于规则的方法来检测模型最终输出中的拒绝指示（例如，“抱歉”或“无法协助”等短语）。附录中的图 7 提供了一个合成安全指南的示例。该示例说明了指南如何根据语言线索、语义内容和潜在意图识别有害提示。值得注意的是，模型认识到许多越狱尝试伪装成教育性查询，这与这些攻击策略背后的原始意图相一致。

To support fine tuning, we require a sufficient number of prompts that share characteristics with the provided examples. We therefore adopt a self instruction approach to generate additional training data. Harmful and benign prompts are synthesized separately, with each type generated only from source contexts of the same label. Even under this constraint, a substantial portion of the generated prompts may still fail to reflect their intended categories. To improve data quality, we further instruct the model to assess whether each generated prompt is consistent with its designated type. Prompts that do not meet the target criteria are filtered out, and only those that conform are retained for subsequent training. Implementation details are provided in the experimental settings (Section 4.1). The prompt templates are shown in Figure 4 and Figure 5 in the appendix.
为了支持微调，我们需要足够多的与提供的示例具有相似特征的提示。因此，我们采用自指导方法来生成额外的训练数据。有害提示和无害提示分别合成，每种类型仅从相同标签的源上下文中生成。即使在这种限制下，生成的大部分提示可能仍然无法反映其预期类别。为了提高数据质量，我们进一步指示模型评估每个生成的提示是否与其指定类型一致。不符合目标标准的提示被过滤掉，仅保留符合标准的提示用于后续训练。具体实现细节在实验设置（第 4.1 节）中提供。提示模板显示在附录中的图 4 和图 5 中。

3.2 Alignment Fine-tuning
3.2 对齐微调

As illustrated in Figure 2 (bottom), during the SFT stage, the model is trained to internalize safety guidelines and learn to apply them appropriately when responding to prompts. In the subsequent DPO stage, we construct preference data based on model-generated responses to further improve the model’s safety in handling harmful prompts, while simultaneously mitigating excessive refusals in response to benign ones.
如图 2（底部）所示，在 SFT 阶段，模型被训练以内化安全指南，并学习在回应提示时适当应用它们。在随后的 DPO 阶段，我们基于模型生成的响应构建偏好数据，以进一步改进模型处理有害提示时的安全性，同时减轻对良性提示的过度拒绝。

For SFT data collection, we randomly select a guideline from a predefined set and provide it as part of the input context along with the user’s request. The model is then instructed to generate a response that adheres to the given guideline. The responses are filtered as follows: for harmful prompts, only refusal responses are retained; for benign prompts, we use the model’s own scoring mechanism to rank candidate responses and select the highest-scoring one to ensure usefulness. 对于 SFT 数据收集，我们从预定义的指南集中随机选择一条指南，并将其作为输入上下文的一部分，连同用户请求一起提供。然后指示模型生成符合给定指南的响应。响应的筛选方式如下：对于有害提示，仅保留拒绝响应；对于良性提示，我们使用模型自身的评分机制对候选响应进行排序，并选择评分最高的响应以确保有用性。SFT 损失在公式 1 中定义，其中 qi,yiq_{i},y_{i} 代表上述描述中获得的提示和响应对。

4 Experiments   4 实验

4.1 Experimental Settings
4.1 实验设置

Datasets. To reflect low-resource alignment settings, we restrict training to 10 original jailbreak prompts per dataset, including 5 adversarial harmful and 5 adversarial benign examples.
数据集。为了反映低资源对齐设置，我们将训练限制在每个数据集的 10 个原始越狱提示上，包括 5 个对抗性有害示例和 5 个对抗性良性示例。

  • •

    WildJailbreak wildteaming2024 is an open-source safety dataset consisting of adversarial harmful prompts with concealed malicious intent and structurally similar adversarial benign prompts. We sample 5 prompts of each type for training and randomly select 210 prompts per type to form a 420-sample test set.

Self-Align is a fine-tuning-based approach in which the model generates and ranks its own responses to construct synthetic training data. In Self-Align (SFT), the highest-scoring response is used as the supervision target, whereas in Self-Align (DPO), the highest- and lowest-scoring responses are treated as chosen and rejected samples.

Evaluation. Following guan2024deliberative, we evaluate model performance in terms of both safety against harmful prompts and avoidance of excessive refusals for benign prompts, measured by the “Not unsafe” and “Not overrefuse” metrics, respectively. The “Not unsafe” metric evaluates the proportion of harmful samples that GPT-4o labels as safe. Conversely, the “Not overrefuse” metric measures the proportion of benign samples that GPT-4o judges as not excessively refused. An example of the evaluation instruction is shown in Figure 6. The overall performance score is computed by averaging the two metrics.

Implementation Details. In the data pre-synthesis stage, the model generates 10 verified safety guidelines. Starting from 10 limited jailbreak examples, we synthesize approximately 5,000 prompts for prompt augmentation to support effective response sampling during fine-tuning. During alignment fine-tuning, responses are sampled based on both the synthesized guidelines and the augmented prompts. For SFT, we select 250 qualified (prompt, response) pairs per data type, yielding 500 samples in total, while for DPO, we select 100 samples per data type, resulting in 200 samples. Only a small subset of synthesized prompts is ultimately used, as many fail to elicit responses meeting the required safety and quality standards.
实现细节。在数据预合成阶段，模型生成 10 条经过验证的安全指南。从 10 个有限的越狱示例开始，我们合成约 5000 个提示用于提示增强，以支持微调期间有效的响应采样。在对齐微调期间，响应根据合成的指南和增强的提示进行采样。对于 SFT，我们每种类别选择 250 个合格的（提示，响应）对，总共产生 500 个样本，而 DPO 每种类别选择 100 个样本，最终得到 200 个样本。最终只有一小部分合成的提示被使用，因为许多提示无法引出符合所需安全和质量标准的响应。

We adopt LoRA Hu2021LoRALA for efficient fine-tuning. All experiments are conducted for one epoch with a learning rate of 5×10−45\times 10^{-4}, a 10% warm-up ratio, and a cosine scheduler. Inference is performed using vLLM kwon2023efficient with a temperature of 0.6 and a maximum sequence length of 2048. All experiments are run on a single 80GB A800 GPU.
我们采用 LoRA Hu2021LoRALA 进行高效的微调。所有实验均使用学习率为 5×10−45\times 10^{-4} 、10%的预热比例和余弦调度器进行单轮训练。推理过程使用 vLLM kwon2023efficient，温度设置为 0.6，最大序列长度为 2048。所有实验均在单块 80GB A800 GPU 上运行。

4.3 Impact of Data Quantity and Distribution on Alignment Fine-Tuning
4.3 数据数量和分布对对齐微调的影响

In this subsection, we study how the quantity and ratio of synthetic safety-guided data affect alignment performance. Figure 3 (top) reports SFT results across three datasets, with the harmful-to-benign ratio fixed at 1:1. We summarize three key observations. (1) Diminishing returns with increasing data volume. Increasing training data generally improves performance, but marginal gains decrease as scale grows. When increasing from 200 to 800 samples, safety scores improve despite a slight rise in overrefuse, leading to better overall alignment. However, performance slightly degrades at 1000 samples across models and datasets, indicating potential overfitting. This effect likely stems from inherent performance ceilings and noise in self-synthesized data. Thus, effective alignment requires balancing data scale with quality control to mitigate bias amplification in feedback loops liu2024best; dong2025selfboosting. (2) Stronger models exhibit better self-alignment. R1-Llama-8B consistently outperforms R1-Qwen-7B across all datasets and metrics, showing greater stability and alignment efficiency. This advantage is attributed to its larger parameter scale, which supports higher-quality synthetic data generation and more effective learning from alignment data. (3) Task-specific sensitivity to data quantity. The impact of data volume varies by task. Performance on MaliciousEducator and WildJailbreak remains relatively stable as data increases, whereas MathPrompt exhibits greater volatility. This suggests that simply increasing data volume may not benefit all tasks. For adversarial settings such as MathPrompt, alternative methods like DPO, which emphasize preference discrimination, may provide more targeted alignment benefits.
在本小节中，我们研究了合成安全指导数据的数量和比例如何影响对齐性能。图 3（上）报告了在有害与无害比例固定为 1:1 的情况下，跨三个数据集的 SFT 结果。我们总结了三个关键观察结果。（1）随着数据量的增加，收益递减。增加训练数据通常会提高性能，但随着规模的扩大，边际收益会减少。当从 200 增加到 800 个样本时，尽管过度满足略有上升，安全分数有所提高，从而带来更好的整体对齐。然而，在所有模型和数据集上，1000 个样本时性能略有下降，表明可能存在过拟合。这种效应可能源于固有的性能上限和自我合成数据中的噪声。因此，有效的对齐需要平衡数据规模与质量控制，以减轻反馈循环中的偏差放大[liu2024best; dong2025selfboosting]。（2）更强的模型表现出更好的自我对齐。R1-Llama-8B 在所有数据集和指标上始终优于 R1-Qwen-7B，显示出更高的稳定性和对齐效率。 这一优势归因于其更大的参数规模，这支持了更高品质的合成数据生成，并能更有效地从对齐数据中学习。（3）任务特定的数据量敏感性。数据量的影响因任务而异。随着数据量的增加，MaliciousEducator 和 WildJailbreak 的性能保持相对稳定，而 MathPrompt 则表现出更大的波动性。这表明简单地增加数据量可能不会对所有任务都有益。对于 MathPrompt 等对抗性设置，强调偏好区分的 DPO 等替代方法可能会提供更具针对性的对齐效益。

For example, on WildJailbreak with R1-Llama-8B, the safety score drops from 0.914 to 0.876, while the “Not overrefuse” score rises from 0.843 to 0.886, reflecting a well-known alignment tension tan2025equilibraterlhfbalancinghelpfulnesssafety. Second, data ratio tuning enables fine-grained behavioral control. A balanced 1:1 ratio is often suboptimal, while moderately skewed ratios such as 3:5 or 5:3 typically yield higher average scores. This indicates that sample distribution should be adjusted based on post-SFT model behavior. In summary, both the quantity and ratio of training data substantially influence the model’s final alignment equilibrium. Adjusting the harmful-to-benign ratio provides an effective mechanism for calibrating behavioral boundaries and response thresholds.
图 3（底部）展示了在不同数据比例下的 DPO 结果。首先，我们观察到明显的权衡关系：增加良性提示的比例会降低安全性，但能缓解过度满足。例如，在 WildJailbreak 上使用 R1-Llama-8B 时，安全评分从 0.914 降至 0.876，而“未过度满足”评分从 0.843 升至 0.886，这反映了众所周知的对齐张力 tan2025equilibraterlhfbalancinghelpfulnesssafety。其次，数据比例调整可以实现细粒度的行为控制。平衡的 1:1 比例往往不是最优的，而适度倾斜的比例如 3:5 或 5:3 通常能获得更高的平均分数。这表明样本分布应根据 SFT 后模型的行为进行调整。总之，训练数据的数量和比例都会显著影响模型的最终对齐均衡。调整有害与良性的比例提供了校准行为边界和响应阈值的有效机制。

5 Conclusion  5 结论

In this work, we propose SGASA, an adaptive framework designed to enhance the safety alignment of reasoning models against evolving adversarial jailbreak prompts. By synthesizing and integrating safety guidelines into reasoning models through fine-tuning strategies, SGASA effectively balances improved safety performance with a reduction in unnecessary refusals. Comprehensive evaluations across diverse models and datasets demonstrate that SGASA significantly outperforms existing approaches, highlighting its robust and generalizable capabilities in safety alignment. Future work will focus on refining the generation of synthetic safety guidelines and extending adaptive safety techniques to a wider range of adversarial inputs, ensuring continuous improvement in model safety.
在这项工作中，我们提出了 SGASA，一个旨在增强推理模型对不断变化的对抗性越狱提示的安全对齐的自适应框架。通过微调策略将安全指南合成并集成到推理模型中，SGASA 有效地在提高安全性能和减少不必要的拒绝之间取得了平衡。在多种模型和数据集上的综合评估表明，SGASA 显著优于现有方法，突出了其在安全对齐方面的鲁棒性和泛化能力。未来的工作将集中于改进合成安全指南的生成，并将自适应安全技术扩展到更广泛的对抗性输入，以确保模型安全的持续改进。

6 Limitations  6 局限性

Despite the strong empirical gains, SGASA has several limitations. First, our evaluation focuses on medium-scale reasoning models; while they exhibit representative jailbreak behaviors, the scalability of SGASA to larger or proprietary models remains to be explored. Second, while we address semantic and structural obfuscations, we do not evaluate against optimization-based attacks (e.g., gradient-free search or adaptive multi-turn attacks). Third, the self-synthesized data may inherit biases or blind spots of the base model; this could be mitigated in future work through scalable oversight mechanisms, such as cross-model guideline generation, ensemble-based verification, or stronger automated critics. Finally, relying on GPT-4o as an automated evaluator may introduce systematic biases. SGASA is a step toward adaptive safety alignment, but further research is needed to ensure robustness against evolving threats.
尽管 SGASA 取得了显著的实证成果，但它仍存在一些局限性。首先，我们的评估集中在中等规模的推理模型上；虽然这些模型表现出具有代表性的越狱行为，但 SGASA 在更大规模或专有模型上的可扩展性仍有待探索。其次，虽然我们处理了语义和结构上的混淆，但我们并未针对基于优化的攻击（例如无梯度搜索或自适应多轮攻击）进行评估。第三，自合成数据可能继承基础模型的偏差或盲点；这可以通过可扩展的监督机制在未来工作中得到缓解，例如跨模型指南生成、集成式验证或更强的自动化评论。最后，依赖 GPT-4o 作为自动化评估器可能会引入系统性偏差。SGASA 是自适应安全对齐方向上的一步，但需要进一步研究以确保其对抗不断演变的威胁的鲁棒性。
