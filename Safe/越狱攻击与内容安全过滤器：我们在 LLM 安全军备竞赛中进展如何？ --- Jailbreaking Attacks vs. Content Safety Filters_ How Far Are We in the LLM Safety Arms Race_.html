Jailbreaking Attacks vs. Content Safety Filters: How Far Are We in the LLM Safety Arms Race?
越狱攻击与内容安全过滤器：我们在 LLM 安全军备竞赛中进展如何？

Yuan Xin1, Dingfan Chen2, Linyi Yang3, Michael Backes1, Xiao Zhang1
1CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security, Germany
2Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems, Germany
3Southern University of Science and Technology, China
Abstract  摘要

As large language models (LLMs) are increasingly deployed, ensuring their safe use is paramount. Jailbreaking, adversarial prompts that bypass model alignment to trigger harmful outputs, present significant risks, with existing studies reporting high success rates in evading common LLMs. However, previous evaluations have focused solely on the models, neglecting the full deployment pipeline, which typically incorporates additional safety mechanisms like content moderation filters. To address this gap, we present the first systematic evaluation of jailbreak attacks targeting LLM safety alignment, assessing their success across the full inference pipeline, including both input and output filtering stages. Our findings yield two key insights: first, nearly all evaluated jailbreak techniques can be detected by at least one safety filter, suggesting that prior assessments may have overestimated the practical success of these attacks; second, while safety filters are effective in detection, there remains room to better balance recall and precision to further optimize protection and user experience. We highlight critical gaps and call for further refinement of detection accuracy and usability in LLM safety systems.
随着大型语言模型（LLMs）的日益广泛部署，确保其安全使用至关重要。越狱攻击，即绕过模型对齐以触发有害输出的对抗性提示，带来了重大风险，现有研究报道了在规避常见 LLMs 方面的高成功率。然而，之前的评估仅关注模型本身，忽视了完整部署流程，该流程通常包含内容审核过滤器等额外的安全机制。为填补这一空白，我们首次对针对 LLM 安全对齐的越狱攻击进行了系统评估，评估了它们在整个推理流程中的成功率，包括输入和输出过滤阶段。我们的研究得出两项关键见解：首先，几乎所有评估的越狱技术至少能被一种安全过滤器检测到，这表明之前的评估可能高估了这些攻击的实际成功率；其次，虽然安全过滤器在检测方面很有效，但仍需更好地平衡召回率和精确率，以进一步优化保护和用户体验。我们强调了关键差距，并呼吁进一步改进 LLM 安全系统中的检测准确性和可用性。

1 Introduction  1 引言

Large Language Models (LLMs) have demonstrated tremendous progress in recent years and become integral to a wide array of applications, ranging from conversational AI and content creation to code generation and scientific research liu2024llm; yao2024survey; li2024personal; minaee2024large; dubey2024llama; achiam2023gpt. Their ability to generate human-like responses has unlocked unprecedented levels of automation and accessibility. However, significant security and safety concerns have emerged as LLMs are increasingly deployed in real-world applications. One of the most critical challenges is the potential misuse of these models to generate harmful, toxic, or hateful content, particularly on sensitive topics gehman2020realtoxicityprompts; bommasani2021opportunities; weidinger2021ethical; tamkin2021understanding.
大型语言模型（LLMs）近年来取得了巨大进步，已成为各种应用的组成部分，从对话式人工智能和内容创作到代码生成和科学研究的广泛应用 liu2024llm; yao2024survey; li2024personal; minaee2024large; dubey2024llama; achiam2023gpt。它们生成类人回复的能力解锁了前所未有的自动化和可访问性。然而，随着 LLMs 越来越多地部署在实际应用中，出现了重大的安全和安全问题。其中最关键的一个挑战是这些模型可能被用于生成有害、有毒或仇恨内容，特别是在敏感话题上 gehman2020realtoxicityprompts; bommasani2021opportunities; weidinger2021ethical; tamkin2021understanding。

To address these concerns, deployed LLMs are typically subjected to safety alignment ouyang2022training; kenton2021alignment; bai2022constitutional; korbak2023pretraining; rafailov2024direct, a process where models are fine-tuned with carefully curated datasets and explicit constraints to recognize and avoid generating unsafe outputs. While safety alignment is effective in many cases, it is not foolproof. A growing body of research has demonstrated that LLMs remain vulnerable to jailbreaking—adversarial attacks that craft inputs specifically designed to bypass these internal safety mechanisms and induce the model to generate unethical, harmful, or policy-violating outputs zou2023universal; liu2023autodan; chao2023jailbreaking; mehrotra2023tree; yuan2023gpt; andriushchenko2024jailbreaking. The increasing sophistication of jailbreak techniques poses a significant threat to the reliability and safety of LLM-based systems, highlighting the need for robust defense mechanisms to safeguard their deployment and ensure they operate within acceptable safety boundaries.
为解决这些问题，部署中的 LLM 通常会进行安全对齐训练，通过精心策划的数据集和明确的约束来微调模型，使其能够识别并避免生成不安全的输出。尽管安全对齐在很多情况下是有效的，但它并非万无一失。越来越多的研究表明，LLM 仍然容易受到越狱攻击——这是一种专门设计输入以绕过内部安全机制、诱使模型生成不道德、有害或违反政策的输出的对抗性攻击。越狱技术的日益复杂对基于 LLM 系统的可靠性和安全性构成了重大威胁，凸显了需要建立强大的防御机制来保障其部署，并确保它们在可接受的安全范围内运行。

Addressing the growing challenge of jailbreak attacks necessitates robust and adaptable defense mechanisms. While prior research has primarily focused on model-level interventions, such as fine-tuning, adversarial training, and safe decoding techniques cao2023defending; yi2024jailbreak; xu2024safedecoding; mo2024fight, an alternative and complementary approach is system-level content filtering. Content filters operate externally to the model, analyzing and blocking unsafe inputs before they reach the LLM or filtering harmful outputs before they are presented to the user inan2023llama; PromptGuard; markov2023holistic; xie2024gradsafe. Despite their potential, content filters remain underexplored, and their real-world effectiveness against jailbreak attacks is not well understood. Given that jailbreak attacks often exploit subtle weaknesses in model behavior, evaluating the strengths and limitations of content filtering is critical for determining whether it provides a viable line of defense or if adversaries can easily circumvent it. This ongoing arms race between increasingly sophisticated jailbreak techniques and evolving defensive measures necessitates a deeper examination of content filters’ role in safeguarding LLMs.
应对日益严峻的越狱攻击挑战，需要构建强大且灵活的防御机制。以往研究主要集中于模型层面的干预措施，如微调、对抗训练和安全解码技术 cao2023defending; yi2024jailbreak; xu2024safedecoding; mo2024fight；而系统层面的内容过滤则是一种替代性和补充性方法。内容过滤器在模型外部运行，通过分析并阻止不安全输入以防止其到达 LLM，或在有害输出呈现给用户前进行过滤 inan2023llama; PromptGuard; markov2023holistic; xie2024gradsafe。尽管内容过滤器具有潜力，但它们仍被探索不足，其在现实世界中对越狱攻击的有效性尚未得到充分理解。鉴于越狱攻击常利用模型行为的细微弱点，评估内容过滤的优势与局限性对于判断其是否提供可行的防御线或攻击者是否容易绕过它至关重要。这场日益复杂的越狱技巧与不断发展的防御措施之间的军备竞赛，要求我们更深入地审视内容过滤器在保护 LLMs 方面的作用。

We develop a more comprehensive evaluation dataset by consolidating existing jailbreak benchmarks and systematically removing semantically redundant queries. The final set consists of 417 distinct harmful prompts spanning 10 harm categories, each paired with a topic-aligned benign counterpart, enabling rigorous and controlled assessment of jailbreak detection systems. Our findings reveal that most jailbreak attacks can be reliably detected, positioning jailbreak detectors as an effective defense against these threats. However, some more advanced attacks manage to evade detection at the cost of reduced overall attack performance. This trade-off highlights the ongoing challenge of balancing attack effectiveness with the ability to bypass safety filters, underscoring the need for continued development in both attack and defense strategies. To our knowledge, this work represents the first comprehensive analysis of safety filters against top-performing jailbreak attacks, providing a standardized approach to assess their resilience.
我们通过整合现有的越狱基准测试并系统地移除语义冗余的查询，构建了一个更全面的评估数据集。最终数据集包含 417 个不同的有害提示，涵盖 10 个危害类别，每个提示都配有一个主题对齐的良性对应物，从而能够对越狱检测系统进行严格和受控的评估。我们的研究发现，大多数越狱攻击可以被可靠地检测到，这使得越狱检测器成为对抗这些威胁的有效防御手段。然而，一些更高级的攻击设法规避了检测，但代价是整体攻击性能的降低。这种权衡突显了在攻击有效性与绕过安全过滤器能力之间保持平衡的持续挑战，强调了攻击和防御策略持续发展的必要性。据我们所知，这项工作代表了首次对安全过滤器针对顶级性能越狱攻击的全面分析，提供了一种标准化的方法来评估其韧性。

2 Related Work  2 相关工作

Jailbreak Attack.  越狱攻击。

Jailbreaking of LLMs has become a critical challenge, where adversaries craft prompts that bypass safety mechanisms to generate harmful outputs yi2024jailbreak; shayegani2023jailbreak; jin2024attackeval; xiao2024distract. Existing attacks can be classified into five categories: optimization-based, LLM-assisted, obfuscation-based, function/tool-based, and multi-turn. Optimization-based attacks refine adversarial prompts using algorithmic techniques. GCG zou2023universal applies gradient-guided optimization to construct universal adversarial prompts, while AutoDAN liu2023autodan employs hierarchical genetic algorithms. Adaptive Attacks andriushchenko2024jailbreaking introduced dynamic strategies that adjust to evolving LLM defenses. LLM-assisted attacks employ auxiliary LLMs to generate jailbreak prompts autonomously. PAIR chao2023jailbreaking utilizes an LLM for red-teaming, TAP mehrotra2023tree applies tree-search methods to refine queries, and AdvPrompter paulus2024advprompter trains a separate LLM to generate human-readable adversarial suffixes ∼\sim800×\times faster than optimization-based approaches. Obfuscation-based attacks conceal harmful intent through paraphrasing, scenario nesting, or encryption. DeepInception li2023deepinception exploits LLMs’ personification ability to construct multi-layered scenarios, while ReNeLLM ding2024wolf employs prompt rewriting and scenario nesting. Decomposition attacks like DrAttack li2024drattack split harmful prompts into simpler sub-components to evade safety filters ye2023large; liu2023goal. Function/tool-based attacks exploit LLMs’ capability to call external functions or tools. CodeChameleon lv2024codechameleon recasts malicious queries as function-based code completion tasks, embedding a decryption function within the prompt to reconstruct harmful instructions while bypassing intent security checks. Multi-turn attacks leverage extended conversations to gradually steer LLMs toward harmful outputs. Crescendo russinovich2025great begins with benign questions and progressively escalates by referencing the model’s own responses, evading input filters that focus on individual prompts rather than conversational context. These strategies closely mimic benign queries, making them difficult for current safety frameworks to detect and mitigate.
LLMs 的越狱攻击已成为一个关键挑战，攻击者设计提示以绕过安全机制来生成有害输出 yi2024jailbreak; shayegani2023jailbreak; jin2024attackeval; xiao2024distract。现有攻击可分为五类：基于优化的、基于 LLM 的、基于混淆的、基于函数/工具的和多轮的。基于优化的攻击使用算法技术来优化对抗性提示。GCG zou2023universal 应用梯度引导优化来构建通用对抗性提示，而 AutoDAN liu2023autodan 采用分层遗传算法。Adaptive Attacks andriushchenko2024jailbreaking 引入了动态策略，以适应不断发展的 LLM 防御。基于 LLM 的攻击使用辅助 LLM 自动生成越狱提示。PAIR chao2023jailbreaking 使用 LLM 进行红队演练，TAP mehrotra2023tree 应用树搜索方法来优化查询，AdvPrompter paulus2024advprompter 训练一个独立的 LLM 来生成人类可读的对抗性后缀 ∼\sim 800 ×\times ，比基于优化的方法更快。 基于混淆的攻击通过释义、场景嵌套或加密来隐藏有害意图。DeepInception li2023deepinception 利用 LLMs 的人格化能力构建多层场景，而 ReNeLLM ding2024wolf 采用提示重写和场景嵌套。分解攻击如 DrAttack li2024drattack 将有害提示分解为更简单的子组件以规避安全过滤器 ye2023large; liu2023goal。基于函数/工具的攻击利用 LLMs 调用外部函数或工具的能力。CodeChameleon lv2024codechameleon 将恶意查询重构为基于函数的代码补全任务，在提示中嵌入解密函数以重建有害指令同时绕过意图安全检查。多轮攻击利用扩展对话逐步引导 LLMs 产生有害输出。Crescendo russinovich2025great 从良性问题开始，通过引用模型自身的响应逐步升级，规避专注于单个提示而非对话上下文输入过滤器。这些策略与良性查询高度相似，使它们难以被当前安全框架检测和缓解。

Jailbreak Defense. In the literature, System-level and model-level defenses are two complementary approaches to safeguarding LLMs against jailbreak attacks. Model-level defenses involve altering the model’s architecture or training processes, such as safety training and fine-tuning, refusal mechanisms, and adversarial training ouyang2022training; bai2022constitutional; korbak2023pretraining; rafailov2024direct. These methods, often fine-tuned with safety datasets or manual red-teaming, equip models with the ability to recognize and reject harmful inputs. In contrast, system-level defenses operate externally to the LLM, adding safety measures on top of the target model. These include input and output filtering inan2023llama; PromptGuard; markov2023holistic; xie2024gradsafe, input sanitization jain2023baseline, and constrained inference rebedea2023nemo. While prior work has primarily focused on model-level defenses, system-level approaches—such as the content filtering investigated in this work—have received limited exploration. Our work addresses this gap by systematically studying the effectiveness of system-level defenses, providing a more comprehensive understanding of their role in mitigating jailbreak attacks.
越狱防御。在文献中，系统级防御和模型级防御是两种互补的方法，用于保护 LLMs 免受越狱攻击。模型级防御涉及改变模型的架构或训练过程，例如安全训练和微调、拒绝机制以及对抗性训练（ouyang2022training; bai2022constitutional; korbak2023pretraining; rafailov2024direct）。这些方法通常使用安全数据集或手动红队进行微调，使模型具备识别和拒绝有害输入的能力。相比之下，系统级防御在 LLM 外部运行，在目标模型之上添加安全措施。这些包括输入和输出过滤（inan2023llama; PromptGuard; markov2023holistic; xie2024gradsafe）、输入清理（jain2023baseline）和约束推理（rebedea2023nemo）。虽然以往工作主要集中于模型级防御，但系统级方法——例如本工作研究的内容过滤——却受到了有限的探索。 我们的研究通过系统地研究系统级防御的有效性，弥补了这一空白，从而更全面地理解它们在减轻越狱攻击中的作用。

Benchmarking LLM Safety. Our work is related to recent efforts in benchmarking the vulnerabilities of LLMs and systematically summarizing various jailbreaking attacks. Many existing benchmarks, such as PromptBench zhu2023promptbench, DecodingTrust wang2024decodingtrust, HarmBench mazeika2024harmbench, and JailbreakBench chao2024jailbreakbench provide structured comparisons of attack methodologies. However, these benchmarks primarily focus on attack effectiveness, often overlooking systematic evaluations of defensive strategies. In contrast, our work specifically investigates test-time defenses, with a primary emphasis on content filtering mechanisms markov2023holistic; inan2023llama; xie2024gradsafe. While content filtering is frequently mentioned as a potential defense in the literature, it has not been systematically evaluated in the context of jailbreaking. By focusing on this critical and underexplored defense mechanism, we offer an important complement to existing efforts. Our work also emphasizes a community-driven approach, encouraging the continuous and structured addition of new attacks, models, and defenses to the benchmark, fostering a collaborative environment for improving LLM safety.
评估 LLM 安全。我们的工作与近期评估 LLM 漏洞和系统性地总结各种越狱攻击的努力相关。许多现有基准，如 PromptBench（zhu2023promptbench）、DecodingTrust（wang2024decodingtrust）、HarmBench（mazeika2024harmbench）和 JailbreakBench（chao2024jailbreakbench），提供了攻击方法的结构化比较。然而，这些基准主要关注攻击效果，往往忽略了防御策略的系统评估。相比之下，我们的工作专门研究测试时防御，重点在于内容过滤机制（markov2023holistic；inan2023llama；xie2024gradsafe）。虽然内容过滤在文献中经常被提及作为一种潜在的防御措施，但在越狱的背景下尚未得到系统评估。通过聚焦这一关键且尚未充分探索的防御机制，我们为现有工作提供了重要的补充。 我们的工作还强调社区驱动的方法，鼓励持续且有组织地向基准添加新的攻击、模型和防御措施，为改进 LLM 安全营造协作环境。

3.1 Problem Formulation  3.1 问题公式化

Jailbreak Attack. Let 𝖫𝖫𝖬\mathsf{LLM} : 𝒯∗\mathcal{T}^{*} →\to 𝒯∗\mathcal{T}^{*} be a target LLM, which maps a sequence of input tokens to a sequence of output tokens. A jailbreak attack aims to design input prompts that cause the target LLM to generate text that is harmful, toxic, or objectionable. Formally, let 𝖩𝗎𝖽𝗀𝖾\mathsf{Judge} :: (𝒯∗,𝒯∗)(\mathcal{T}^{*},\mathcal{T}^{*}) →\to {0,1}\{0,1\} be a judge model that decides whether the generated output 𝖫𝖫𝖬​(P)\mathsf{LLM}(P) aligns with a predefined harmful goal GG. The 𝖩𝗎𝖽𝗀𝖾\mathsf{Judge} returns 11 if 𝖫𝖫𝖬​(P)\mathsf{LLM}(P) is deemed harmful (i.e., satisfies GG), and 0 otherwise. The objective of an attack is to exploit vulnerabilities in 𝖫𝖫𝖬\mathsf{LLM} by crafting inputs that elicit harmful responses from 𝖫𝖫𝖬\mathsf{LLM}, which can be formalized as:
越狱攻击。设 𝖫𝖫𝖬\mathsf{LLM} : 𝒯∗\mathcal{T}^{*} →\to 𝒯∗\mathcal{T}^{*} 为一个目标 LLM，它将输入标记序列映射到输出标记序列。越狱攻击旨在设计输入提示，使目标 LLM 生成有害、有毒或令人反感的文本。形式上，设 𝖩𝗎𝖽𝗀𝖾\mathsf{Judge} :: (𝒯∗,𝒯∗)(\mathcal{T}^{*},\mathcal{T}^{*}) →\to {0,1}\{0,1\} 为一个判断模型，它决定生成的输出 𝖫𝖫𝖬​(P)\mathsf{LLM}(P) 是否与预定义的有害目标 GG 一致。如果 𝖫𝖫𝖬​(P)\mathsf{LLM}(P) 被认为是有害的（即满足 GG ），则 𝖩𝗎𝖽𝗀𝖾\mathsf{Judge} 返回 11 ，否则返回 0 。攻击的目标是通过设计输入来利用 𝖫𝖫𝖬\mathsf{LLM} 中的漏洞，使 𝖫𝖫𝖬\mathsf{LLM} 产生有害响应，这可以形式化为：

where PP is the input prompt and 𝒯∗{\mathcal{T}}^{*} denotes the set of all sequences of tokens of arbitrary length.

Content Filtering. Typically, jailbreak attacks focus solely on bypassing the inherent alignment of LLMs without considering additional filtering mechanisms. However, real-world systems often incorporate an additional safety filtering mechanism to mitigate harmful outputs, creating a gap in existing research that largely overlooks the impact of these filters. The simplest version is a binary classifier, denoted as 𝖥𝗂𝗅𝗍𝖾𝗋bin\mathsf{Filter}_{\text{bin}} :: 𝒯∗\mathcal{T}^{*} →\to {0,1}\{0,1\} where 𝖥𝗂𝗅𝗍𝖾𝗋bin​(P)\mathsf{Filter}_{\text{bin}}(P) == 11 indicates harmful content. If harmful content is detected, the system may block or modify the input using a modifying filter. 3.2 Safety Filters  3.2 安全过滤器

While substantial research has focused on harmful content filtering ghorbanpour2025fine; zampieri2019predicting; bourgeade2023did, these systems have rarely been tested against jailbreak attacks, leaving a critical gap in understanding the safety arms race. We bridge this gap by systematically applying established filtering methods to detect both jailbreak prompts and adversarial outputs.
尽管大量研究集中于有害内容过滤技术，如 Ghorbanpour (2025) 的精细过滤、Zampieri (2019) 的预测模型以及 Bourgeade (2023) 的实证研究，但这些系统很少经过越狱攻击的测试，导致在理解安全军备竞赛方面存在关键空白。我们通过系统性地应用既有的过滤方法来检测越狱提示和对抗性输出，从而填补这一空白。

General-Purpose LLMs as Zero-Shot Detectors. General-purpose LLMs leverage inherent capabilities to detect harmful content without task-specific adaptation. Safety-aligned models like GPT-4 achiam2023gpt and O3 o3mini2024 are trained on large-scale corpora and undergo extensive alignment to identify harmful prompts and outputs, with O3 leveraging reasoning capabilities to engage more deeply with semantic content liu2025logical; el2025competitive.
通用型 LLMs 作为零样本检测器。通用型 LLMs 利用其固有能力在不进行特定任务适配的情况下检测有害内容。与安全对齐的模型如 GPT-4 (Achiam, 2023) 和 O3 (o3mini, 2024) 在大规模语料库上训练，并经过广泛的对齐过程以识别有害提示和输出，其中 O3 借助推理能力更深入地处理语义内容 (Liu, 2025; El, 2025)。

Fine-Tuned LLMs as Standalone Classifiers. Fine-tuned LLMs are optimized for specific risks. LlamaGuard inan2023llama is fine-tuned on a safety taxonomy to classify violence and hate speech. PromptGuard PromptGuard is trained on adversarial data to detect benign, injected, and jailbreak prompts. InjecGuard li2024injecguard introduces the NotInject dataset to mitigate over-defense, enabling accurate discrimination between benign and injected prompts. OpenAI’s Content Moderation API markov2023holistic uses hybrid LLM-based classification and active learning for real-world filtering.
精调 LLMs 作为独立分类器。精调 LLMs 针对特定风险进行了优化。LlamaGuard inan2023llama 在安全分类法上进行精调，用于分类暴力和仇恨言论。PromptGuard PromptGuard 在对抗性数据上进行训练，用于检测良性、注入和越狱提示。InjecGuard li2024injecguard 引入了 NotInject 数据集以减轻过度防御，能够准确区分良性提示和注入提示。OpenAI 的内容审核 API markov2023holistic 使用基于 LLM 的混合分类和主动学习进行现实世界过滤。

Gradient-based Detectors. GradSafe (xie2024gradsafe) detects jailbreak prompts by analyzing gradient patterns of safety-critical parameters. When processing jailbreak prompts paired with compliance responses, LLM gradients exhibit consistent patterns distinct from safe prompts, enabling accurate detection without additional training.
基于梯度的检测器。GradSafe (xie2024gradsafe)通过分析安全关键参数的梯度模式来检测越狱提示。在处理与合规响应配对的越狱提示时，LLM 梯度表现出与安全提示不同的稳定模式，无需额外训练即可实现准确检测。

4.2 Analysis of Detector’s Behavior
4.2 检测器行为分析

The results in Section 4.1 show that most existing jailbreak attacks—especially those not explicitly designed to bypass safety filters—can often be blocked by current moderation systems, potentially leading to an overestimation of their true success rates. This raises a central question: Is simply integrating a content safety filter into the LLM inference pipeline sufficient to resolve safety concerns? Can we effectively end the safety arms race by simply adding extra costs through content filters and enforcing them in the pipeline? While this seems promising, we discuss its limitations below.

5 Conclusion  5 结论

In this work, we conducted the first comprehensive evaluation of jailbreak detection systems, and our analysis provides a standardized assessment of safety filters against top-performing jailbreak attacks, offering valuable insights into their effectiveness. Yet, success at blocking existing attacks does not absolve detectors from scrutiny: when the recall is already high, the focus should shift to lowering false positives. The result highlights the ongoing trade-off between adversarial success and evading safety mechanisms, pointing to the need for steady progress on both attack and defense, and for improving filter precision without loss of recall.
