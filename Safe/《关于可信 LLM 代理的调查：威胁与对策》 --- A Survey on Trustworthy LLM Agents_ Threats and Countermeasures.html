A Survey on Trustworthy LLM Agents: Threats and Countermeasures
对可信 LLM 智能体的调查：威胁与对策

Miao Yu1,†, Fanci Meng1,†, Xinyun Zhou4, Shilong Wang1, Junyuan Mao1, Linsey Pang2,
Tianlong Chen3, Kun Wang4, Xinfeng Li4,∗, Yongfeng Zhang5, Bo An4, Qingsong Wen1,∗ 1Squirrel AI Learning, 2Salesforce, 3The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill,
4Nanyang Technological University, 5Rutgers University

Abstract.  摘要。

With the rapid evolution of Large Language Models (LLMs), LLM-based agents and Multi-agent Systems (MAS) have significantly expanded the capabilities of LLM ecosystems. This evolution stems from empowering LLMs with additional modules such as memory, tools, environment, and even other agents. However, this advancement has also introduced more complex issues of trustworthiness, which previous research focusing solely on LLMs could not cover. In this survey, we propose the TrustAgent framework, a comprehensive study on the trustworthiness of agents, characterized by modular taxonomy, multi-dimensional connotations, and technical implementation. By thoroughly investigating and summarizing newly emerged attacks, defenses, and evaluation methods for agents and MAS, we extend the concept of Trustworthy LLM to the emerging paradigm of Trustworthy Agent. In TrustAgent, we begin by deconstructing and introducing various components of the Agent and MAS. Then, we categorize their trustworthiness into intrinsic (brain, memory, and tool) and extrinsic (user, agent, and environment) aspects. Subsequently, we delineate the multifaceted meanings of trustworthiness and elaborate on the implementation techniques of existing research related to these internal and external modules. Finally, we present our insights and outlook on this domain, aiming to provide guidance for future endeavors. For easy reference, we categorize all the studies mentioned in this survey according to our taxonomy, available at: https://github.com/Ymm-cll/TrustAgent.
随着大型语言模型（LLM）的快速发展，基于 LLM 的智能体和多智能体系统（MAS）显著扩展了 LLM 生态系统的能力。这种发展源于通过添加记忆、工具、环境甚至其他智能体等模块来增强 LLM。然而，这种进步也引入了更复杂的可信性问题，这些问题是先前仅关注 LLM 的研究无法涵盖的。在本调查中，我们提出了 TrustAgent 框架，这是一个关于智能体可信性的全面研究，其特点在于模块化分类、多维内涵和技术实现。通过深入研究和总结智能体和 MAS 的新兴攻击、防御和评估方法，我们将可信 LLM 的概念扩展到可信智能体这一新兴范式。在 TrustAgent 中，我们首先分解并介绍了智能体和 MAS 的各种组件。然后，我们将它们的可信性分为内在（大脑、记忆和工具）和外在（用户、智能体和环境）两个方面。 随后，我们阐述了可信性的多维度含义，并详细介绍了与这些内部和外部模块相关的研究中现有的实现技术。最后，我们提出了对该领域的见解和展望，旨在为未来的工作提供指导。为了方便参考，我们将本综述中提到的所有研究按照我们的分类体系进行分类，可在以下网址获取：https://github.com/Ymm-cll/TrustAgent。

1. Introduction  1. 引言

The advent of large language models (LLMs) has catalyzed a paradigm shift in artificial intelligence systems (Zhao et al., 2023; Minaee et al., 2024; Chang et al., 2024; Wang et al., 2024b). The integration of LLMs as backbone with extra modules (e.g. memory (Zhang et al., 2024a), tool (Masterman et al., 2024; Qin et al., 2024), and environment (Yang et al., 2024c)) as extensions produces the concept of “LLM-based Agent”, transforming static neural network into dynamic cognitive subject capable of memory retrieval, tool utilization, and environmental interaction. Furthermore, the introduction of inter-agent communication has given rise to the more advanced concept of Multi-agent System (MAS), making the “hyper LLM ecosystem” become even more intricate, interactive and intelligent (Wang et al., 2025a; Zhang et al., 2025a; Yue et al., 2025; Zhang et al., 2025b; Guo et al., 2024; Xie et al., 2024).
大型语言模型（LLMs）的出现推动了人工智能系统（Zhao 等人，2023；Minaee 等人，2024；Chang 等人，2024；Wang 等人，2024b）的范式转变。将 LLMs 作为核心，结合额外模块（例如记忆（Zhang 等人，2024a）、工具（Masterman 等人，2024；Qin 等人，2024）和环境（Yang 等人，2024c）作为扩展）产生了“基于 LLM 的智能体”的概念，将静态神经网络转化为能够进行记忆检索、工具利用和环境交互的动态认知主体。此外，智能体间通信的引入催生了更高级的概念——多智能体系统（MAS），使得“超 LLM 生态系统”变得更加复杂、交互和智能（Wang 等人，2025a；Zhang 等人，2025a；Yue 等人，2025；Zhang 等人，2025b；Guo 等人，2024；Xie 等人，2024）。

Extensive academic research and industry practices have validated this performance hierarchy: MAS ¿ Single Agent ¿ LLM (Li et al., 2023a; Wu et al., 2023; Talebirad and Nadiri, 2023; Chan et al., 2023). However, the incorporation of additional modules is a double-edged sword, which has raised new concerns regarding trustworthiness across multiple dimensions, including safety, privacy, fairness, and truthfulness (He et al., 2024; Wang et al., 2024c; Xi et al., 2025). From a risk perspective, introducing new modules expands the system’s attack surface, potentially leading to unforeseen vulnerabilities (Yuan et al., 2024a; Tang et al., 2024; Gan et al., 2024). On the other hand, this integration poses new challenges to existing defense mechanisms and trustworthiness evaluations, necessitating the expansion and upgrade of previous research that focuses solely on the trustworthiness of LLMs or a single agent (Zhang et al., 2024b; Hua et al., 2024).
广泛的学术研究和行业实践已经验证了这种性能层级：MAS ¿ 单一代理 ¿ LLM（Li 等人，2023a；Wu 等人，2023；Talebirad 和 Nadiri，2023；Chan 等人，2023）。然而，增加额外模块是一把双刃剑，这在不同维度上引发了关于可信性的新担忧，包括安全性、隐私性、公平性和真实性（He 等人，2024；Wang 等人，2024c；Xi 等人，2025）。从风险角度来看，引入新模块扩大了系统的攻击面，可能导致未预见到的漏洞（Yuan 等人，2024a；Tang 等人，2024；Gan 等人，2024）。另一方面，这种集成给现有的防御机制和可信性评估提出了新的挑战，需要扩展和升级之前仅关注 LLM 或单一代理可信性的研究（Zhang 等人，2024b；Hua 等人，2024）。

Previous survey efforts have delved deeply into the realm of trustworthy LLMs. Liu et al. (Liu et al., 2023b) dissect trustworthiness into seven major categories, focusing specially on establishing standards and guidelines for LLM alignment. Similarly, Huang et al. (Huang et al., 2024) interpret trustworthiness from 6 perspectives but concentrate on creating benchmarks to evaluate trustworthiness. However, these studies are only partially effective in agent scenarios, highlighting an urgent need to address new trustworthiness issues arising from the introduction of additional modules. Other specialized surveys on trustworthiness in agents primarily focus on the sub-aspects like security and privacy and largely overlap with the content of Trustworthy LLMs (Wang et al., 2024c, c; Gan et al., 2024). In fact, some works merely address the new issues arising from LLMs serving as the “brain” module of agents (He et al., 2024; Li et al., 2024b), while overlooking the unexplored challenges introduced by other additional modules. 以往的调研工作已深入探讨了可信 LLM 的领域。刘等人（Liu et al, 2023b）将可信性分解为七个主要类别，特别关注建立 LLM 对齐的标准和指南。类似地，黄等人（Huang et al, 2024）从六个视角解读可信性，但专注于创建基准来评估可信性。然而，这些研究在智能体场景中仅部分有效，凸显了应对新增模块引入的新可信性问题的迫切需求。其他关于智能体可信性的专门调研主要关注安全性和隐私等子方面，且与《可信 LLM》（Wang et al, 2024c, c; Gan et al, 2024）的内容大量重叠。事实上，一些研究仅处理 LLM 作为智能体“大脑”模块时出现的新问题（He et al, 2024; Li et al, 2024b），而忽略了其他新增模块引入的未解决挑战。为突出我们的创新点，我们在表 1 中将 TrustAgent 与其他调研进行了比较。

TrustAgent rigorously categorizes trustworthiness issues based on the internal and external components of agents, specifically divided into intrinsic and extrinsic aspects. The former includes the trustworthiness of the brain, memory, and tools, while the latter encompasses the parts related to users, other agents, and the environment. (II) Technical. TrustAgent focuses on the implementation of trustworthy agents, providing a comprehensive summary and outlook on the relevant technology stack from three aspects: attack, defense, and evaluation (a comprehensive picture in Appendix B). (III) Multi-dimensional. 2. Intrinsic Trustworthiness
2. 内在可信性

In this section, we focus on the trustworthiness of the internal modules of agent systems. In our definition, the agent system is an independent entity with human-like cognition, composed of brain (Sec. 2.1) with memory (Sec. 2.2), and behavior in the tool form (Sec. 2.3). Due to the different functions and natures of these modules, the resulting trustworthiness issues vary. To provide in-depth analysis and instructive insight, we first introduce the role and functions of each module in the agent system and then summarize technical paradigms for attack, defense, and evaluation methods.
在本节中，我们重点关注智能体系统内部模块的可信性。根据我们的定义，智能体系统是一个具有类人认知的独立实体，由大脑（第 2.1 节）构成，具备记忆（第 2.2 节），并以工具形式表现行为（第 2.3 节）。由于这些模块具有不同的功能和特性，由此产生的可信性问题也各不相同。为了进行深入分析和提供有指导意义的见解，我们首先介绍智能体系统中每个模块的作用和功能，然后总结攻击、防御和评估方法的技术范式。

2.1. Trustworthy LLM as Brain
2.1 可信 LLM 作为大脑

LLM agents consist of a central LLM “brain” module (Wang et al., 2023). The brain serves as the core reasoning and decision-making center, integrating inputs from various auxiliary modules to guide the agent towards its goal. Though the powerful brain connected with other modules enhances the agent’s ability, this integration expands the potential attack surface, making the system more vulnerable to trustworthiness threats. In this section, we center on brain-related attacks, defenses, and evaluations, with illustration in Figure 2.
LLM 代理由一个中央 LLM“大脑”模块（Wang 等人，2023 年）组成。大脑作为核心推理和决策中心，整合来自各种辅助模块的输入，引导代理实现其目标。虽然与其他模块相连的大脑增强了代理的能力，但这种集成扩大了潜在的攻击面，使系统更容易受到可信性威胁。在本节中，我们重点关注与大脑相关的攻击、防御和评估，如图 2 所示。

2.1.1. Attacks.  2.1.1. 攻击

Unlike single-LLM systems, the brain module in agent or MAS experiences more frequent and complex information dynamics. The textual and visual inputs from the internal and external provide attackers with more interfaces and methods to compromise the trustworthiness of the core brain module. We do not discuss Misalignment attacks (e.g., fine-tuning an LLM to break its alignment) on agents here, as attackers typically lack access to modify the internal parameters of the agents. 与单一 LLM 系统不同，智能体或 MAS 中的大脑模块经历着更频繁和复杂的信息动态。内部和外部提供的文本和视觉输入为攻击者提供了更多接口和方法来破坏核心大脑模块的可信度。我们在此不讨论针对智能体的 Misalignment 攻击（例如，微调 LLM 以破坏其一致性），因为攻击者通常无法访问修改智能体内部参数。根据操纵机制（如图 2 所示），我们将攻击分为三种范式：

Jailbreak attempts to bypass the aligned trustworthy mechanisms within the agent brain via human-designed or optimized adversarial prompts (Kumar et al., 2023). Early methods, such as GCG and its variants (Zou et al., 2023; Jia et al., 2024; Wang et al., 2024d), optimize adversarial suffixes to manipulate responses, coercing a “Sure” reply to malicious queries. MRJ-Agent (Wang et al., 2024a) designs a single attack agent to automatically generate covert jailbreak prompts, enhanced by information from multi-round dialogue. PrivAgent (Nie et al., 2024) trains a single LLM as an attack agent through reinforcement learning to generate jailbreak prompts that can induce the target model to leak system prompts or training data. Furthermore, by leveraging multi-agent collaboration, Evil Geniuses (Tian et al., 2023) and PANDORA (Chen et al., 2024c) construct MAS as attack optimizers to perform role-specific jailbreak on the brain of target agents or MAS. They reinforced jailbreak effectiveness through Red-Blue exercises and MAS multi-step reasoning, respectively. Additionally, interactions between different brains give rise to viral jailbreak. Specifically, AgentSmith (Gu et al., 2024) and Tan et al. (Tan et al., 2024) optimize self-replicating images to attack the brain of a single agent and discover an exponential spread of infectious jailbreak at MAS-level.
越狱尝试通过人类设计或优化的对抗性提示（Kumar 等人，2023）绕过代理大脑中的对齐可信机制。早期方法，如 GCG 及其变体（Zou 等人，2023；Jia 等人，2024；Wang 等人，2024d），优化对抗性后缀以操纵响应，迫使对恶意查询给出“确定”的回复。MRJ-Agent（Wang 等人，2024a）设计了一个单一的攻击代理来自动生成隐蔽的越狱提示，并通过多轮对话的信息进行增强。PrivAgent（Nie 等人，2024）通过强化学习训练一个单一的 LLM 作为攻击代理，以生成越狱提示，这些提示可以诱导目标模型泄露系统提示或训练数据。此外，通过利用多代理协作，Evil Geniuses（Tian 等人，2023）和 PANDORA（Chen 等人，2024c）构建 MAS 作为攻击优化器，对目标代理或 MAS 的大脑执行特定角色的越狱。它们分别通过红蓝演练和 MAS 多步推理强化了越狱的有效性。此外，不同大脑之间的交互导致了病毒式越狱。 具体来说，AgentSmith（Gu 等人，2024 年）和 Tan 等人（Tan 等人）。2024) 优化自我复制图像以攻击单个代理的头脑，并在 MAS 级别发现传染性越狱的指数级传播。

Prompt Injection embeds malicious prompts to override the original instructions to the agent brain, thereby manipulating its output or behavior (Liu et al., 2023a; Perez and Ribeiro, 2022). Early research manually crafts instructions and injects them into data which may be retrieved during inference, potentially causing the agent brain to deviate from intended tasks (Greshake et al., 2023). Later studies automate this process via gradient-based or optimization-based methods (Liu et al., 2024b; Shi et al., 2024). Beyond injecting prompts in text modality, Bagdasaryan et al. (Bagdasaryan et al., 2023) explore that multi-modal adversarial perturbations embedded in images or audio can manipulate the agent’s brain to follow the attacker’s instructions. The interaction between the agent’s brain and other modules also provides more surfaces for prompt injection. Zhang et al. (Zhang et al., 2024i) exploit various interactions across multiple surfaces to mislead the agent’s brain into performing repetitive or irrelevant actions, inducing logical errors and causing malfunctions.
提示注入将恶意提示嵌入到代理大脑的原始指令中，从而操纵其输出或行为（Liu 等人，2023a；Perez 和 Ribeiro，2022）。早期研究通过手动编写指令并将其注入到推理过程中可能检索到的数据中，可能导致代理大脑偏离预期任务（Greshake 等人，2023）。后期研究通过基于梯度或基于优化的方法自动化这一过程（Liu 等人，2024b；Shi 等人，2024）。除了在文本模态中注入提示，Bagdasaryan 等人（Bagdasaryan 等人，2023）探索了嵌入到图像或音频中的多模态对抗性扰动可以操纵代理大脑以遵循攻击者的指令。代理大脑与其他模块之间的交互也提供了更多提示注入的表面。Zhang 等人（Zhang 等人，2024i）利用跨多个表面的各种交互来误导代理大脑执行重复或不相关的操作，诱导逻辑错误并导致功能故障。

Backdoor attacks involve the insertion of malicious triggers during training, which will be retrieved during inference, forcing predefined generation in the agent’s brain for specific inputs (Zhou et al., 2025c). Yang et al. (Yang et al., 2024a) categorize agent backdoor attacks into two main types: The first type manipulates the final output distribution, by inserting triggers in different phases (query, observation), while the second one inserts triggers in agent’s thinking process to introduce malicious intermediate reasoning without altering the final output. The first type is exemplified by DemonAgent (Zhu et al., 2025), which fragments the original backdoor into multiple sub-backdoor segments and employs dynamic encryption, making the backdoor continuously change during execution. BLAST (Yu et al., 2025) follows the second type and enables “infectious backdoor”, implanting a backdoor in a single agent and using it to influence other agents’ reasoning.
后门攻击涉及在训练过程中插入恶意触发器，这些触发器将在推理时被检索，从而迫使代理的大脑针对特定输入生成预定义的内容（Zhou 等人，2025c）。Yang 等人（Yang 等人，2024a）将代理后门攻击分为两种主要类型：第一种类型通过在不同阶段（查询、观察）插入触发器来操纵最终输出分布，而第二种类型则在代理的思考过程中插入触发器，以引入恶意中间推理而不改变最终输出。第一种类型以 DemonAgent（Zhu 等人，2025）为例，该攻击将原始后门分割成多个子后门片段，并采用动态加密，使后门在执行过程中不断变化。BLAST（Yu 等人，2025）遵循第二种类型，实现“感染性后门”，通过在单个代理中植入后门来影响其他代理的推理。

2.1.2. Defenses.  2.1.2. 防御措施。

The agent brain is not only more complex than a single LLM in terms of interactions, but also faces more types of attacks. Therefore, designing corresponding defenses to ensure its trustworthiness across all dimensions deserves holistic study. Drawing on the defense mechanisms’ operational scopes, we organize defense approaches into three paradigms:
智能体的大脑在交互方面不仅比单个 LLM 更为复杂，而且面临着更多类型的攻击。因此，设计相应的防御措施以确保其在各个维度上的可信性，值得进行整体性研究。基于防御机制的操作范围，我们将防御方法分为三种范式：

Alignment ensures that LLM agents operate in accordance with human values and ethical principles through fine-tuning, updating reward functions, etc. For instance, some studies embed human values directly into agents through intrinsic reward functions (Tennant et al., 2024) or neuro-symbolic rule-learning frameworks (Frisch and Giulianelli, 2024), validated in open-world environments like Minecraft. Another work fine-tunes agents with the data generated in social simulation scenarios (Pang et al., 2024) to align agents with nuanced human behaviors and beliefs. Additionally, iterative alignment through user feedback and belief networks is leveraged to personalize agent behavior and reduce reliance on expert oversight, such as using historical edits (Gao et al., 2024) or empirically derived human belief structures (Zhang et al., 2024d).
对齐确保 LLM 智能体通过微调、更新奖励函数等方式，按照人类价值观和伦理原则运行。例如，一些研究通过内在奖励函数（Tennant 等人，2024 年）或神经符号规则学习框架（Frisch 和 Giulianelli，2024 年）将人类价值观直接嵌入智能体，并在 Minecraft 等开放世界环境中进行了验证。另一项工作通过使用社交模拟场景中生成的数据来微调智能体（Pang 等人，2024 年），以使智能体与微妙的人类行为和信念保持一致。此外，利用用户反馈和信念网络进行迭代对齐，以个性化智能体行为并减少对专家监督的依赖，例如使用历史编辑（Gao 等人，2024 年）或经验推导出的人类信念结构（Zhang 等人，2024d）。

Single-model Filter uses an external model to prevent attacks through input and output monitoring on the agent brain. Ayub et al. (Ayub and Majumdar, 2024) employ traditional embedding classifiers to detect prompt injection by analyzing input semantic features, while Kwon et al. (Kwon et al., 2024) use a small model for harmful query detection. As for LLM filter, StruQ (Chen et al., 2024b) mitigates prompt injection by converting inputs into structured queries with a specially trained LLM. ShieldLM (Zhang et al., 2024h) fine-tunes an LLM safety detector that aligns with safety standards. Additionally, agent serves as an even more powerful filter. For example, GuardAgent (Xiang et al., 2024b) and AgentGuard (Chen and Cong, 2025) utilize a guardrail agent to protect target agents via safety constraint generation, action checking, and tool-use validation.
单模型过滤器使用外部模型来防止通过输入和输出监控对代理大脑的攻击。Ayub 等人（Ayub 和 Majumdar，2024）采用传统嵌入分类器通过分析输入语义特征来检测提示注入，而 Kwon 等人（Kwon 等人，2024）使用小型模型进行有害查询检测。至于 LLM 过滤器，StruQ（Chen 等人，2024b）通过将输入转换为结构化查询来减轻提示注入，该查询由专门训练的 LLM 生成。ShieldLM（Zhang 等人，2024h）微调一个与安全标准一致的 LLM 安全检测器。此外，代理本身可以作为一个更强大的过滤器。例如，GuardAgent（Xiang 等人，2024b）和 AgentGuard（Chen 和 Cong，2025）利用一个防护代理通过生成安全约束、动作检查和工具使用验证来保护目标代理。

Multi-agent Shield leverages collaborative MAS to enhance the trustworthiness of the target agent’s brain. The main difference of these guard MAS lies in their communication pattern. Specifically, one pattern is multi-agent debate, where agents critique the reasoning processes of other components of the brain over multiple rounds to reach a consensus final answer (Du et al., 2023). Apart from debating, Kwartler et al. (Kwartler et al., 2024) use a reviewing agent to provide feedback and correct the target agent’s generation. AutoDefense (Zeng et al., 2024a) assigns different roles to each agent, allowing smaller models to collaborate and protect the brain of the targeted agent from jailbreak attacks.
多智能体盾牌利用协作多智能体系统来增强目标智能体大脑的可信度。这些守卫智能体系统的主要区别在于它们的通信模式。具体来说，一种模式是多智能体辩论，智能体在多轮中批评大脑其他组件的推理过程，以达成共识的最终答案（Du 等人，2023）。除了辩论之外，Kwartler 等人（Kwartler 等人，2024）使用一个审查智能体来提供反馈并纠正目标智能体的生成。AutoDefense（Zeng 等人，2024a）为每个智能体分配不同的角色，允许较小的模型协作，并保护目标智能体的大脑免受越狱攻击。

2.1.3. Evaluation.  2.1.3. 评估

Different from static LLMs, agents continuously adapt to their environment, making their behavior highly context-dependent and variable, which also complicates the evaluation. Current evaluation methodologies for agent brain trustworthiness can be categorized along two dimensions:
与静态 LLMs 不同，智能体会持续适应其环境，使其行为高度依赖于上下文且具有可变性，这也使得评估变得更加复杂。当前用于评估智能体可信度的方法可以沿两个维度进行分类：

Focused Assessment assesses the trustworthiness of agent brain when facing certain types of attacks or in specific domains. InjecAgent (Zhan et al., 2024) and AgentDojo (Debenedetti et al., 2025) benchmark the brain’s vulnerability to Indirect Prompt Injection, while DemonAgent (Zhu et al., 2025) introduces AgentBackdoorEval for Backdoor scenarios. RedAgent (Xu et al., 2024) addresses context-aware red teaming, enabling test cases of context-sensitive Jailbreak. In addition to evaluating brain trustworthiness in specific domains, RiskAwareBench (Zhu et al., 2024) introduces an automated framework for assessing physical risk awareness in LLM-based embodied agents. RedCode (Guo et al., 2025) provides a benchmark for evaluating the code agents under risky code execution and generation with challenging test cases.
聚焦评估评估智能体在面对特定类型攻击或在特定领域中的可信度。InjecAgent（Zhan 等人，2024 年）和 AgentDojo（Debenedetti 等人，2025 年）基准测试了智能体对间接提示注入的脆弱性，而 DemonAgent（Zhu 等人，2025 年）引入了 AgentBackdoorEval 用于后门场景。RedAgent（Xu 等人，2024 年）处理了上下文感知的红队测试，支持上下文敏感的 Jailbreak 测试用例。除了在特定领域评估智能体可信度外，RiskAwareBench（Zhu 等人，2024 年）引入了一个用于评估基于 LLM 的具身智能体物理风险意识的自动化框架。RedCode（Guo 等人，2025 年）提供了一个基准，用于在具有挑战性测试用例的 risky 代码执行和生成下评估代码智能体。

General Benchmark literally involves systematic evaluations to test the agent brain in diverse domains and from multiple dimensions. S-Eval (Yuan et al., 2024b) automates multiple dimensional and open-ended safety evaluation for LLMs with four hierarchical levels consisting of eight risk dimensions. While BELLS (Dorn et al., 2024) evaluates LLM Safeguard with a structured collection of tests, including established failure tests, emerging failure tests, and next-gen architecture tests. Meanwhile, recent efforts have introduced comprehensive benchmarks for LLM agents. Agent-SafetyBench (Zhang et al., 2024c) and Agent Security Bench (Zhang et al., 2024f) are benchmarks that evaluate LLM agents with multiple scenarios and different types of attack/defense methods. AgentHarm (Andriushchenko et al., 2024) contains 110 explicitly malicious agent tasks to evaluate LLM agent misuse, covering multiple harmful categories, including fraud, cybercrime, and harassment. RJudge (Yuan et al., 2024a) benchmarks the trustworthiness of multi-turn interactions based on agent interaction records, with 27 risk scenarios across multiple application categories and risk types.
通用基准测试字面意义上涉及系统性的评估，用于测试代理在不同领域和多维度上的能力。S-Eval（Yuan 等人，2024b）通过四个层次、包含八个风险维度的四级结构，自动化地评估 LLMs 的多维度开放式安全。而 BELLS（Dorn 等人，2024）通过一系列结构化测试评估 LLM 安全防护，包括既定失败测试、新兴失败测试和下一代架构测试。与此同时，近期研究引入了针对 LLM 代理的全面基准测试。Agent-SafetyBench（Zhang 等人，2024c）和 Agent Security Bench（Zhang 等人，2024f）是评估 LLM 代理的基准测试，它们通过多种场景和不同类型的攻击/防御方法进行评估。AgentHarm（Andriushchenko 等人，2024）包含 110 个明确恶意的代理任务，用于评估 LLM 代理的滥用情况，涵盖多种有害类别，包括欺诈、网络犯罪和骚扰。RJudge（Yuan 等人，2024a）基于代理交互记录，对多轮交互的可信度进行基准测试，包含多个应用类别和风险类型中的 27 种风险场景。

2.1.4. Insight.  2.1.4. 见解。

Current collaborative attacks in MAS can spread from a single compromised agent’s brain to multiple agents’ brain modules (Gu et al., 2024; Yu et al., 2025). Therefore, developing collaborative security mechanisms that enable agents to monitor and validate each other’s actions is crucial. For example, a distributed consensus protocol can be implemented, where agents collectively verify and agree on critical decisions before execution. Additionally, current evaluations primarily rely on static datasets. However, given the agent brain module’s frequently dynamic interactions with external information, such evaluations are clearly insufficient. To address this, dynamic evaluation mechanisms should be implemented to better simulate real-world scenarios. For example, a continuous learning-based evaluation framework can be designed, where agents are tested in real-time environments with evolving data streams.
当前在多智能体系统（MAS）中的协同攻击可以从一个被攻陷的智能体的“大脑”传播到多个智能体的“大脑”模块（Gu 等人，2024；Yu 等人，2025）。因此，开发协同安全机制，使智能体能够相互监控和验证彼此的行动至关重要。例如，可以实施分布式共识协议，智能体在执行前共同验证并达成对关键决策的共识。此外，当前的评估主要依赖于静态数据集。然而，鉴于智能体“大脑”模块与外部信息频繁的动态交互，此类评估显然是不充分的。为解决这一问题，应实施动态评估机制以更好地模拟现实场景。例如，可以设计基于持续学习的评估框架，在实时环境中对智能体进行测试，并使用不断变化的数据流。

2.2. Trustworthy Memory in Retrieval
2.2 可信记忆在检索中

Memory mechanisms in agent systems are crucial for interaction with the environment and user, categorized into long-term and short-term memory. Long-term memory is often associated with RAG, utilizing vector database to store real-world data for generation tasks, while short-term memory holds real-time interaction history, such as dialogue context or task logs. These mechanisms enhance agent capabilities but also introduce trustworthiness risks. In this section, we analyze the trustworthiness challenges posed by memory, focusing on attacks, defenses, and evaluation strategies. The overall framework is shown in Figure 3.
智能体系统中的记忆机制对于与环境及用户的交互至关重要，分为长期记忆和短期记忆。长期记忆通常与 RAG 相关联，利用向量数据库存储真实世界数据以支持生成任务，而短期记忆则存储实时交互历史，例如对话上下文或任务日志。这些机制增强了智能体的能力，但也引入了可信性风险。在本节中，我们分析了记忆带来的可信性挑战，重点关注攻击、防御和评估策略。整体框架如图 3 所示。

2.2.1. Attack.  2.2.1.攻击。

We categorize attacks related to memory into three types: Memory Poisoning, Privacy Leakage, and Memory Misuse.
我们将与内存相关的攻击分为三种类型：内存中毒、隐私泄露和内存滥用。

Memory Poisoning refers to attackers injecting malicious data into the long-term memory (Xiang et al., 2024a; Chen et al., 2025b; Zou et al., 2024; Zhong et al., 2023; Zhang et al., 2024b; Gu et al., 2024), which will be retrieved and then mislead the agent system to generate incorrect outputs, undermining its truthfulness. This attack’s danger stems from its stealthiness: once malicious data is injected into memory, it may continuously influence the agent until detected and removed. Specifically, we summarize the following attack paradigms: ❶ Memory Injection. PoisonedRAG (Zou et al., 2024) optimizes malicious text through retrieval and generation conditions, enabling this text to be easily retrieved, while GARAG (Cho et al., 2024) continuously uses genetic algorithms to optimize adversarial examples for injection. Additionally, RobustRag (Xiang et al., 2024a) and Zhong et al. (Zhong et al., 2023) demonstrate that injecting even a small amount of malicious information into vector database can successfully attack agents with high probability. ❷ Backdoor. AgentPoison (Chen et al., 2025b) optimizes backdoor triggers and attaching them to queries, enhancing retrieval possibility of malicious samples.
记忆中毒是指攻击者将恶意数据注入长期记忆（Xiang et al, 2024a; Chen et al, 2025b; Zou et al, 2024; Zhong et al, 2023; Zhang et al, 2024b; Gu et al, 2024），这些数据随后会被检索并误导代理系统生成错误输出，从而削弱其真实性。这种攻击的危险性源于其隐蔽性：一旦恶意数据被注入记忆，它可能会持续影响代理，直到被检测和移除。具体来说，我们总结了以下攻击范式：❶ 记忆注入。PoisonedRAG (Zou et al, 2024) 通过检索和生成条件优化恶意文本，使该文本容易被检索，而 GARAG (Cho et al, 2024) 则持续使用遗传算法优化用于注入的对抗性样本。此外，RobustRag (Xiang et al, 2024a) 和 Zhong et al. (Zhong et al, 2023) 表明，即使向向量数据库注入少量恶意信息，也有很大概率成功攻击代理。❷ 后门。 AgentPoison (陈等人, 2025b) 优化后门触发器并将其附加到查询中，增强了恶意样本的检索可能性。

Privacy Leakage refers to attackers leveraging the connection between agent and its long-term memory to steal the stored private data (Li et al., 2025; Zeng et al., 2024b; Anderson et al., 2024). Beyond data theft, it enables criminal activities like phishing and identity trafficking, dramatically increasing privacy risks. Technically, we outline the following attack methods: ❶ Jailbreak. RAG-Thief (Jiang et al., 2024) continuously optimizes anchor queries and adversarial commands, forming jailbreak prompts to conduct data stealing. RAG-MIA (Anderson et al., 2024) designs specific jailbreak templates to extract private data from vector database. ❷ Embedding Inversion directly restores the original data from its embedding. Specifically, Morris et al. (Morris et al., 2023) iteratively optimize the text hypothesis to bring its embedding closer to the target embedding, while Li et al.(Li et al., 2023b) reconstruct the data via a generative decoder.
隐私泄露是指攻击者利用智能体与其长期记忆之间的联系来窃取存储的私有数据 (李等人, 2025; 曾等人, 2024b; 安德森等人, 2024)。除了数据盗窃，它还支持钓鱼和身份贩卖等犯罪活动，显著增加了隐私风险。技术上，我们概述以下攻击方法：❶ 越狱。RAG-Thief (蒋等人, 2024) 持续优化锚点查询和对抗性命令，形成越狱提示以进行数据盗窃。RAG-MIA (安德森等人, 2024) 设计特定的越狱模板以从向量数据库中提取私有数据。❷ 嵌入反转直接从其嵌入中恢复原始数据。具体而言，Morris 等人 (Morris 等人, 2023) 迭代优化文本假设，使其嵌入更接近目标嵌入，而李等人 (李等人, 2023b) 通过生成解码器重建数据。

Memory Misuse crafts specific query sequences to gradually bypass intrinsic safety alignment via multi-turn interaction, leveraging the storage property of agent short-term memory (Russinovich et al., 2024; Li et al., 2024a; Cheng et al., 2024a; Priyanshu and Vijay, 2024; Agarwal et al., 2024; Tong et al., 2024). Therefore, the safety of an agent system is reduced. From a technical perspective, we outline the following two attack methodologies: ❶ Jailbreak. Agarwal et al. (Agarwal et al., 2024) send an initial query along with an attack prompt in the first round, followed by a flattering challenge and a reiteration of the attack prompt in the second round, while Russinovich et al. (Russinovich et al., 2024) begin with harmless questions related to the target task and gradually steer harmful generation. ❷ Backdoor. Tong et al.(Tong et al., 2024) utilize multi-turn dialogue memory to conceal backdoor triggers, activating malicious responses only when all triggers appear, thus stealthily achieving the attack. As a core component of agents, defense technologies against short-term memory misuse are even more important.
内存滥用通过设计特定的查询序列，在多轮交互中逐步绕过内在安全对齐，利用智能体短期记忆的存储特性（Russinovich 等人，2024；Li 等人，2024a；Cheng 等人，2024a；Priyanshu 和 Vijay，2024；Agarwal 等人，2024；Tong 等人，2024），因此降低了智能体系统的安全性。从技术角度来看，我们概述以下两种攻击方法：❶ 越狱。Agarwal 等人（Agarwal 等人，2024）在第一轮发送初始查询并附带攻击提示，然后在第二轮进行奉承的挑战并重申攻击提示，而 Russinovich 等人（Russinovich 等人，2024）从与目标任务相关的无害问题开始，逐渐引导有害生成。❷ 后门。Tong 等人（Tong 等人，2024）利用多轮对话记忆来隐藏后门触发器，仅在所有触发器出现时激活恶意响应，从而隐蔽地实现攻击。作为智能体的核心组件，针对短期记忆滥用的防御技术更为重要。

2.2.2. Defense.  2.2.2. 防御

To address the aforementioned memory attacks, several corresponding defense strategies have been proposed (Mao et al., 2025; Zhou et al., 2025a; Xian et al., 2024; Agarwal et al., 2024; Anderson et al., 2024), which can be classified into the following three types:
为应对上述内存攻击，已提出多种相应的防御策略（Mao et al, 2025; Zhou et al, 2025a; Xian et al, 2024; Agarwal et al, 2024; Anderson et al, 2024），这些策略可分为以下三种类型：

Detection typically involves identifying and removing harmful portions of text retrieved from long-term memory. TrustRAG (Zhou et al., 2025a) employs K-means clustering on the retrieved embeddings to distinguish between clean and potentially malicious documents based on the embedding distribution. Xian et al. (Xian et al., 2024) calculate the Mahalanobis distance between query and documents (poisoned or clean) retrieved from long-term memory and set a threshold to filter out malicious documents. Besides traditional methods for detection, Agarwal et al. (Agarwal et al., 2024) utilize LLMs to detect and filter out parts of the prompt that access private data. Additionally, ASB (Zhang et al., 2024b) uses both perplexity-based and LLM-based detection to identify whether the text retrieved from memory is compromised.
检测通常涉及识别并移除从长期记忆中检索到的文本的有害部分。TrustRAG (Zhou et al, 2025a) 对检索到的嵌入应用 K-means 聚类，根据嵌入分布区分干净文档和潜在恶意文档。Xian 等人 (Xian et al, 2024) 计算查询与从长期记忆中检索到的文档（中毒或干净）之间的马氏距离，并设置阈值以过滤掉恶意文档。除了传统的检测方法，Agarwal 等人 (Agarwal et al, 2024) 利用 LLMs 检测并过滤掉访问私人数据的提示部分。此外，ASB (Zhang et al, 2024b) 结合基于困惑度和基于 LLM 的检测方法，识别从记忆中检索到的文本是否被篡改。

Prompt Modification refers to altering the query sent to the agent to make it safer. Anderson et al. (Anderson et al., 2024) embed user queries into a designed prompt template, which enables the LLM to ignore direct requests for querying the content of vector database. Agarwal et al. (Agarwal et al., 2024) propose various modification strategies, such as adding security instructions to the original prompt, explicitly requiring the agent not to disclose data; or directly utilize the LLM to rewrite the query and filter out potentially privacy-leaking parts.
提示修改是指改变发送给代理的查询，使其更安全。Anderson 等人（Anderson 等人，2024）将用户查询嵌入到设计的提示模板中，这使 LLM 能够忽略直接请求查询向量数据库的内容。Agarwal 等人（Agarwal 等人，2024）提出了各种修改策略，例如在原始提示中添加安全指令，明确要求代理不要泄露数据；或者直接利用 LLM 重写查询并过滤掉可能泄露隐私的部分。

Output Intervention refers to intervening in the agent’s output before it generates the final response to prevent it from producing incorrect or unsafe replies. RobustRAG (Xiang et al., 2024a) employs an Isolate-then-Aggregate approach that independently generates responses for each retrieved passage and aggregates them via keyword, effectively reduce ASR as malicious passage are in the minority. Chen et al. (Chen et al., 2023) demonstrate that safety filter can prevent the generation of unsafe tokens in multi-turn dialogues when agents use memory.
输出干预是指在代理生成最终响应之前对其输出进行干预，以防止其产生不正确或不安全的回复。RobustRAG（Xiang 等人，2024a）采用隔离后聚合的方法，独立为每个检索到的段落生成响应，并通过关键词进行聚合，有效减少 ASR，因为恶意段落是少数。Chen 等人（Chen 等人，2023）表明，当代理使用内存时，安全过滤器可以防止在多轮对话中生成不安全的标记。

2.2.3. Evaluation.  2.2.3. 评估。

Since there is currently no systematic and reliable evaluation of memory, we summarize some commonly used metrics in research. For memory poisoning and privacy leakage attacks, which rely on retrieving relevant data, evaluation metrics extend beyond ASR to include target text retrieval effectiveness. PoisonedRAG (Zou et al., 2024) uses precision, recall, and F1-score to assess the retrieval effectiveness of malicious text. AgentPoison (Chen et al., 2025b) employs ASR for retrieval (ASR-r) to measure malicious trigger effectiveness. Zeng et al. (Zeng et al., 2024b) evaluate attacks by quantifying retrieved private data, and RAG-Thief (Jiang et al., 2024) uses chunk recovery rate (CRR) and semantic similarity (SS) to gauge data theft from vector database.
由于目前还没有针对记忆的系统性和可靠性评估，我们总结了研究中常用的一些指标。对于依赖检索相关数据的内存中毒和隐私泄露攻击，评估指标已超出语音识别（ASR）范围，还包括目标文本检索的有效性。PoisonedRAG（Zou 等人，2024）使用精确率、召回率和 F1 分数来评估恶意文本的检索有效性。AgentPoison（Chen 等人，2025b）采用语音识别检索（ASR-r）来衡量恶意触发效果。Zeng 等人（Zeng 等人，2024b）通过量化检索到的私有数据来评估攻击，而 RAG-Thief（Jiang 等人，2024）则使用块恢复率（CRR）和语义相似度（SS）来衡量从向量数据库中窃取数据的效果。

2.2.4. Insight.  2.2.4. 见解。

Current memory-centric attack methods often lack generalization across tasks. Memory poisoning relies on task-specific malicious samples to ensure retrieval by relevant task queries. Memory misuse, on the other hand, heavily depends on dialogue context, requiring tailored designs for various scenarios. From the defense perspective, for memory poisoning defense, future defenses should focus on the vector database end to prevent the injection of toxic samples, thereby reducing the defense time during agent responses. For privacy leakage defense, privacy protection mechanisms such as query rewriting or fine-tuning LLMs must be implemented in applications where privacy breaches are possible. For memory misuse defense, developing a multi-round adversarial dialogue training paradigm for safety alignment is essential to enhance robustness of agent. For the evaluation, as there is currently no systematic and reliable benchmarks, we recommend establishing benchmarks for the paradigms of attacks and defenses mentioned above.
当前以内存为中心的攻击方法往往缺乏跨任务泛化能力。内存中毒依赖于特定任务的恶意样本以确保被相关任务查询检索。而内存滥用则严重依赖于对话上下文，需要对各种场景进行定制化设计。从防御角度来看，针对内存中毒防御，未来的防御措施应聚焦于向量数据库端，以防止有毒样本的注入，从而减少代理响应过程中的防御时间。对于隐私泄露防御，必须在可能发生隐私泄露的应用中实施查询重写或微调 LLMs 等隐私保护机制。对于内存滥用防御，开发用于安全对齐的多轮对抗性对话训练范式对于增强代理的鲁棒性至关重要。在评估方面，由于目前尚无系统且可靠的基准测试，我们建议为上述提到的攻击和防御范式建立基准测试。

2.3. Trustworthy Tool for Action
2.3 可信行动工具

In the components of the agent or MAS, the tool module serves as the medium for interaction between the system and the external world (Liu et al., 2024a). It can either gather information from outside (e.g., using a search engine for information retrieval (Chowdhury and Chowdhury, 2024)) or reflect the internally decided actions back to the external environment (e.g., mapping UI operations to the interface (Zhang et al., 2024g)). The typical forms of tools include API functions, sensors, embodied robots, etc (Yuan et al., 2024c). However, different forms of tools exhibit varied properties and risks, making trustworthiness problems complicated and challenging. Simultaneously, although tools are also used in the context of LLMs (Shen, 2024; Zhuang et al., 2023), the closer integration with agents imposes stricter challenges on their trustworthiness, especially since some agents’ actions can affect the real world. Risks associated with agent tools may lead to more severe negative impacts compared to LLMs (Zhang et al., 2024b). In this section, we present agent tool trustworthiness from the attack, defense, and evaluation perspectives, illustrated in Figure 4.
在智能体或多智能体系统的组件中，工具模块作为系统与外部世界交互的媒介（刘等，2024a）。它可以从外部收集信息（例如，使用搜索引擎进行信息检索（乔杜里和乔杜里，2024）），或者将内部决定的行动反映回外部环境（例如，将 UI 操作映射到接口（张等，2024g））。工具的典型形式包括 API 函数、传感器、具身机器人等（袁等，2024c）。然而，不同形式的工具表现出不同的属性和风险，使得可信性问题变得复杂且具有挑战性。同时，尽管工具也在 LLMs 的上下文中使用（沈，2024；庄等，2023），但与智能体的更紧密集成对其可信性提出了更严格的挑战，特别是由于某些智能体的行动会影响现实世界。与 LLMs 相比，智能体工具相关的风险可能导致更严重的负面影响（张等，2024b）。在本节中，我们从攻击、防御和评估的角度介绍了智能体工具的可信性，如图 4 所示。

2.3.1. Attack  2.3.1. 攻击

The planning, selection, and execution phases of tool invocation expose additional threat interfaces to attackers. Besides, tools enable agents to conduct certain malicious actions. Based on tool’s role in attacks, we summarize the following two paradigms:
工具调用的规划、选择和执行阶段为攻击者提供了额外的威胁接口。此外，工具使代理能够执行某些恶意行为。根据工具在攻击中的作用，我们总结了以下两种范式：

Tool Manipulation targets specifically at certain steps in the tool calling process to induce execution of malicious or sensitive behaviors. Specifically and technically, we can further list the following methods: ❶ Jailbreak: Cheng et al. (Cheng et al., 2024b) manually design jailbreak prompts to extract personal information from the training data of code generation agent. Furthermore, Imprompter (Fu et al., 2024) and Fu et al. (Fu et al., 2023) utilize gradient optimization to search for input prompts or images automatically, which induce agents to invoke tools to leak privacy from dialogues or execute destructive actions on users’ resources. ❷ Prompt Injection: BreakingAgents (Zhang et al., 2024i) uses human-written prompt injection to achieve malfunction attacks, causing agents to perform repetitive or irrelevant actions, with further exploration on the attack propagation in MAS. ❸ Tool Injection: ToolCommander (Wang et al., 2024f) introduces a two-stage attack strategy: injecting malicious tools to steal user queries first and then manipulating tool selection using the stolen data, achieving privacy theft and denial-of-service attacks. ❹ Command Forgery: AUTOCMD (Jiang et al., 2025) utilizes another LLM, trained on tool calling datasets and enhanced by target-specific examples, to generate and mimic valid commands to deduce sensitive information from the tools. ❺ Direct Manipulation: Zhao et al. (Zhao et al., 2024) manipulate the outputs of third-party APIs by injecting malicious content or deleting critical information, leading to incorrect or biased behaviors.
工具操控专门针对工具调用过程中的特定步骤，以诱导执行恶意或敏感行为。具体而言，从技术角度，我们可以进一步列举以下方法：❶ 越狱攻击：Cheng 等人（Cheng 等人，2024b）手动设计越狱提示词，从代码生成代理的训练数据中提取个人信息。此外，Imprompter（Fu 等人，2024）和 Fu 等人（Fu 等人，2023）利用梯度优化自动搜索输入提示词或图像，诱导代理调用工具从对话中泄露隐私或在用户资源上执行破坏性操作。❷ 提示词注入：BreakingAgents（Zhang 等人，2024i）使用人工编写的提示词注入实现功能失效攻击，导致代理执行重复或不相关的操作，并对多代理系统（MAS）中的攻击传播进行了进一步探索。❸ 工具注入：ToolCommander（Wang 等人，2024f）引入了两阶段攻击策略：首先注入恶意工具来窃取用户查询，然后利用窃取的数据操纵工具选择，实现隐私窃取和拒绝服务攻击。 ❹ 命令伪造：AUTOCMD (Jiang 等人, 2025) 利用另一个 LLM，该 LLM 在工具调用数据集上训练并通过对特定目标的示例进行增强，来生成和模仿有效命令，以从工具中推断敏感信息。❺ 直接操控：Zhao 等人 (Zhao 等人, 2024) 通过注入恶意内容或删除关键信息来操控第三方 API 的输出，导致出现错误或带有偏见的行为。

Tool Abuse refers to the attack type that exploits the tool using capabilities of agent or MAS to achieve or enhance attacks on external entities. For example, in web security, Fang et al. (Fang et al., 2024) explore how agents can autonomously hack websites when equipped with tools, while Fang et al. (Fang et al., 2024) demonstrate that tool-integrated agents can autonomously exploit one-day vulnerabilities in real-world systems. Besides deliberate guidance, BadAgent (Wang et al., 2024e) and Kumar et.al (Kumar et al., 2024) reveal that backdoor attacks or even refusal-based safety alignment can trigger agents to exploit tools for harmful actions.
工具滥用是指利用代理或 MAS 的工具使用能力来对外部实体进行攻击或增强攻击的攻击类型。例如，在网络安全领域，Fang 等人 (Fang 等人, 2024) 探索了代理在配备工具时如何自主地攻击网站，而 Fang 等人 (Fang 等人, 2024) 则展示了集成了工具的代理可以自主地利用现实世界系统中的一日漏洞。除了有意指导之外，BadAgent (Wang 等人, 2024e) 和 Kumar 等人 (Kumar 等人, 2024) 揭示了后门攻击甚至基于拒绝的安全对齐都可能触发代理利用工具进行有害行为。

2.3.2. Defense  2.3.2. 防御

Our investigation reveals that research on defenses against tool-related attacks is notably scarce, whether in the context of LLMs, agents, or MAS. GuardAgent (Xiang et al., 2024b) takes the first step to ensure the trustworthiness of target agents by invoking APIs to execute guardrail code for task plans. In addition, AgentGuard (Chen and Cong, 2025) autonomously identifies unsafe tool-use workflows via LLM orchestrators and generates safety constraints for secure tool using.
我们的调查表明，针对工具相关攻击的防御研究在 LLMs、代理或 MAS 的背景下都明显不足。GuardAgent（Xiang 等人，2024b）率先通过调用 API 执行任务计划的护栏代码，确保目标代理的可信性。此外，AgentGuard（Chen 和 Cong，2025）通过 LLM 协调器自主识别不安全的工具使用工作流，并为安全使用工具生成安全约束。

2.3.3. Evaluation  2.3.3.评估

Due to the unique ability of agents to interact with the environment and their chatbot-like characteristics akin to LLMs, we categorize the paradigms for evaluating the trustworthiness of agent tool invocation into two paradigms:
由于代理具有与环境交互的独特能力，并且其聊天机器人般的特性类似于 LLMs，我们将评估代理工具调用的可信度范式分为两种范式：

Dataset Testing refers to the static evaluation using adversarial query datasets to test the trustworthiness of agent tool invocations. Specifically, ToolSword (Ye et al., 2024) evaluates the safety performance of mainstream LLMs during tool invocation via multiple attack methods: malicious queries and jailbreak attacks at the input phase, noisy misdirection and risky cues during execution, and harmful feedback and error conflicts at the output phase. InjectAgent (Zhan et al., 2024) establishes a benchmark via data generation to test agent trustworthiness against indirect prompt injection attacks during tool usage, including datasets containing malicious queries involving tool invocation. AgentHarm (Andriushchenko et al., 2024) constructs behavioral datasets to assess the harmfulness of agents sequentially invoking multiple tools during task execution, finding that jailbreak templates can be adapted to effectively attack agents. PrivacyLens (Shao et al., 2025) focuses on privacy security assessments during tool execution processes.
数据集测试是指使用对抗性查询数据集对代理工具调用的可信度进行静态评估。具体而言，ToolSword（Ye 等人，2024）通过多种攻击方法评估主流 LLMs 在工具调用过程中的安全性能：输入阶段采用恶意查询和越狱攻击，执行阶段存在噪声误导和风险提示，输出阶段存在有害反馈和错误冲突。InjectAgent（Zhan 等人，2024）通过数据生成建立基准，测试代理在工具使用过程中对间接提示注入攻击的可信度，包括包含恶意工具调用查询的数据集。AgentHarm（Andriushchenko 等人，2024）构建行为数据集，评估代理在任务执行过程中顺序调用多个工具时的危害性，发现越狱模板可被有效用于攻击代理。PrivacyLens（Shao 等人，2025）专注于工具执行过程中的隐私安全评估。

Sandbox Simulation describes the dynamic evaluation simulating tool interactions in controlled environments to assess emergent risks, especially in multi-turn queries. For instance, ToolEmu (Ruan et al., 2023) employs an emulator LLM to simulate tool execution and an evaluator LLM to assess trustworthiness and identify potential risks. In addition, HAICosystem (Zhou et al., 2024) establishes a sandbox environment through role-playing simulations and scenario-specific checklist evaluations to measure safety risks of tool-equipped AI agents in multi-turn interactions, revealing that LLMs exhibit higher risks when functioning as agents with tool access.
沙盒模拟描述了在受控环境中动态评估工具交互的模拟工具，用于评估涌现风险，特别是在多轮查询中。例如，ToolEmu（Ruan 等人，2023 年）采用一个模拟器 LLM 来模拟工具执行，并使用一个评估器 LLM 来评估可信度并识别潜在风险。此外，HAICosystem（Zhou 等人，2024 年）通过角色扮演模拟和特定场景的清单评估建立了一个沙盒环境，以测量配备工具的 AI 代理在多轮交互中的安全风险，揭示当 LLM 作为具有工具访问权限的代理时，它们表现出更高的风险。

2.3.4. Insight.  2.3.4.见地。

A significant pain point in the current study on tool trustworthiness is the lack of defense mechanisms. Specifically, future research directions should focus on ensuring the security of Agents or MAS during tool invocation. Potential defensive measures could include reviewing tools before integration or simulating their execution in an isolated environment to verify safety before actual execution. On the other hand, from an attack perspective, research can follow traditional methods (e.g., jailbreak, prompt injection) while also leveraging tool characteristics to propose novel attack vectors, which may be a future research direction. For instance, since tools are often integrated into agent systems as third-party APIs, and there are currently no mechanisms to prevent malicious API providers, attackers could potentially provide legitimate and seemingly harmless tool descriptions that, when executed, lead to malicious outcomes. Furthermore, as agent systems become more complex, tool invocations may form chains, necessitating a shift in attack, defense, and evaluation research from single tool invocations to multiple invocations based on tool chains.
当前关于工具可信度研究中的一个显著痛点是缺乏防御机制。具体而言，未来的研究方向应聚焦于在工具调用过程中确保代理（Agents）或多代理系统（MAS）的安全。潜在的防御措施可能包括在集成前审查工具，或在隔离环境中模拟其执行以在实际执行前验证安全性。另一方面，从攻击角度出发，研究可以遵循传统方法（例如，越狱、提示注入），同时利用工具特性提出新的攻击向量，这可能成为未来的研究方向。例如，由于工具通常作为第三方 API 集成到代理系统中，而目前没有机制阻止恶意 API 提供者，攻击者可能提供看似合法且无害的工具描述，但在执行时会导致恶意结果。此外，随着代理系统变得更加复杂，工具调用可能形成链式结构，这需要将攻击、防御和评估研究从单个工具调用转向基于工具链的多次调用。

3. Extrinsic Trustworthiness
3. 外部可信度

In this section, we focus on the trustworthiness of external modules interacting with the agent system. During operation, agents continuously engage with the external for purposes such as information gathering or decision execution. We categorize the interactions with the external modules into three types: agent-to-agent (Sec. 3.1), agent-to-environment (Sec. 3.2), and agent-to-user (Sec. 3.3). We first introduce the role of each type and then elaborate on corresponding trustworthiness research, ending up with our insights.
在本节中，我们关注与智能体系统交互的外部模块的可信度。在运行过程中，智能体会持续与外部进行交互，以收集信息或执行决策等目的。我们将与外部模块的交互分为三种类型：智能体与智能体（第 3.1 节）、智能体与环境（第 3.2 节）以及智能体与用户（第 3.3 节）。我们首先介绍每种类型的角色，然后详细阐述相应的可信度研究，最后总结我们的见解。

3.1. Agent-to-Agent Interaction
3.1.智能体间交互

Interactions between agents are crucial to system functioning and take various patterns, such as cooperation, competition and debate (Panait and Luke, 2005). These Agent-to-Agent Interaction, is the key to shaping the system’s dynamics. However, they differ significantly in terms of their nature and associated trustworthiness risks (Hammond et al., 2025). To this end, we introduce current works related to agent interactions from attacks, defenses, and evaluations, with illustration in Figure 5.
智能体之间的交互对系统运行至关重要，并呈现出多种模式，如合作、竞争和辩论（Panait 和 Luke，2005）。这些智能体之间的交互是塑造系统动态的关键。然而，它们在性质和相关的可信度风险方面存在显著差异（Hammond 等人，2025）。为此，我们介绍了与智能体交互相关的当前研究，涵盖攻击、防御和评估，如图 5 所示。

3.1.1. Attack  3.1.1. 攻击

Unlike the simple information flow of a single agent, agent-agent interactions can not only be used to enhance attacks on a single agent via cooperation but also exploit its propagative nature to induce a trustworthiness crisis at the MAS level.
与单个智能体简单的信息流不同，智能体之间的交互不仅可以通过合作来增强对单个智能体的攻击，还可以利用其传播特性在多智能体系统（MAS）层面引发可信度危机。

Cooperative Attack refers to malicious agents collaborateing to compromise the targeted agent-agent interactions, disrupting system safety. In terms of truthfulness, Ju et al. (Ju et al., 2024) coordinate agents in MAS to spread counterfactual and harmful information, amplified by persuasive manipulation. Regarding robustness, Agent-in-the-Middle (He et al., 2025) disrupts MAS coordination by using intermediary agent to intercept and manipulate communication, while Amayuelas et al. (Amayuelas et al., 2024) exploit adversarial persuasion to further weaken system stability. As for safety, Evil Geniuses (Tian et al., 2023) uses compromised agents to create adversarial prompts, refining attack through iterative simulations.
协同攻击是指恶意智能体合作来破坏目标智能体之间的交互，从而扰乱系统安全。在真实性方面，Ju 等人（Ju 等人，2024）协调 MAS 中的智能体传播反事实和有害信息，并通过有说服力的操控加以放大。在鲁棒性方面，中间人攻击（He 等人，2025）通过使用中间智能体拦截和操控通信来破坏 MAS 的协调，而 Amayuelas 等人（Amayuelas 等人，2024）则利用对抗性说服进一步削弱系统稳定性。在安全性方面，邪恶天才（Tian 等人，2023）利用被攻陷的智能体创建对抗性提示，通过迭代模拟来优化攻击。

Infectious Attack spreads malicious effects by infecting others or disrupt agents or components within MAS. Prompt Infection (Lee and Tiwari, 2024) and CORBA (Zhou et al., 2025b) exploit this attack in text modality. Specifically, Prompt Infection uses silent adversarial diffusion to facilitate data theft and misinformation, while CORBA introduces a self-propagating, topology-independent attack that drains resources. In the context of multi-modality, Agent Smith (Gu et al., 2024) and Tan et al. (Tan et al., 2024) extend this attack to image modality, enhancing stealth and contagion, which accelerates the collapse of MAS trustworthiness. Finally, NetSafe (Yu et al., 2024) analyzes how hallucinations and misinformation propagate across MAS topologies, revealing their structural dependencies and adversarial impacts.
感染攻击通过感染他人或破坏 MAS 内的代理或组件来传播恶意影响。即时感染（Lee 和 Tiwari，2024）和 CORBA（Zhou 等人，2025b）利用这种攻击在文本模态中。具体来说，即时感染使用静默对抗扩散来促进数据窃取和虚假信息传播，而 CORBA 引入了一种自我传播、与拓扑无关的攻击，该攻击会消耗资源。在多模态的背景下，Agent Smith（Gu 等人，2024）和 Tan 等人（Tan 等人，2024）将这种攻击扩展到图像模态，增强了隐蔽性和传染性，从而加速了 MAS 可信度的崩溃。最后，NetSafe（Yu 等人，2024）分析了幻觉和虚假信息如何在 MAS 拓扑中传播，揭示了它们的结构依赖性和对抗性影响。

3.1.2. Defense  3.1.2. 防御

Similar to Cooperative Attacks, we can enhance defense mechanisms by leveraging the cooperative ability of MAS. Besides, the interactions between agents allows modeling them as a graph, enabling defense strategies from a topological perspective.
类似于协同攻击，我们可以通过利用多智能体系统的协同能力来增强防御机制。此外，智能体之间的交互使得它们可以被建模为图，从而可以从拓扑学的角度制定防御策略。

Collaborative Defense uses agents cooperation via information sharing for a trusworthy analysis on the target agent response or action. Based on debate pattern, BlockAgents (Chen et al., 2024a) ensures secure coordination through multi-round debate voting with Proof-of-Thought consensus, while Audit-LLM (Song et al., 2024a; Chern et al., 2024) shifts to hallucination, safety and robustness dimension. Except for debating, adversarial techniques are used for defense. For example, AutoDefense (Zeng et al., 2024a) enhances safety through adversarial prompt filtering, while LLAMOS (Lin and Zhao, 2024) establishes dynamic defense mechanisms to foster a robust equilibrium between attacker and defender. PsySafe (Zhang et al., 2024l) specifically targets dark property injection attacks, further solidifying the role of MAS in adversarial defense.
协同防御通过信息共享实现代理间的合作，对目标代理的响应或行为进行可信分析。基于辩论模式，BlockAgents（陈等人，2024a）通过多轮辩论投票和思想证明共识确保安全协调，而 Audit-LLM（宋等人，2024a；陈等人，2024）则转向幻觉、安全性和鲁棒性维度。除了辩论，对抗技术也用于防御。例如，AutoDefense（曾等人，2024a）通过对抗性提示过滤增强安全性，而 LLAMOS（林和赵，2024）建立动态防御机制，在攻击者和防御者之间形成鲁棒平衡。PsySafe（张等人，2024l）专门针对黑暗属性注入攻击，进一步巩固了 MAS 在对抗防御中的作用。

Topological Defense leverages network structure to isolate threats and limit their spread and impact. GPTSwarm (Zhuge et al., 2024) represents an initial exploration into enhancing MAS robustness through topology optimization. In contrast, G-Safeguard (Wang et al., 2025b) delves deeper into combating adversarial attacks and misinformation in MAS across various topologies, employing graph neural networks (GNNs) to detect anomalies in discourse graphs.
拓扑防御利用网络结构来隔离威胁，限制其传播和影响。GPTSwarm（Zhuge 等人，2024）代表了通过拓扑优化增强多智能体系统（MAS）鲁棒性的初步探索。相比之下，G-Safeguard（Wang 等人，2025b）深入研究了在各种拓扑结构中对抗多智能体系统中的对抗攻击和虚假信息，采用图神经网络（GNNs）来检测话语图中的异常。

3.1.3. Evaluation  3.1.3.评估

The evaluation of agent-to-agent trustworthiness is still in its infancy with limited number of research. SafeAgentBench (Yin et al., 2024) introduces SafeAgentEnv, which enables multi-agent execution with various actions and baseline models, to evaluate defense success rate. R-judge (Yuan et al., 2024a) assesses agent interaction safety in multi-turn interactions across several domains and risk scenarios. (BENCHMARK, [n. d.]) proposes JAILJUDGE, a benchmark covering synthetic, adversarial, outdoor, and multilingual risks and featuring a manually annotated dataset and multi-agent evaluation.
智能体之间的可信度评估仍处于起步阶段，研究数量有限。SafeAgentBench（Yin 等人，2024）引入了 SafeAgentEnv，它支持具有各种动作和基线模型的多智能体执行，以评估防御成功率。R-judge（Yuan 等人，2024a）评估了多个领域和多轮交互中的智能体交互安全性。（BENCHMARK，[n. d.]）提出了 JAILJUDGE，这是一个涵盖合成、对抗、户外和多语言风险的基准，具有手动标注的数据集和多智能体评估功能。

3.1.4. Insight  3.1.4.见解

We can clearly observe that agent-agent interactions lead to a novel and severe threat to trustworthiness—Infectious Attack. Future research on the attack side could focus on how to automate such attacks against specific agents or MAS. Simultaneously, corresponding anti-propagation defense mechanisms and evaluation methods are also potential research topics. Additionally, some studies treat MAS as (temporal) graphs, with agents as nodes and interactions as directed edges, and have conducted preliminary explorations of topology-based defenses or trustworthiness evaluations. This perspective might also be a point worthy of further investigation. Moreover, we have not found research strictly focused on the trustworthiness evaluation of inter-agent interactions, a gap that could potentially be filled by using LLMs or agents as dynamic detectors and evaluators. Research on the evaluation side may appear more urgent and deserving of attention.
我们可以清楚地观察到，智能体之间的交互导致了信任的新颖且严重的威胁——感染式攻击。未来针对攻击方面的研究可以聚焦于如何自动化针对特定智能体或多智能体系统（MAS）的此类攻击。同时，相应的反传播防御机制和评估方法也是潜在的研究课题。此外，一些研究将 MAS 视为（时序）图，将智能体作为节点，将交互作为有向边，并对基于拓扑的防御或信任评估进行了初步探索。这一视角也可能是一个值得进一步研究的点。此外，我们尚未发现严格聚焦于智能体间交互信任评估的研究，这一空白有可能通过使用 LLMs 或智能体作为动态检测器和评估器来填补。评估方面的研究可能显得更加紧迫且值得关注。

3.2.1. Physical Environment
3.2.1.物理环境

In this environment, agents are transforming sectors like robotics, autonomous vehicles, and industrial systems, bringing both new opportunities and challenges. Researchers are actively developing innovative solutions to address these challenges, aiming to optimize performance while maintaining the highest truthfulness standards across physical applications.
在这个环境中，智能体正在改变机器人、自动驾驶汽车和工业系统等领域，既带来了新的机遇也带来了挑战。研究人员正积极开发创新解决方案来应对这些挑战，旨在优化性能的同时，在物理应用中保持最高的真实性标准。

Robotics: Yang et al. (Yang et al., 2024b) propose a constraint module based on Linear Temporal Logic, which enables safety violation reasoning and explanation, and unsafe action pruning, ensuring that robot agents meet global safety standards for industrial deployment. Additionally, SELP (Wu et al., 2024) integrates equivalence voting and domain-specific fine-tuning to ensure that robot agents generate safety task plans such as drone navigation and robot manipulation.
机器人学：杨等人（Yang et al, 2024b）提出了一种基于线性时序逻辑的约束模块，该模块能够进行安全违规推理和解释，并剔除不安全动作，确保机器人代理满足工业部署的全球安全标准。此外，SELP（Wu et al, 2024）集成了等价投票和领域特定微调，以确保机器人代理能够生成安全任务计划，例如无人机导航和机器人操作。

Autonomous Driving: Hudson (Song et al., 2024b) formalizes real-time perception data into natural language with attack detection instructions and analyzes causal reasoning to enhance safety and truthfulness during perception attacks. Furthermore, ChatScene (Zhang et al., 2024j) generates safety-critical scenarios for autonomous vehicles by transforming unstructured language instructions into domain-specific code for CARLA platform simulations.
自动驾驶：Hudson（宋等人，2024b）将实时感知数据转化为自然语言，并加入攻击检测指令，通过因果推理分析来增强感知攻击中的安全性和真实性。此外，ChatScene（张等人，2024j）通过将非结构化语言指令转化为特定领域的代码，为自动驾驶车辆生成安全关键场景，用于 CARLA 平台的模拟。

Industrial Control: Vyas et al. (Vyas and Mercangöz, 2024) propose an agent framework for autonomous industrial control, featuring validation and reprompting architectures that ensure real-time error detection, recovery, and safe decision-making. Agents4PLC (Liu et al., 2024c) automates PLC code generation and ensures truthfulness through rigorous code-level verification, improving correctness and reliability in industrial control systems by integrating RAG and COT.
工业控制：Vyas 等人（Vyas 和 Mercangöz，2024）提出了一种用于自主工业控制的代理框架，该框架具有验证和重提示架构，确保实时错误检测、恢复和安全的决策。Agents4PLC（刘等人，2024c）自动化 PLC 代码生成，并通过严格的代码级验证确保真实性，通过集成 RAG 和 COT，提高了工业控制系统的正确性和可靠性。

3.2.2. Digital Environment:
3.2.2. 数字环境：

Research focusing on the applications of virtual data across digital environments aims to enhance task-specific performance such as improving navigation on the web, diagnostic accuracy in healthcare, and efficiency in financial tasks. However, the vulnerability of data leads to a growing focus on addressing critical security, ethical, and compliance issues.
研究聚焦于虚拟数据在数字环境中的应用，旨在提升特定任务的性能，如改善网络导航、医疗保健中的诊断准确性和金融任务的效率。然而，数据的脆弱性导致人们越来越关注解决关键的安全、伦理和合规问题。

Network: Fang et al. (Fang et al., 2024) point out that agents can uncover web vulnerabilities exploited for hacking attacks autonomously. Furthermore, researchers develop frameworks to evaluate the truthfulness of web agents. Debenedetti et al. (Debenedetti et al., 2024) focus on the evaluation of defense in specific tasks like managing email clients.
网络：方等人（Fang et al, 2024）指出，代理可以自主发现用于黑客攻击的网络漏洞。此外，研究人员开发了评估网络代理真实性的框架。Debenedetti 等人（Debenedetti et al, 2024）专注于评估特定任务（如管理电子邮件客户端）中的防御措施。

Healthcare: For privacy, Xiang et al. (Xiang et al., 2024b) propose that LLM agents safeguard sensitive medical data through knowledge-driven reasoning. Regarding robustness, Polaris (Mukherjee et al., 2024) suggests enhancing the reliability and robustness of LLM agents via multi-agent architectures for real-time patient-AI interactions.
医疗保健：在隐私方面，向等人（Xiang et al, 2024b）提出，LLM 代理可以通过知识驱动的推理来保护敏感的医学数据。在鲁棒性方面，Polaris（Mukherjee et al, 2024）建议通过多代理架构增强 LLM 代理的可靠性和鲁棒性，以实现实时的人机交互。

Finance: Chen et al. (Chen et al., 2025a) identify risks such as hallucinations, temporal misalignment, and adversarial vulnerabilities in financial applications for finance agents. Meanwhile, Park et al. (Park, 2024) demonstrate how LLM agents can enhance anomaly detection via role-playing scenarios like manager-analyst communications, thus contributing significantly to risk management.
金融：陈等人（Chen et al, 2025a）在金融代理的金融应用中识别了幻觉、时间错位和对抗性漏洞等风险。同时，朴等人（Park, 2024）展示了 LLM 代理如何通过角色扮演场景（如经理-分析师的沟通）增强异常检测，从而显著促进风险管理。

Social Media: Jeptoo et al. (Jeptoo and Sun, 2024) show that agents can be utilized for fake news detection through multi-agent collaboration and automated workflows. La et al. (La Cava and Tagarelli, 2024) use agents to simulate the evolution of language patterns that try to evade social media regulations, contributing to content regulation.
社交媒体：Jeptoo 等人（Jeptoo and Sun, 2024）表明代理可以通过多代理协作和自动化工作流程用于虚假新闻检测。La 等人（La Cava and Tagarelli, 2024）使用代理模拟试图规避社交媒体法规的语言模式演变，促进内容监管。

3.2.3. Insight.  3.2.3.洞见。

In current research, the environment is often treated as a static backdrop, with safety efforts primarily focused on improving agent action outcomes. However, this approach overlooks the critical issue of trustworthy interactions between agents and environments, which represents a significant point of vulnerability for both attacks and defenses. Consequently, systematic attack and defense mechanisms are necessary for targeting environment-agent interactions to enhance system truthfulness and safety. For example, adversaries might subtly alter environmental feedback to mislead agents into making suboptimal or unsafe decisions.
在当前研究中，环境通常被视为静态背景，安全工作主要集中于提高智能体行为结果。然而，这种方法忽视了智能体与环境之间可信赖交互的关键问题，这既是攻击和防御的重要脆弱点。因此，需要系统性的攻击和防御机制来针对环境-智能体交互，以增强系统真实性和安全性。例如，攻击者可能微妙地改变环境反馈，误导智能体做出次优或危险决策。

Current evaluations are predominantly focused on a limited set of domain-specific safety scenarios, failing to address the broader spectrum of interdisciplinary and cross-domain challenges. To advance the field, developing evaluations that address cross-domain and interdisciplinary safety challenges is essential for progress.
当前评估主要集中于有限的一组特定领域安全场景，未能涵盖更广泛的跨学科和跨领域挑战。为了推动该领域发展，开发能够应对跨领域和跨学科安全挑战的评估对于进步至关重要。

3.3. Agent-to-User Interaction
3.3.代理与用户交互

In this section, we discuss trustworthiness considerations in agent-user interaction, with particular emphasis on the user perspective (He et al., 2024). We elaborate on key challenges in building reliable, human-aligned agent systems. In our survey, the works summarized in previous sections focus largely on agent-to-user interactions, which overlap significantly with the content discussed in this subsection. Therefore, this section will mainly offer discussions and insights.
在本节中，我们讨论代理与用户交互中的可信性问题，特别强调用户视角（He 等人，2024 年）。我们详细阐述了构建可靠、与人类行为一致的代理系统的关键挑战。在我们的综述中，前几节总结的工作主要关注代理与用户的交互，这与本小节讨论的内容有显著重叠。因此，本节将主要提供讨论和见解。

3.3.1. Discussion  3.3.1.讨论

Current research on agent trustworthiness focuses on safety and reliability but overlooks trust mechanisms in interactions process. A key gap is how users adjust trust based on agent behavior. While personalization boosts engagement, it also risks manipulation, requiring a balance with system robustness. Transparency is essential, as clear explanations help users manage risks. As agents evolve, they enhance trust while protecting user data. However, most studies address single-agent interactions, leaving multi-agent trust dynamics largely unexplored.
当前关于代理可信性的研究侧重于安全和可靠性，但忽视了交互过程中的信任机制。一个关键差距是用户如何根据代理行为调整信任。虽然个性化可以提高参与度，但也存在操纵风险，需要与系统鲁棒性保持平衡。透明度至关重要，清晰的解释有助于用户管理风险。随着代理的进化，它们在保护用户数据的同时增强了信任。然而，大多数研究只关注单代理交互，多代理信任动态仍基本未得到探索。

3.3.2. Insight  3.3.2. 见解

To advance research on trust in agent-to-user interactions, future work should focus on developing adaptive trust calibration frameworks that enable users to dynamically adjust trust thresholds based on real-time interactions. Feedback mechanisms should be optimized to reinforce beneficial agent behaviors while preventing trust erosion due to errors or biases. Additionally, a unified paradigm can be established to regulate and ensure fairness and reliability across different users and personalized agents.
为推进对智能体与用户交互中信任的研究，未来的工作应聚焦于开发自适应信任校准框架，使用户能够根据实时交互动态调整信任阈值。反馈机制应优化，以强化有益的智能体行为，同时防止因错误或偏见导致的信任侵蚀。此外，可以建立统一范式来规范和确保不同用户与个性化智能体之间的公平性和可靠性。

A promising direction to improve transparency is by developing explainable agents that provide decisions and interpretable reasons to users. In the context of multi-agent, maintaining trust requires innovative monitoring—using supervisors agents to oversee interactions with user and ensure consistent responses.
提高透明度的一个有前景的方向是开发可解释的智能体，它们向用户提供决策和可解释的理由。在多智能体环境中，维持信任需要创新的监控——使用监督智能体来监督与用户的交互，并确保一致的响应。

4. Conclusion  4. 结论

In this survey, we introduce the six key components of agent systems, both internal and external. Focusing on each module, we summarize current research on trustworthy agents across multiple dimensions such as safety and privacy, from the perspectives of attack, defense, and evaluation. We summarize and define the typical method paradigm in each perspective to standardize and inspire further study. Notably, we identify several research and technical gaps in existing works, such as defense mechanisms for trustworthy tool invocation and the evaluation of trustworthiness in memory and agent interactions. Based on this, we highlight future research directions and key insights about the trustworthiness of agent and MAS, emphasizing their distinctive attributes and implications. We believe that this survey may encourage and inspire further research and exploration of trustworthy agent by both researchers and developers.
在这篇综述中，我们介绍了智能体系统的六个关键组成部分，包括内部和外部。聚焦于每个模块，我们从攻击、防御和评估的角度，总结了关于可信智能体的当前研究，涵盖安全性和隐私等多个维度。我们总结了并定义了每个视角下的典型方法范式，以标准化并启发进一步的研究。值得注意的是，我们指出了现有工作中的若干研究和技术空白，例如可信工具调用的防御机制以及记忆和智能体交互中的可信度评估。基于此，我们强调了关于智能体和 MAS 可信度的未来研究方向和关键见解，突出了它们的独特属性和影响。我们相信，这篇综述能够鼓励和启发研究人员和开发者对可信智能体进行进一步的研究和探索。

Appendix A Trustworthiness Definition
附录 A 可信性定义

In this section, we provide our definitions on the different dimensions (safety, privacy, truthfulness, fairness and robustness) of agent trustworthiness which we consider in TrustAgent for guideline.
在本节中，我们提供了关于智能体可信度不同维度（安全性、隐私性、真实性、公平性和鲁棒性）的定义，这些定义构成了 TrustAgent 的指导方针。

Safety in agents and MAS refers to preventing harmful actions or outputs, ensuring protection against adversarial behaviors and system failures. In MAS, cooperative attacks, such as Evil Geniuses refining adversarial prompts through iterative simulations, and infectious attacks, like Agent Smith’s self-replicating images causing exponential risk spread, exploit inter-agent communication to amplify threats. At the agent level, jailbreak attacks (e.g., MRJ-Agent generating covert prompts) and prompt injections (e.g., BreakingAgents inducing repetitive or irrelevant actions) manipulate the agent’s brain module to bypass safety mechanisms. Ensuring safety requires collaborative defenses, such as multi-agent debate (e.g., BlockAgents) and guardrail agents (e.g., GuardAgent), to dynamically monitor and validate actions.
智能体和 MAS 中的安全性是指防止有害行为或输出，确保对抗行为和系统故障的防护。在 MAS 中，合作攻击（例如，邪恶天才通过迭代模拟改进对抗性提示）和传染性攻击（例如，Agent Smith 的自我复制图像导致指数级风险传播）利用智能体间通信来放大威胁。在智能体层面，越狱攻击（例如，MRJ-Agent 生成隐蔽提示）和提示注入（例如，BreakingAgents 诱导重复或不相关的行为）操纵智能体的脑模块以绕过安全机制。确保安全性需要协作防御，例如多智能体辩论（例如，BlockAgents）和护栏智能体（例如，GuardAgent），以动态监控和验证行为。

Privacy involves the protection of user data and autonomy in single agent and MAS, preventing unauthorized access or leakage through inter-agent communication. In MAS, privacy risks escalate through attacks like Prompt Infection, which silently spreads adversarial prompts to steal data, and ToolCommander, which manipulates tool selection to leak sensitive information. At the agent level, memory poisoning (e.g., PoisonedRAG injecting malicious text into vector databases) and embedding inversion (e.g., reconstructing private data from embeddings) exploit memory modules. Privacy defenses include query rewriting (e.g., Anderson et al.’s prompt templates) and multi-round adversarial dialogue training to enhance robustness against memory misuse.
隐私涉及在单智能体和多智能体系统（MAS）中保护用户数据和自主性，通过防止智能体间通信中的未授权访问或信息泄露来实现。在 MAS 中，隐私风险会因 Prompt Infection 等攻击而加剧，此类攻击会悄无声息地传播对抗性提示以窃取数据，以及 ToolCommander，它通过操纵工具选择来泄露敏感信息。在智能体层面，记忆中毒（例如，PoisonedRAG 将恶意文本注入向量数据库）和嵌入反转（例如，从嵌入中重建私人数据）会利用记忆模块。隐私防御措施包括查询重写（例如，Anderson 等人提出的提示模板）和多轮对抗性对话训练，以增强对记忆滥用的鲁棒性。

Truthfulness ensures the accurate and reliable generation of information across MAS, maintaining consistency and avoiding misinformation propagation among agents. In MAS, misinformation spreads through topological dependencies, as seen in CORBA’s self-propagating attacks that drain resources or NetSafe’s analysis of hallucination propagation across network structures. At the agent level, hallucinations are exacerbated by tool misuse (e.g., faulty API returns) or environmental misalignment (e.g., perception attacks in autonomous driving). Ensuring truthfulness requires multi-agent debate (e.g., Du et al.’s factuality improvement) and graph-based anomaly detection (e.g., G-Safeguard) to verify information consistency and reliability.
真实性确保在多智能体系统（MAS）中准确可靠地生成信息，保持一致性并避免错误信息在智能体间传播。在 MAS 中，错误信息通过拓扑依赖性传播，例如 CORBA 的自传播攻击消耗资源或 NetSafe 对网络结构中幻觉传播的分析。在智能体层面，工具误用（例如，有缺陷的 API 返回）或环境错位（例如，自动驾驶中的感知攻击）会加剧幻觉。确保真实性需要多智能体辩论（例如，Du 等人提高事实性的研究）和基于图的异常检测（例如，G-Safeguard）来验证信息的一致性和可靠性。

Fairness in agents means impartial user treatment and equitable resource allocation, free from bias or discrimination. In MAS, fairness issues arise from resource monopolization (e.g., high-capability agents dominating API access in financial systems) and task allocation biases (e.g., GPTSwarm’s central nodes becoming overloaded). At the agent level, memory retrieval biases (e.g., underrepresented data in RAG pipelines) and tool access disparities (e.g., privileged agents in industrial control systems) exacerbate inequities. Addressing fairness requires dynamic resource scheduling (e.g., game-theoretic approaches) and federated reward mechanisms to ensure balanced participation and equitable outcomes.
代理中的公平意味着无偏见的用户对待和公平的资源分配，不受偏见或歧视。在多智能体系统（MAS）中，公平问题源于资源垄断（例如，高能力代理在金融系统中主导 API 访问）和任务分配偏见（例如，GPTSwarm 的中心节点过载）。在代理层面，记忆检索偏见（例如，RAG 管道中代表性不足的数据）和工具访问差异（例如，工业控制系统中的特权代理）加剧了不平等。解决公平问题需要动态资源调度（例如，博弈论方法）和联邦奖励机制，以确保平衡参与和公平结果。

Robustness in the context of agent and MAS is the ability to maintain stable performance under diverse environments, uncertainties, and adversarial conditions. In MAS, robustness is challenged by topological vulnerabilities (e.g., GPTSwarm’s optimization failures under adversarial conditions) and dynamic environmental changes (e.g., real-time error recovery in industrial control systems). At the agent level, robustness is compromised by tool chain failures (e.g., ToolEmu’s simulated execution errors) and memory retrieval corruption (e.g., GARAG’s genetic algorithm-based poisoning). Enhancing robustness involves topology-guided defenses (e.g., G-Safeguard’s graph neural networks) and real-time error detection (e.g., Vyas et al.’s validation architectures) to ensure resilience in complex and evolving scenarios.
在智能体和多智能体系统（MAS）的语境中，鲁棒性是指在不同环境、不确定性和对抗性条件下保持稳定性能的能力。在 MAS 中，鲁棒性受到拓扑脆弱性（例如 GPTSwarm 在对抗性条件下的优化失败）和动态环境变化（例如工业控制系统中的实时错误恢复）的挑战。在智能体层面，鲁棒性受到工具链故障（例如 ToolEmu 的模拟执行错误）和内存检索损坏（例如 GARAG 基于遗传算法的污染）的影响。增强鲁棒性涉及拓扑引导的防御（例如 G-Safeguard 的图神经网络）和实时错误检测（例如 Vyas 等人提出的验证架构），以确保在复杂和不断变化的环境中保持韧性。

Others: Trustworthiness in MAS extends beyond safety, privacy, truthfulness, fairness, and robustness to include agent accountability, ethics, transparency, and explainability. Accountability ensures traceable actions (e.g., blockchain logging in finance) to prevent blame-shifting. Ethics aligns agents with human values, addressing issues like bias or misuse. Transparency reveals internal processes (e.g., ToolCommander’s tool selection logic) to build trust. Explainability provides clear justifications for actions (e.g., natural language explanations in healthcare) to validate behavior. Together, these dimensions create a robust framework for trustworthy MAS across applications.
其他：在多智能体系统（MAS）中，可信性不仅包括安全性、隐私性、真实性、公平性和鲁棒性，还包括智能体问责制、伦理道德、透明度和可解释性。问责制确保可追溯的行为（例如金融领域的区块链日志记录），以防止责任推诿。伦理道德使智能体与人类价值观保持一致，解决偏见或滥用等问题。透明度揭示内部流程（例如 ToolCommander 的工具选择逻辑），以建立信任。可解释性为行为提供清晰的解释（例如医疗保健中的自然语言解释），以验证行为。这些维度共同为跨应用的可信 MAS 构建了一个强大的框架。
