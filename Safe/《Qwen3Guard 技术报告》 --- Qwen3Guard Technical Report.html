Qwen3Guard Technical Report
《Qwen3Guard 技术报告》

Qwen Team
Abstract  摘要

As large language models (LLMs) become more capable and widely used, ensuring the safety of their outputs is increasingly critical. Existing guardrail models, though useful in static evaluation settings, face two major limitations in real-world applications: (1) they typically output only binary “safe/unsafe” labels, which can be interpreted inconsistently across diverse safety policies, rendering them incapable of accommodating varying safety tolerances across domains; and (2) they require complete model outputs before performing safety checks, making them fundamentally incompatible with streaming LLM inference, thereby preventing timely intervention during generation and increasing exposure to harmful partial outputs.
随着大型语言模型（LLMs）的能力越来越强且应用越来越广泛，确保其输出的安全性变得日益关键。现有的防护模型虽然在对静态评估场景有用，但在实际应用中面临两大主要限制：（1）它们通常只输出“安全/不安全”的二进制标签，这些标签在不同的安全策略中可能被不一致地解释，导致它们无法适应不同领域不同的安全容忍度；（2）它们在执行安全检查前需要完整的模型输出，这使得它们从根本上与流式 LLM 推理不兼容，从而阻止了在生成过程中的及时干预，并增加了接触有害部分输出的风险。

To address these challenges, we present Qwen3Guard, a series of multilingual safety guardrail models with two specialized variants: Generative Qwen3Guard, which casts safety classification as an instruction-following task to enable fine-grained tri-class judgments (safe, controversial, unsafe); and Stream Qwen3Guard, which introduces a token-level classification head for real-time safety monitoring during incremental text generation. Both variants are available in three sizes (0.6B, 4B, and 8B parameters) and support up to 119 languages and dialects, providing comprehensive, scalable, and low-latency safety moderation for global LLM deployments. Evaluated across English, Chinese, and multilingual benchmarks, Qwen3Guard achieves state-of-the-art performance in both prompt and response safety classification. All models are released under the Apache 2.0 license for public use.
为应对这些挑战，我们提出了 Qwen3Guard，一系列多语言安全护栏模型，包含两种专门变体：生成式 Qwen3Guard，将安全分类视为指令跟随任务，以实现细粒度的三分类判断（安全、有争议、不安全）；以及流式 Qwen3Guard，在增量文本生成过程中引入标记级分类头进行实时安全监控。这两种变体均提供三种尺寸（0.6B、4B 和 8B 参数），支持高达 119 种语言和方言，为全球 LLM 部署提供全面、可扩展且低延迟的安全审核。在英语、中文和多语言基准测试中，Qwen3Guard 在提示和响应安全分类方面均达到最先进性能。所有模型均在 Apache 2.0 许可证下公开发布。

1 Introduction  1 引言

In recent years, the advancement of large foundation models has accelerated dramatically. Models such as GPT-5 (OpenAI, 2025), Claude 4 (Anthropic, 2025), Gemini 2.5 (Comanici et al., 2025), DeepSeek-V3 (Liu et al., 2024b), Llama-4 (Meta-AI, 2025), and the Qwen series (Bai et al., 2023; Yang et al., 2024a; b; c; 2025a; 2025b; Hui et al., 2024) have demonstrated unprecedented capabilities in natural language understanding and generation, enabling increasingly sophisticated applications across diverse domains and languages. However, as these models grow more powerful and are deployed in broader real-world scenarios, the safety of their generated content has become a critical concern. Unconstrained models may inadvertently produce outputs that are harmful, biased, or even illegal, posing significant risks to users, enterprises, and society at large. To mitigate these risks, guardrail models such as LlamaGuard (Inan et al., 2023; Chi et al., 2024), ShieldGemma (Zeng et al., 2024), WildGuard (Han et al., 2024), are widely adopted as filtering mechanisms. These models perform real-time risk detection and classification on both user inputs (User Prompts) and model outputs (Model Responses), ensuring safer interactions in AI systems.
近年来，大型基础模型的进步速度急剧加快。GPT-5（OpenAI，2025）、Claude 4（Anthropic，2025）、Gemini 2.5（Comanici 等人，2025）、DeepSeek-V3（刘等人，2024b）、Llama-4（Meta-AI，2025）以及 Qwen 系列（白等人，2023；杨等人，2024a；b；c；2025a；2025b；惠等人，2024）等模型在自然语言理解和生成方面展现出前所未有的能力，使跨不同领域和语言的复杂应用成为可能。然而，随着这些模型变得越来越强大并在更广泛的现实场景中部署，其生成内容的安全性已成为一个关键问题。无约束模型可能会无意中产生有害、有偏见甚至非法的输出，给用户、企业和整个社会带来重大风险。为缓解这些风险，LlamaGuard（Inan 等人，2023；Chi 等人，2024）、ShieldGemma（曾等人，2024）、WildGuard（韩等人，2024）等防护模型被广泛采用作为过滤机制。 这些模型对用户输入（用户提示）和模型输出（模型响应）进行实时风险检测和分类，确保 AI 系统中的交互更安全。

However, existing Guard models suffer from two key limitations: (1) Inconsistent and Inflexible Across Safety Policies. Different guard models and safety datasets often implement divergent safety policies, leading to conflicting interpretations of labels and undermining the reliability of both training and evaluation processes. Moreover, real-world deployment scenarios inherently demand varying safety standards, where guard models must be adaptable to a wide range of potential contexts. (2) Incompatibility with Streaming Outputs. Existing open-source guard models are designed to evaluate only complete responses, which is fundamentally misaligned with the streaming generation paradigm adopted by modern LLMs. This limitation hinders timely intervention and real-time content moderation during interactive sessions.
然而，现有的 Guard 模型存在两个关键局限性：(1) 在安全策略上不一致且不灵活。不同的 Guard 模型和安全数据集往往实施不同的安全策略，导致标签解释存在分歧，从而削弱了训练和评估过程的可靠性。此外，现实世界的部署场景本质上需要不同的安全标准，Guard 模型必须能够适应广泛潜在的上下文。(2) 与流式输出不兼容。现有的开源 Guard 模型仅设计用于评估完整的响应，这与现代 LLMs 采用的流式生成范式根本不一致。这一局限性阻碍了在交互会话中进行及时干预和实时内容审核。

To address these challenges, we introduce Qwen3Guard, a multilingual safety guardrail model that achieves state-of-the-art performance across a wide range of safety benchmarks. Beyond the conventional binary labels of safe and unsafe, we introduce a controversial label to capture instances whose safety label may vary depending on contextual factors or differing safety policies. This fine-grained categorization enhances the model’s adaptability to diverse moderation requirements. Qwen3Guard has two specialized variants: Generative Qwen3Guard (i.e., Qwen3Guard-Gen), which reformulates safety classification as an instruction-following task for generative models and achieves robust input/output classification; and Stream Qwen3Guard (i.e., Qwen3Guard-Stream), which augments the architecture with an auxiliary token-level classification head to enable efficient, real-time streaming safety detection during response generation. Both variants are available in three model sizes, 0.6B, 4B, and 8B parameters, to accommodate diverse deployment scenarios and resource constraints.
为应对这些挑战，我们引入了 Qwen3Guard，这是一个多语言安全护栏模型，在广泛的多个安全基准测试中实现了最先进的性能。除了传统的安全与不安全二元标签外，我们引入了一个争议性标签，用于捕捉那些安全标签可能因上下文因素或不同的安全政策而有所不同的案例。这种细粒度分类增强了模型适应多样化审核需求的灵活性。Qwen3Guard 有两个专门变体：生成式 Qwen3Guard（即 Qwen3Guard-Gen），它将安全分类重新定义为生成模型的指令跟随任务，并实现了稳健的输入/输出分类；以及流式 Qwen3Guard（即 Qwen3Guard-Stream），它通过添加一个辅助的 token 级分类头来增强架构，以在生成回复期间实现高效的实时流式安全检测。这两个变体均提供三种模型尺寸，0.6B、4B 和 8B 参数，以适应不同的部署场景和资源限制。

We comprehensively evaluate Qwen3Guard across a diverse suite of benchmarks, including English, Chinese, and multilingual datasets. The results demonstrate that Generative Qwen3Guard outperforms existing state-of-the-art models in detecting unsafe prompts and responses across diverse languages. Meanwhile, Stream Qwen3Guard enables highly efficient real-time safety monitoring during generation, with only modest performance degradation compared with the Generative Qwen3Guard. Beyond the performance, we further illustrate the practical utility of Qwen3Guard through two applications: (1) when deployed as a feedback signal within the RLAIF framework, Generative Qwen3Guard substantially enhances model safety while preserving overall output helpfulness; and (2) when integrated into streaming inference pipelines, Stream Qwen3Guard facilitates on-the-fly intervention to ensure safe outputs, without requiring a re-training of the model.
我们对 Qwen3Guard 在一系列多样化的基准测试中进行了全面评估，包括英语、中文和多语言数据集。结果表明，生成式 Qwen3Guard 在检测不同语言中的不安全提示和响应方面优于现有最先进模型。同时，流式 Qwen3Guard 在生成过程中实现了高效的实时安全监控，与生成式 Qwen3Guard 相比，性能下降仅适度。除了性能表现外，我们通过两个应用进一步展示了 Qwen3Guard 的实际效用：(1) 当作为 RLAIF 框架中的反馈信号部署时，生成式 Qwen3Guard 显著增强了模型安全性，同时保留了整体输出的有用性；(2) 当集成到流式推理管道中时，流式 Qwen3Guard 支持实时干预以确保安全输出，无需重新训练模型。

The main contribution of Qwen3Guard include:
Qwen3Guard 的主要贡献包括：

  • •

    Three-tiered Severity Classification: Enables detailed risk assessment by categorizing outputs into safe, controversial, and unsafe severity levels, supporting adaptation to diverse deployment scenarios.


    • 三层严重性分类：通过将输出分为安全、有争议和危险三个严重性级别，实现详细的风险评估，支持适应不同的部署场景。
  • •

    Real-Time Detection: Stream Qwen3Guard is specifically optimized for streaming scenarios, allowing efficient and timely moderation during incremental token generation.


    • 实时检测：Stream Qwen3Guard 特别针对流式场景进行了优化，允许在增量生成标记时进行高效及时的审核。
  • •

    Multilingual Coverage: Qwen3Guard supports 119 languages and dialects, ensuring robust performance in global and cross-lingual applications.


    • 多语言覆盖：Qwen3Guard 支持 119 种语言和方言，确保在全球和跨语言应用中的稳健性能。
  • •

    State-of-the-art Performance: Qwen3Guard achieves state-of-the-art performance on safety benchmarks, excelling in both prompt and response classification across English, Chinese, and multilingual tasks.


    • 前沿性能：Qwen3Guard 在安全基准测试中达到前沿性能，在英语、中文和多语言任务中的提示和响应分类方面均表现优异。

2 Safety Policy  2 安全策略

The Safety Policy constitutes a foundational element in the implementation of Guardrails. By defining the scope of dialogues deemed unsafe or warranting caution, it guides the alignment objective of our guard model. This policy also ensures consistency in the annotation of training data and provides clear criteria for interpreting the Guard’s evaluation results.
安全策略是实施护栏的基础要素。通过定义被认为不安全或需要谨慎对待的对话范围，它指导了我们护栏模型的对齐目标。该策略还确保了训练数据标注的一致性，并为解释护栏的评估结果提供了明确标准。

In Qwen3Guard, the safety policy adheres to the following principles:
在 Qwen3Guard 中，安全策略遵循以下原则：

  1. 1.

    Input/Output Harm Detection: For user inputs, we aim to identify queries that raise potentially harmful topics or attempt to elicit unsafe model responses. For model outputs, we flag content that delivers harmful information or advice to users.


    1. 输入/输出危害检测：对于用户输入，我们旨在识别提出潜在有害话题或试图诱导模型产生不安全响应的查询。对于模型输出，我们标记传递有害信息或建议给用户的内容。
  2. 2.

    Comprehensive Coverage: The defined safety categories should encompass widely recognized societal and ethical safety concerns.


    2. 全面覆盖：定义的安全类别应涵盖广泛公认的社会和伦理安全关切。
  3. 3.

    Severity-Level Adaptability: The policy defines tiered harm severity levels (e.g., Safe, Controversial, Unsafe) that can be selectively enforced based on application-specific risk tolerance.


    3. 严重程度适应性：该策略定义了分层的危害严重程度级别（例如，安全、有争议、不安全），可以根据特定应用的风险容忍度选择性地执行。

In the current version of Qwen3Guard, we consider the following safety categories:
在 Qwen3Guard 的当前版本中，我们考虑以下安全类别：

  • •

    Violent: Content that provides detailed instructions, methods, or advice on how to commit acts of violence, including the manufacture, acquisition, or use of weapons. Also includes depictions of violence.


    • 暴力：提供详细说明、方法或建议如何实施暴力行为的内容，包括制造、获取或使用武器。也包括暴力描绘。
  • •

    Non-violent Illegal Acts: Content providing guidance or advice for non-violent illegal activities like hacking, unauthorized drug production, or stealing.


    • 非暴力非法行为：提供指导或建议进行非暴力非法活动（如黑客攻击、非法药物生产或盗窃）的内容。
  • •

    Sexual Content or Sexual Acts: Content offering any sexual imagery, references, or descriptions featuring individuals. Also includes content that describes explicit sexual imagery, references, or descriptions containing illegal or unethical sexual acts, such as rape, bestiality, incest, and sexual slavery.


    • 性内容或性行为：包含任何性意象、指涉或描述涉及个人的内容。也包括描述明确性意象、指涉或描述包含非法或不道德性行为的内容，如强奸、兽交、乱伦和性奴役。
  • •

    Personally Identifiable Information: Content offering unauthorized sharing or disclosure of sensitive personal identifying information, such as name, ID number, address, phone number, medical records, financial details, and account passwords, etc.


    • 个人身份信息：提供未经授权的分享或披露敏感个人身份信息的内容，如姓名、身份证号、地址、电话号码、医疗记录、财务详情、账户密码等。
  • •

    Suicide & Self-Harm: Content advocating, directly encouraging, or detailing methods for self-harm, suicide, or dangerous activities that could lead to serious injury or death.


    • 自杀与自残：提倡、直接鼓励或详细描述自残、自杀或可能导致严重伤害或死亡的危险活动的内容。
  • •

    Unethical Acts: Any immoral or unethical content or acts, including but not limited to bias, discrimination, stereotype, injustice, hate speech, offensive language, harassment, insults, threat, defamation, extremism, misinformation regarding ethics, and other behaviors that while not illegal are still considered unethical.


    • 不道德行为：任何不道德或违反伦理的内容或行为，包括但不限于偏见、歧视、刻板印象、不公、仇恨言论、攻击性语言、骚扰、侮辱、威胁、诽谤、极端主义、关于伦理的错误信息以及其他虽然不违法但被视为不道德的行为。
  • •

    Politically Sensitive Topics: The deliberate creation or spread of false information about government actions, historical events, or public figures that is demonstrably untrue and poses risk of public deception or social harm.


    • 政治敏感话题：故意制造或传播关于政府行为、历史事件或公众人物的虚假信息，这些信息明显不实，并可能导致公众欺骗或社会危害。
  • •

    Copyright Violation: Content offering unauthorized reproduction, distribution, public display, or derivative use of copyrighted materials, such as novels, scripts, lyrics, and other creative works protected by law, without the explicit permission of the copyright holder.


    • 版权侵权：提供未经授权的复制、分发、公开展示或演绎使用受法律保护的作品（如小说、剧本、歌词和其他创作作品）的内容，而未获得版权持有人的明确许可。
  • •

    Jailbreak (Only for input): Content that explicitly attempts to override the model's system prompt or model conditioning.


    • 越狱攻击（仅适用于输入）：明确尝试绕过模型系统提示或模型条件的内容。

Since Jailbreak attacks are typically carried out via carefully engineered prompts designed to manipulate the model into producing harmful outputs, the "jailbreak" label applies exclusively to input classification. With regard to the output, its specific categorization depends on the potential harm that the generated response may cause.
由于越狱攻击通常是通过精心设计的提示来执行的，这些提示旨在操纵模型以产生有害输出，因此“越狱”标签仅适用于输入分类。对于输出，其具体分类取决于生成的响应可能造成的潜在危害。

Furthermore, we classify potential harms into three severity levels:
此外，我们将潜在危害分为三个严重程度级别：

  • •

    Unsafe: Content generally considered harmful across most scenarios.


    • 不安全：在大多数情况下通常被认为是有害的内容。
  • •

    Controversial: Content whose harmfulness may be context-dependent or subject to disagreement across different applications.


    • 有争议的：内容的有害性可能因上下文而异，或在不同应用中存在分歧。
  • •

    Safe: Content generally considered safe across most scenarios.


    • 安全的：内容在大多数情况下通常被认为是安全的。

Prompt Synthesis  提示合成

To ensure comprehensive coverage of all categories defined in our safety policy, we adopt the Self-Instruct framework (Wang et al., 2023) to synthesize diverse and policy-aligned prompts. Specifically, we first decompose the safety policy into a fine-grained taxonomy, collect seed prompts for each target category, and then prompt LLMs to generate additional relevant examples based on these seeds. To enhance the quality and robustness of the synthesized data, we employ two complementary strategies:
为确保全面覆盖安全政策中定义的所有类别，我们采用 Self-Instruct 框架（Wang 等人，2023）来合成多样化和符合政策的提示。具体来说，我们首先将安全政策分解为细粒度的分类体系，为每个目标类别收集种子提示，然后提示 LLMs 基于这些种子生成额外的相关示例。为了提高合成数据的质量和鲁棒性，我们采用了两种互补的策略：

  • •

    Keyword-guided prompt synthesis. For each safety category, we curate a set of semantically relevant keywords and condition prompt generation on each keyword individually. For instance, when synthesizing prompts related to hazardous explosives, we explicitly instruct the model to incorporate terms such as ``bomb,'' ``TNT,'' ``C4,'' and ``black powder,'' thereby encouraging lexical and topical variation while preserving category alignment.


    • 关键词引导的提示合成。对于每个安全类别，我们精心策划一组语义相关的关键词，并对每个关键词单独进行提示生成。例如，在合成与危险爆炸物相关的提示时，我们明确指示模型包含诸如``炸弹''、``TNT''、``C4''和``黑火药''等术语，从而在保持类别一致性的同时鼓励词汇和主题的多样性。
  • •

To prevent the model from associating safety labels with irrelevant syntactic or lexical cues, we generate positive (safe) and negative (unsafe) prompt pairs that share similar surface structures. For example, alongside the unsafe prompt ``How to make a bomb,'' we generate its safe counterpart ``How to make a cake,'' ensuring that the model does not erroneously classify verbs like ``make'' as inherently unsafe.


    • 正反配对示例。为了防止模型将安全标签与无关的句法或词汇线索关联起来，我们生成具有相似表面结构的正向（安全）和负向（不安全）提示对。例如，对于不安全的提示“如何制造炸弹”，我们生成其对应的安全提示“如何制作蛋糕”，以确保模型不会错误地将“制作”之类的动词归类为本质上不安全的。

However, the current training method still presents several challenges:

    Due to the inherent ambiguity of the ``controversial'' severity level, instances belonging to this category are limited in number in both human-annotated and synthetically generated data.

  • •

Building Controversial Labels
构建有争议的标签

Our preliminary experiments reveal that the label distribution in the training data significantly influences the model's tolerance toward potentially harmful content. For instance, compared to a balanced training set with a 1:1 ratio of Safe to Unsafe samples, doubling the proportion of Safe examples leads the model to become more permissive, causing it to reclassify certain borderline test samples from Unsafe to Safe. This motivates our data rebalancing strategy by intentionally adjust the Safe/Unsafe ratio during training to approximate the decision boundary of the Controversial category.
我们的初步实验表明，训练数据中的标签分布显著影响模型对潜在有害内容的容忍度。例如，与具有 1:1 安全/不安全样本比例的平衡训练集相比，将安全样本比例加倍会导致模型变得更加宽容，使其将某些边缘测试样本从不安全重新分类为安全。这促使我们采用数据再平衡策略，在训练过程中有意调整安全/不安全比例，以近似争议类别的决策边界。

The overall pipeline is illustrated in Figure 3. We begin by evenly partitioning the full training dataset into two disjoint subsets, denoted as Part A and Part B. To mitigate potential overfitting, we train models on one subset and use them to refine annotations on the other.

Specifically, on Part A, we train two models using distinct sampling strategies:

  • •

    PartA-Strict: trained with an enriched proportion of Safe samples,

Consequently, PartA-Strict tends to predict Unsafe, while PartA-Loose tends to predict Safe. The Safe/ Unsafe ratios are calibrated based on the model performance on the most conservative and most permissive on the validation set.
因此，PartA-Strict 倾向于预测不安全，而 PartA-Loose 倾向于预测安全。安全/不安全比例根据模型在验证集上表现最保守和最宽松时的性能进行校准。

We then apply these two models to Part B and assign labels via majority voting. Instances yielding conflicting predictions are labeled as Controversial. Reversing the roles allows us to identify controversial instances in Part A as well. Aggregating the results from both partitions yields the complete set of controversial labels across the entire training dataset.
然后我们将这两个模型应用于 Part B，并通过多数投票分配标签。产生冲突预测的实例被标记为有争议。角色反转使我们也能识别 Part A 中的有争议实例。从两个分区汇总结果，即可获得整个训练数据集中所有有争议标签的完整集合。

Label Distillation  标签蒸馏

After building the controversial label, we further employ a distillation-based approach to refine the dataset. Specifically, we split the dataset into two disjoint subsets again and use models trained on one subset to improve the annotations of the other. In this process, Qwen3-32B serves as the teacher model. Through knowledge distillation, annotation errors are effectively reduced.
在构建有争议的标签后，我们进一步采用基于蒸馏的方法来优化数据集。具体来说，我们将数据集再次分割成两个互不相交的子集，并使用在一个子集上训练的模型来改进另一个子集的标注。在这个过程中，Qwen3-32B 作为教师模型。通过知识蒸馏，标注错误得到有效减少。

4 Stream Qwen3Guard

Current mainstream open-source guard models, as well as Generative Qwen3Guard, assess safety after a response is fully generated, making real-time monitoring during generation almost impossible. To address this, we developed a token-level streaming classifier that evaluates each token as it's generated, categorizing it as safe, unsafe, or potentially controversial in real time.
当前主流的开源防护模型以及生成式 Qwen3Guard，都是在响应完全生成后进行安全评估，使得生成过程中的实时监控几乎不可能。为此，我们开发了一种基于 token 级别的流式分类器，它在每个 token 生成时进行评估，实时将其归类为安全、不安全或可能存在争议。

An overview of Stream Qwen3Guard is illustrated in Figure 7. During a conversation: (1) The user's prompt is simultaneously submitted to both the LLM assistant and Stream Qwen3Guard. Stream Qwen3Guard evaluates the prompt and assigns a safety label; based on this assessment, the upper-level framework determines whether to interrupt the conversation. (2) If the conversation proceeds, the LLM assistant begins generating its response in a streaming fashion. Each output token is immediately forwarded to Stream Qwen3Guard, which performs real-time safety evaluation on a per-token basis, enabling dynamic content moderation throughout the generation process.
Stream Qwen3Guard 的概述如图 7 所示。在对话过程中：(1) 用户的提示同时提交给 LLM 助手和 Stream Qwen3Guard。Stream Qwen3Guard 评估提示并分配安全标签；根据此评估，上层框架决定是否中断对话。(2) 如果对话继续，LLM 助手开始以流式方式生成其响应。每个输出标记立即转发到 Stream Qwen3Guard，该系统对每个标记进行实时安全评估，从而在整个生成过程中实现动态内容审核。

为了支持实时、逐标记地对助手流式输出的内容进行审核，响应流的损失在每个生成的标记处计算。总响应损失 ℒr\mathcal{L}_{r} 是响应中所有 TT 标记的联合风险等级（r-risk）和安全类别（r-cat）预测交叉熵损失的均值：

ℒr=1T​∑t=1T(ℒr-risk(t)+ℒr-cat(t)).\mathcal{L}_{r}=\frac{1}{T}\sum_{t=1}^{T}\left(\mathcal{L}_{\text{r-risk}}^{(t)}+\mathcal{L}_{\text{r-cat}}^{(t)}\right).
Conditional Category Loss
条件类别损失

A conditional mechanism is applied to the safety category losses (ℒq-cat\mathcal{L}_{\text{q-cat}} and ℒr-cat\mathcal{L}_{\text{r-cat}}). Specifically, the category loss is computed only when the corresponding ground-truth risk level is labeled as “unsafe” or “controversial.” If the true risk level is “safe,” the category loss is omitted from the total loss calculation. This ensures the model focuses category prediction efforts only where safety concerns are present.
对安全类别损失（ ℒq-cat\mathcal{L}_{\text{q-cat}} 和 ℒr-cat\mathcal{L}_{\text{r-cat}} ）应用了一种条件机制。具体来说，只有当相应的真实风险级别被标记为“不安全”或“有争议”时，才会计算类别损失。如果真实风险级别为“安全”，则从总损失计算中省略类别损失。这确保了模型只在存在安全问题时才集中精力进行类别预测。

5 Related Work  5 相关工作

With the rapid advancement of Large Language Models (LLMs), safety has become a critical and increasingly prominent research focus. A growing body of work (Choi et al., 2024; Ji et al., 2025; Bai et al., 2022; Li et al., 2025b) has explored diverse strategies across multiple training stages, including supervised fine-tuning (SFT) and reinforcement learning (RL), to develop LLMs that are not only highly capable but also safe and socially responsible.
随着大型语言模型（LLMs）的快速发展，安全已成为一个关键且日益突出的研究重点。越来越多的研究工作（Choi 等人，2024；Ji 等人，2025；Bai 等人，2022；Li 等人，2025b）探索了在多个训练阶段采用多种策略，包括监督微调（SFT）和强化学习（RL），以开发不仅能力强大而且安全、负责任的大型语言模型。

Beyond intrinsic model safety, guard models serve as external safety mechanisms designed to monitor both user inputs and model outputs, enforcing predefined safety policies. Most existing guard models (Inan et al., 2023; Jiang et al., 2024; Zeng et al., 2024; Ghosh et al., 2025) adopt an instruction-following paradigm, leveraging supervised fine-tuning to classify inputs and outputs into discrete safety categories with corresponding labels. More recently, incorporating explicit reasoning capabilities has significantly enhanced guard performance: DuoGuard (Deng et al., 2025) improves safety enforcement through reinforcement learning, while GuardReasoner (Liu et al., 2025) achieves higher classification accuracy by explicitly modeling the reasoning process underlying safety judgments. Current research also has further extended safety moderation into multilingual and multi-modal settings (Upadhayay et al., 2025; Verma et al., 2025; Kumar et al., 2025; Gu et al., 2024).
除了内在模型安全之外，防护模型作为外部安全机制，旨在监控用户输入和模型输出，并执行预定义的安全策略。大多数现有的防护模型（Inan 等人，2023；Jiang 等人，2024；Zeng 等人，2024；Ghosh 等人，2025）采用指令遵循范式，利用监督微调将输入和输出分类到具有相应标签的离散安全类别中。最近，通过整合显式推理能力显著提升了防护性能：DuoGuard（Deng 等人，2025）通过强化学习改进了安全执行，而 GuardReasoner（Liu 等人，2025）通过显式建模安全判断背后的推理过程实现了更高的分类准确率。当前研究还进一步将安全审核扩展到多语言和多模态环境（Upadhayay 等人，2025；Verma 等人，2025；Kumar 等人，2025；Gu 等人，2024）。

Motivated by similar goals, some prior work has explored token-level classification approaches (Sharma et al., 2025; Xuan et al., 2025; Li et al., 2025a). However, these methods typically approximate token-level labels using sentence-level annotations or rely on indirect learning methods. In contrast, during the training of Stream Qwen3Guard, we sample multiple rollouts and leverage predictions from Generative Qwen3Guard to estimate labels for incomplete sentences, thereby obtaining more accurate token-level annotations. To address the challenge of inconsistent safety policies across different contexts, Zhang et al. (2024) propose the concept of a ``controversial'' label, which they annotate using rule-based heuristics. In our approach, we instead derive the controversial labels through ensemble voting among multiple models. Additionally, Zeng et al. (2024) mitigate policy inconsistency from a different angle by dynamically adjusting classifier thresholds.
受相似目标驱动，一些先前工作探索了基于 token 的分类方法（Sharma 等人，2025；Xuan 等人，2025；Li 等人，2025a）。然而，这些方法通常使用句子级标注来近似 token 级标签，或依赖间接学习方法。相比之下，在 Stream Qwen3Guard 的训练过程中，我们采样多个 rollouts，并利用 Generative Qwen3Guard 的预测来估计不完整句子的标签，从而获得更精确的 token 级标注。为了应对不同语境下安全政策不一致的挑战，Zhang 等人（2024）提出了“有争议”标签的概念，并使用基于规则的启发式方法进行标注。在我们的方法中，我们通过多模型集成投票来推导有争议标签。此外，Zeng 等人（2024）从不同角度通过动态调整分类器阈值来缓解政策不一致问题。

6 Conclusion  6 结论

In this work, we introduce Qwen3Guard, a series of multilingual safety classification models designed to enhance content moderation in diverse contexts. Departing from conventional binary safe/unsafe classification, Qwen3Guard introduces a controversial category, enabling more flexible moderation decisions where safety judgments may vary across regions, platforms, or use cases. We present two specialized variants: Generative Qwen3Guard, which reformulates safety classification as a generative task, and Stream Qwen3Guard, which performs token-level safety detection during incremental text generation, thereby enabling real-time intervention and dynamic moderation. Extensive experiments demonstrate that Qwen3Guard achieves strong performance across multiple safety benchmarks, spanning English, Chinese, and multilingual datasets.
在这项工作中，我们介绍了 Qwen3Guard，一系列多语言安全分类模型，旨在提升不同环境下的内容审核能力。不同于传统的安全/不安全二元分类，Qwen3Guard 引入了一个有争议的分类，使审核决策更加灵活，因为安全判断可能因地区、平台或使用场景而异。我们提出了两种专门变体：生成式 Qwen3Guard，将安全分类重新定义为生成任务；流式 Qwen3Guard，在增量文本生成过程中进行分词级安全检测，从而实现实时干预和动态审核。大量实验表明，Qwen3Guard 在多个安全基准测试中均表现出色，涵盖英语、中文和多语言数据集。

AI safety is a complex and ongoing challenge. While Qwen3Guard provides an off-the-shelf moderation tool, it is not a complete solution. We remain committed to advancing more flexible, efficient, and robust safety methods, including enhancing the intrinsic safety of models through architectural and training innovations, as well as developing dynamic, inference-time intervention strategies that can adapt to emerging risks. Looking ahead, our goal is to build AI systems that are not only technically capable but also inherently aligned with human values, ethical norms, and societal expectations. We envision safety frameworks that adapt dynamically alongside advances in AI capabilities and shifting cultural contexts, ensuring responsible, fair, and trustworthy deployment in diverse global settings.
AI 安全是一个复杂且持续的挑战。虽然 Qwen3Guard 提供了一种即用型的内容审核工具，但它并非完整的解决方案。我们仍然致力于推进更灵活、高效和稳健的安全方法，包括通过架构和训练创新来增强模型的内在安全性，以及开发能够在推理时动态干预的策略以适应新兴风险。展望未来，我们的目标是构建不仅技术能力强大，而且与人类价值观、伦理规范和社会期望内在一致的 AI 系统。我们设想安全框架能够随着 AI 能力的进步和文化环境的变化而动态适应，确保在各种全球环境中负责任、公平和值得信赖地部署。

Limitations  局限性

Despite the strong empirical performance of Qwen3Guard, several important limitations remain that warrant careful consideration in real-world deployment:
尽管 Qwen3Guard 具有强大的实证性能，但仍存在一些重要的局限性，在现实世界部署时需要仔细考虑：

Vulnerability to Adversarial Attacks.
易受对抗性攻击。

Like most other guardrail models, Qwen3Guard may be susceptible to adversarial prompt engineering, where malicious users may employ paraphrasing, obfuscation, or context manipulation to bypass safety filters. While our model demonstrates robustness on standard benchmarks, its performance may degrade under sophisticated, targeted attacks.
像大多数其他护栏模型一样，Qwen3Guard 可能容易受到对抗性提示工程的影响，恶意用户可能会采用释义、混淆或上下文操纵来绕过安全过滤器。尽管我们的模型在标准基准测试中表现出鲁棒性，但在复杂、有针对性的攻击下，其性能可能会下降。

Fairness and Bias in Moderation Decisions.
审核决策中的公平性和偏见。

The safety annotations used to train Qwen3Guard inevitably reflect the biases and cultural assumptions embedded in the source datasets. As a result, the model may disproportionately flag content from certain demographic, linguistic, or cultural groups as “unsafe” or “controversial,” even when such content is contextually appropriate. This may warrant careful consideration to ensure fairness and inclusivity, especially in multilingual and multicultural contexts.
用于训练 Qwen3Guard 的安全标注不可避免地反映了源数据集中嵌入的偏见和文化假设。因此，模型可能会不成比例地将来自某些人口统计、语言或文化群体的内容标记为“不安全”或“有争议”，即使这些内容在上下文中是恰当的。这可能需要仔细考虑，以确保公平性和包容性，特别是在多语言和多文化环境中。

Limited Generalization.  泛化能力有限。

While GenerativeGuard benefits from full-context understanding, StreamGuard’s token-level classification head operates under strict latency and partial-context constraints. This architectural trade-off can lead to reduced robustness when handling ambiguous or context-dependent phrases during early generation stages. As a result, StreamGuard may exhibit higher false-positive or false-negative rates in out-of-distribution or low-resource language scenarios, limiting its reliability in highly dynamic or noisy environments.
生成式 Guard 受益于全上下文理解，而流式 Guard 的 token 级分类头则在严格的延迟和部分上下文约束下运行。这种架构权衡在早期生成阶段处理模糊或上下文依赖短语时可能导致鲁棒性降低。因此，在分布外或低资源语言场景中，流式 Guard 可能出现更高的假阳性或假阴性率，限制其在高度动态或嘈杂环境中的可靠性。

Insensitivity to Regional and Cultural Nuances.
对区域和文化细微差别的敏感性不足。

Although Qwen3Guard supports a wide range of languages, its safety judgments may not adequately account for regional legal frameworks, social norms, or cultural sensitivities. The current model does not incorporate geolocation-aware or policy-adaptive mechanisms, which may restrict its effectiveness in localized deployments.
尽管 Qwen3Guard 支持多种语言，但其安全判断可能未能充分考虑区域法律框架、社会规范或文化敏感性。当前模型未包含地理位置感知或策略自适应机制，这可能会限制其在本地化部署中的有效性。

We remain committed to actively addressing these limitations through continued research and iterative improvements to our safety classifiers.
