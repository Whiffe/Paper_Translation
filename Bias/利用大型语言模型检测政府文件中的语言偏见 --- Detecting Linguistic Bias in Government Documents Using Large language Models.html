Detecting Linguistic Bias in Government Documents
Using Large language Models
检测政府文件中的语言偏见 使用大型语言模型

Abstract.  抽象的。

This paper addresses the critical need for detecting bias in government documents, an underexplored area with significant implications for governance. Existing methodologies often overlook the unique context and far-reaching impacts of governmental documents, potentially obscuring embedded biases that shape public policy and citizen-government interactions. To bridge this gap, we introduce the Dutch Government Data for Bias Detection (DGDB), a dataset sourced from the Dutch House of Representatives and annotated for bias by experts. We fine-tune several BERT-based models on this dataset and compare their performance with that of generative language models. Additionally, we conduct a comprehensive error analysis that includes explanations of the models’ predictions. Our findings demonstrate that fine-tuned models achieve strong performance and significantly outperform generative language models, indicating the effectiveness of DGDB  for bias detection. This work underscores the importance of labeled datasets for bias detection in various languages and contributes to more equitable governance practices.
本文旨在解决政府文件中偏见检测这一关键问题，这是一个尚未充分探索但对治理具有重大意义的领域。现有方法往往忽略政府文件的独特背景和深远影响，可能掩盖了影响公共政策和公民与政府互动中存在的隐性偏见。为了弥补这一不足，我们引入了荷兰政府偏见检测数据集（DGDB），该数据集来源于荷兰众议院，并由专家标注了偏见信息。我们基于该数据集对多个基于 BERT 的模型进行了微调，并将其性能与生成式语言模型进行了比较。此外，我们还进行了全面的误差分析，包括对模型预测结果的解释。研究结果表明，微调后的模型性能优异，显著优于生成式语言模型，这表明 DGDB 在偏见检测方面具有显著优势。这项工作强调了标注数据集对于各种语言偏见检测的重要性，并有助于促进更加公平的治理实践。

1. Introduction
1. 引言

Linguistic bias emerges in various forms of communication, including online information and text, particularly when addressing categories of individuals or categorizing individuals (Maass, 1999). It is known to perpetuate stereotypes and increase discrimination, while also decreasing self-esteem, deteriorating mental and physical health, and reducing performance.
语言偏见出现在各种形式的交流中，包括在线信息和文本，尤其是在提及特定群体或对个体进行分类时 （Maass， 1999 ） 。众所周知，语言偏见会加剧刻板印象和歧视，同时还会降低自尊心，损害身心健康，并降低工作表现。

Linguistic bias can be defined as “a systematic asymmetry in word choice as a function of the social category to which the target belongs” (Beukeboom and Burgers, 2017). The words that refer to these social categories, as well as any terms that appear within such asymmetries in the context of these categories are referred to as biased terms. Table 1 lists some example sentences illustrating linguistic bias. The causes of linguistic bias lie with stereotypical expectations of the sender about the category of interest. Notably, linguistic bias may occur even when the sender is unaware of these expectations or when they do not intend to encode them in their communication. These characteristics make linguistic bias important to be mindful of, yet challenging to avoid in practice.
语言偏见可以定义为“一种系统性的用词不对称，这种不对称源于目标对象所属的社会类别” （Beukeboom 和 Burgers， 2017 ） 。指代这些社会类别的词语，以及在这些类别语境中出现的任何不对称词汇，都被称为带有偏见的词汇 。表 1 列举了一些示例句子来说明语言偏见。语言偏见的根源在于发送者对目标类别的刻板印象。值得注意的是，即使发送者没有意识到这些刻板印象，或者无意在沟通中体现这些刻板印象，语言偏见仍然可能发生。这些特点使得语言偏见既值得关注，又难以在实践中完全避免。

Researchers have therefore explored the use of automated tools for the detection of linguistic bias. Initially, these efforts mainly resulted in lists of terms associated with bias (Nosek et al., 2002). Subsequent studies, for example, by Recasens et al. (2013), have highlighted the role of linguistic context in identifying bias in text. Automated detection of bias has gained more prominence with the emergence of pre-trained generative language models (LMs) as these can handle large context windows (Blodgett et al., 2020; Garrido-Muñoz et al., 2021; Gallegos et al., 2024). The rise of generative LMs simultaneously exacerbates the problems associated with linguistic bias in source documents, as they may repeat or even amplify existing biases in these documents. Generative LMs therefore present both new opportunities to study bias in source documents while also imposing novel risks of perpetuating existing biases.
因此，研究人员探索了利用自动化工具检测语言偏见的方法。最初，这些努力主要产生了与偏见相关的术语列表（Nosek et al, 2002）。随后的研究，例如 Recasens 等人（2013）的研究，强调了语言上下文在识别文本偏见中的作用。随着预训练生成式语言模型（LM）的出现，偏见的自动检测变得更加重要，因为这些模型可以处理较大的上下文窗口（Blodgett et al, 2020; Garrido-Muñoz et al, 2021; Gallegos et al, 2024）。生成式语言模型的兴起同时也加剧了源文档中与语言偏见相关的问题，因为它们可能会重复甚至放大这些文档中已存在的偏见。因此，生成式语言模型既为研究源文档中的偏见提供了新的机遇，也带来了延续现有偏见的新风险。

All efforts to automatically detect bias in linguistic texts benefit from the development of high-quality and realistic examples of biased text, and several datasets have been developed for this purpose. Most of these have focused on publicly available encyclopedia and news articles in English (Recasens et al., 2013; Hube and Fetahu, 2018; Spinde et al., 2021),
所有旨在自动检测语言文本中偏见的努力都受益于高质量、真实的偏见文本示例的开发，为此已经开发了多个数据集。其中大多数数据集都集中于公开的英文百科全书和新闻文章（Recasens et al, 2013; Hube and Fetahu, 2018; Spinde et al, 2021）。

but the creation of datasets that contain real-world examples of bias in various underrepresented languages and outside of the domain of news articles and social media remains challenging.
但是，创建包含各种代表性不足的语言以及新闻文章和社交媒体领域之外的真实世界偏见示例的数据集仍然具有挑战性。

Existing methodologies focus on linguistic features of texts in general, often overlooking the distinctive context and broad societal impacts of government documents. This oversight can obscure how embedded biases in such texts not only perpetuate inequalities but also shape public policy and citizen-government interactions in profound ways. In this paper, we identify and address the pressing need for bias detection in governmental documents, an area that remains underexplored despite its significant implications for governance and public administration, and therefore on the UN goals for sustainable development. By focusing on government texts, this research aims to unveil the specific biases that influence legislative frameworks and policy directives, paving the way for more equitable governance practices. Our approach seeks to refine existing analytical tools to better understand and mitigate bias, ensuring that government communications uphold fairness and integrity, which is crucial to maintaining public trust and democratic ideals.
现有方法论侧重于文本的语言特征，往往忽略了政府文件的独特语境及其广泛的社会影响。这种疏忽可能掩盖了此类文本中固有的偏见如何不仅加剧不平等，而且深刻地影响着公共政策和公民与政府的互动。本文旨在识别并解决政府文件中偏见检测的迫切需求。尽管政府文件对治理和公共管理，乃至联合国可持续发展目标都具有重大意义，但这一领域的研究仍然不足。本研究聚焦于政府文本，旨在揭示影响立法框架和政策指令的具体偏见，从而为更加公平的治理实践铺平道路。我们的方法力求改进现有的分析工具，以更好地理解和减少偏见，确保政府沟通秉持公平和诚信，这对于维护公众信任和民主理念至关重要。

In this paper, we propose a methodology for creating bias detection datasets that generalize to various languages by utilizing publicly available government documents. We apply this methodology to create a bias detection dataset in the Dutch language and use the resulting dataset to train and evaluate the bias detection capabilities of various LMs in two regimes: an in-domain regime where sentences with biased terms are present during training and an out-of-domain regime where the models are evaluated on sentences with biased terms that were held out during training.
本文提出了一种利用公开的政府文件创建可推广至多种语言的偏见检测数据集的方法。我们将此方法应用于荷兰语偏见检测数据集的创建，并使用该数据集在两种情况下训练和评估各种语言模型的偏见检测能力：一种是领域内环境，即在训练过程中使用包含偏见词的句子；另一种是领域外环境，即在训练过程中排除包含偏见词的句子，并在这些句子上评估模型。

In our evaluations, we find that trained models significantly outperform untrained models on this task, including pre-trained generative LMs. This implies that DGDB  is sufficiently rich to learn how to address the challenging task of detecting bias based on the linguistic context. It thereby not only contributes to the academic discourse on linguistic bias in general and in government texts specifically, but also serves as a vital resource for policymakers and educators aiming to create more equitable societies.
在我们的评估中，我们发现训练过的模型在该任务上的表现显著优于未经训练的模型，包括预训练的生成式语言模型。这意味着 DGDB 数据库足够丰富，能够学习如何应对基于语言上下文检测偏见这一具有挑战性的任务。因此，它不仅有助于学术界对语言偏见（尤其是政府文本中的语言偏见）的探讨，而且还能为致力于构建更加公平社会的政策制定者和教育工作者提供重要的资源。

Finally, our findings highlight the importance of specialized datasets for bias detection, especially in the presence of large generative LMs. The presented models can be used to detect bias in Dutch text in practice, the dataset can be used to further study how to protect underrepresented communities under Dutch governance, while the presented approach to collect this dataset may inspire the development of additional bias detection datasets in other underrepresented languages. The methodologies introduced for creating a bias detection dataset and for training bias detection models may thus have utility across linguistic boundaries in fostering fair and unbiased communication.
最后，我们的研究结果强调了专门数据集对于偏见检测的重要性，尤其是在存在大型生成式语言模型的情况下。本文提出的模型可用于实际检测荷兰语文本中的偏见，该数据集可用于进一步研究如何在荷兰治理体系下保护弱势群体，同时，本文提出的数据集收集方法或可启发其他弱势语言中偏见检测数据集的开发。因此，本文提出的用于创建偏见检测数据集和训练偏见检测模型的方法，在促进公平公正的交流方面，或可跨越语言界限发挥作用。

2. Related Work
2. 相关工作

We examine related datasets and models for bias detection and compare them to our contributions. Table 2 summarizes various efforts, detailing the specific tasks addressed, data modalities, and dataset sizes.
我们考察了相关的数据集和偏差检测模型，并将它们与我们的研究成果进行比较。表 2 总结了各项研究工作，详细列出了具体任务、数据模态和数据集规模。

Among the tasks explored in prior work, some datasets are dedicated specifically to gender bias detection and hate speech detection. These can be considered subtasks of the broader bias detection field: gender bias focuses on a particular target group, while hate speech is typically intentional. In contrast, general bias detection encompasses various target groups and may involve both intentional and unintentional biases. Table 1 presents examples that highlight the differences between these concepts. Additionally, Table 2 includes datasets designed to study the existence of biases and stereotypes in generative LMs. While these datasets contain labelled sentences, they often consist of (masked) sentences used to test whether generative LMs prefer certain tokens, thereby measuring the inherent bias in these models.
在以往研究中探索的任务中，一些数据集专门用于性别偏见检测和仇恨言论检测。这些可以被视为更广泛的偏见检测领域的子任务：性别偏见侧重于特定目标群体，而仇恨言论通常是故意的。相比之下，一般偏见检测涵盖各种目标群体，并且可能涉及有意和无意的偏见。表 1 列举了一些示例，突显了这些概念之间的差异。此外，表 2 还包含一些旨在研究生成式语言模型中是否存在偏见和刻板印象的数据集。虽然这些数据集包含已标注的句子，但它们通常由（经过掩码处理的）句子组成，用于测试生成式语言模型是否偏好某些词元，从而衡量这些模型中固有的偏见。

Existing datasets targeting bias detection are generally of moderate size and are derived from news articles and encyclopedias in English. There are two main reasons for this. First, these sources are extensively used by the computational linguistics and natural language processing communities and are therefore readily accessible. Second, these sources typically strive to exclude linguistic biases from their content to some degree, making the detection of bias both technically challenging and practically relevant. The texts in our dataset are also assumed to be written without intentional bias but are sourced from government documents rather than the commonly used sources within the community.
现有的用于偏见检测的数据集规模通常适中，且大多来源于英文新闻文章和百科全书。这主要有两个原因。首先，这些资源被计算语言学和自然语言处理领域广泛使用，因此易于获取。其次，这些资源通常会在一定程度上排除语言偏见，使得偏见检测在技术上具有挑战性，在实践中也具有重要意义。我们数据集中的文本也被假定为无偏见撰写，但其来源是政府文件，而非该领域常用的资源。

Several models have been proposed for the detection of linguistic bias. Some focus on detecting bias at the word level (Recasens et al., 2013; Hube and Fetahu, 2018; Spinde et al., 2021), while others emphasize the importance of context in bias detection (Kuang and Davison, 2016; Recasens et al., 2013; Bartl et al., 2020; Zhang et al., 2020). While word-level bias detection aids in understanding specific linguistic markers of bias, utilizing context is crucial for detecting subtle forms of bias and is generally considered more suitable for bias detection tasks. Therefore, we focus on models that take linguistic context into account when detecting bias within DGDB.
已有多种模型被提出用于检测语言偏见。一些模型侧重于在词级层面检测偏见（Recasens et al, 2013; Hube and Fetahu, 2018; Spinde et al, 2021），而另一些模型则强调语境在偏见检测中的重要性（Kuang and Davison, 2016; Recasens et al, 2013; Bartl et al, 2020; Zhang et al, 2020）。词级偏见检测有助于理解特定的语言偏见标记，但利用语境对于检测细微的偏见形式至关重要，并且通常被认为更适合偏见检测任务。因此，我们重点关注在 DGDB 中检测偏见时考虑语言语境的模型。

3. DGDB: a Bias Detection Dataset from Government Documents
3. DGDB：一个基于政府文件的偏见检测数据集

This section details the collection, annotation, and compilation of publicly available government documents into the Dutch Government Data for Bias Detection (DGDB) dataset. The dataset was created through a multistep process, as illustrated in Figure 1.
本节详细介绍了如何收集、标注和汇编公开的政府文件，最终形成荷兰政府偏见检测数据集（DGDB）。该数据集的创建过程分为多个步骤，如图 1 所示。

Initially, we defined various types of linguistic bias to develop a comprehensive list of associated keywords. These keywords were utilized to query publicly available government documents from the Dutch House of Representatives (Tweede Kamer). From these documents, sentences containing bias-related terms and their contextual information were extracted, mined, and normalized. This process resulted in a collection of 42,800 matches spanning over 238,000 pages, which were then deduplicated to ensure data quality.
首先，我们定义了各种类型的语言偏见，并据此构建了一个全面的相关关键词列表。这些关键词被用于查询荷兰众议院（Tweede Kamer）的公开政府文件。从这些文件中，我们提取、挖掘并规范化了包含偏见相关术语及其上下文信息的句子。最终，我们获得了涵盖超过 238,000 页的 42,800 个匹配结果，随后对这些数据进行了去重处理，以确保数据质量。

Subsequently, 12,076 sentences identified as at risk of bias were sampled and annotated by a team of 14 expert annotators specializing in discrimination, racism, and bias detection. Through iterative sampling and expert labeling, a final dataset of 3,632 sentences with annotations was compiled. This meticulous process ensures that the dataset accurately reflects various forms of bias as defined in our guidelines. 随后，我们抽取了 12,076 个被识别为存在偏见风险的句子，并由一个由 14 位专家组成的团队进行标注，这些专家专门从事歧视、种族主义和偏见检测。通过迭代抽样和专家标注，最终构建了一个包含 3,632 个带标注句子的数据集。这一严谨的过程确保了数据集能够准确反映我们指南中定义的各种形式的偏见。数据集 （ https://huggingface.co/datasets/milenamileentje/Dutch-Government-Data-for-Bias-detection/ ） 、 模型 （ https://huggingface.co/milenamileentje/BiasBERTje ） 和代码 （ https://github.com/milenadeswart/Datalab_PDR/tree/Milena ） 均已公开提供。

3.1. Bias Definition
3.1. 偏见定义

A comprehensive keyword list was created by integrating established sources that advocate for social inclusion and address racism, discrimination, and bias within the fields of cultural policy and art curation. These sources were developed in collaboration with advisory panels representing diverse perspectives and backgrounds (Modest and Schoonderwoerd, 2018; Samuel, 2021). The list includes examples and detailed clarifications to distinguish between biased and unbiased language usage.
我们整合了倡导社会包容、关注文化政策和艺术策展领域内种族主义、歧视和偏见的权威资源，创建了一份全面的关键词列表。这些资源是在与代表不同观点和背景的顾问小组合作下制定的 （Modest 和 Schoonderwoerd， 2018 ；Samuel， 2021 ） 。该列表包含示例和详细解释，以区分带有偏见和不带偏见的语言使用。

The terms used in this work are grouped into one of nine categories to express the kind of bias they signal: algemeen (general), beperkingen (disabilities), cultuur (culture), geloof (religion), gender (gender), kolonialisme (colonialism), migratie (migration), onderwijs (education), and seksualiteit (sexuality). Among these categories, kolonialisme is the largest with 28 terms, and cultuur contains the fewest terms, with only three. In most cases, the context in which these terms are used is essential for identifying the presence of bias.
本文中使用的术语被分为九类，以表达它们所代表的偏见类型： algemeen （一般的）， beperkingen （残疾）、 cultuur （文化）、 geloof （宗教）、 gender （性别）、 kolonialisme （殖民主义）、 migratie （移民）、 onderwijs （教育）和 seksualiteit （性欲）。 在这些类别中， “殖民主义”一词最多，有 28 个词条； “文化” 一词最少，只有 3 个。在大多数情况下，这些词条的使用语境对于识别是否存在偏见至关重要。

To streamline the annotation process, the terms were systematically organized into three distinct groups:
为了简化标注过程，术语被系统地分为三个不同的组：

Prohibited:   禁止：

These terms should only be used in direct quotations or to explain why they are harmful; otherwise, they should be avoided. In many cases, alternatives are available, such as replacing gehandicapte (handicapped person) with persoon met beperkingen (person with disabilities).
这些词语仅应用于直接引用或解释其危害性时；否则，应避免使用。在许多情况下，有替代词语可供选择，例如用 “persoon met beperkingen” （残疾人）代替 “gehandicapte” （残疾人）。

Conditionally biased:   条件性偏见：

Terms that exhibit bias depending on the context. This includes (near-)homonyms, such as the noun Christen (Christian) referring to ‘individual(s) subscribing to a particular religion’ and the adjective Christelijk (Christian), which may signify a specific group and could indicate bias based on its usage.
根据语境不同，某些词语会带有偏见。这包括（近）同音词，例如名词 Christen （基督徒）指“信奉特定宗教的人”，而形容词 Christelijk （基督徒的）可能指代特定群体，其用法可能暗示偏见。

Context sensitive:   上下文相关：

Terms where the potential for bias depends heavily on the context, e.g. which are neutral in isolation but can become problematic in certain contexts, such as achtergrond (origin), ouders (parents), man (man), etc.
有些词语的偏见可能性很大程度上取决于上下文，例如，有些词语单独来看是中性的，但在某些上下文中可能会出现问题，例如 achtergrond （起源）、 ouders （父母）、 man （男人）等。

These categories were only used to facilitate the annotation process, and are not part of the bias detection task or DGDB. The comprehensive list of terms can be found in the Appendix A.

3.2. Data Collection
3.2 数据收集

After compiling a list of 120 keywords, Dutch government data from the Dutch House of Representatives (Tweede Kamer) were queried to identify documents containing one or more matches (step 1, highlighted in blue in Figure 1). To ensure consistency, relevance, and alignment with contemporary discourse, we focused on documents from 2010 onward, restricting the collection to those less than 15 years old.
在编制了一份包含 120 个关键词的列表后，我们查询了荷兰众议院（Tweede Kamer）的荷兰政府数据，以识别包含一个或多个匹配项的文件（步骤 1，图 1 中蓝色高亮部分）。为了确保一致性、相关性以及与当代论述的契合度，我们重点关注 2010 年及以后的文件，并将收集范围限定在 15 年以内的文献。

A total of 42,800 matches were identified, spanning over 238,000 pages. These documents were scraped, deduplicated, and normalized, resulting in 12,076 sentences flagged as potentially biased. The textual context of each match was preserved throughout the process. Specifically, all documents were first collected and split into sentences, with capitalization removed for uniformity.
共识别出 42,800 个匹配项，涵盖超过 238,000 页。这些文档经过抓取、去重和规范化处理，最终筛选出 12,076 个可能存在偏见的句子。在整个过程中，每个匹配项的文本上下文均被保留。具体而言，所有文档首先被收集并拆分成句子，为了统一格式，所有句子均被去掉大小写。

收集的文件主要包括正式的政府文件，包括部长备忘录（ Nota ）、解释性备忘录（ Memories van Toelichting ）、部长报告（ Rapporten ）以及这些文件的附件。

3.3. Data Annotation
3.3 数据标注

Data annotation (step 2 marked in red in Figure 1) was conducted by a team of 14 experts from government employees. These annotators were all native speakers, familiar with government documents, and specialized in areas such as discrimination, racism, and bias. Participation in the annotation process was voluntary.
数据标注（图 1 中以红色标记的步骤 2）由一个由 14 名政府雇员组成的专家团队完成。这些标注员均为母语人士，熟悉政府文件，并专长于歧视、种族主义和偏见等领域。参与标注过程完全出于自愿。

The annotators followed to the set of guidelines for identifying bias in sentences, as outlined in Section 3.1. These guidelines, agreed upon by the annotation team, were adapted from those of Nangia et al. (2020). The definitions were designed to capture a broad spectrum of bias, ranging from subtle and possibly unintended language variations to more explicit forms, such as hate speech (Fortuna and Nunes, 2018). For example, sentences containing positive prejudices (e.g., ”women are more emotionally mature”) were labeled as biased if they implied negative prejudices against other groups (e.g., ”men are less or not emotionally mature”).
标注人员遵循第 3.1 节概述的句子偏见识别指南。这些指南由标注团队共同制定，并参考了 Nangia 等人（2020）的研究成果。这些定义旨在涵盖广泛的偏见形式，从细微的、可能并非有意为之的语言差异，到更明确的形式，例如仇恨言论 （Fortuna 和 Nunes， 2018 ） 。例如，如果包含正面偏见的句子（例如，“女性在情感上更成熟”）暗示了对其他群体的负面偏见（例如，“男性在情感上不太成熟”或“男性在情感上不成熟”），则这些句子会被标记为带有偏见 。

The annotation process was divided into three separate annotation sessions. Each session began with discussions to align definitions and refine processes, followed by a training session to introduce the project context and clarify annotation guidelines. A playtest labeling session with an initial set of seven annotators was conducted to test and refine the instructions. Based on feedback, additional examples were incorporated into the guidelines to improve clarity and reduce ambiguity. Annotators participated in sessions based on availability, ensuring that each session included experts on bias, discrimination, and racism.
标注过程分为三个独立的标注环节。每个环节都以讨论开始，旨在统一定义并完善流程，随后进行培训，介绍项目背景并阐明标注指南。我们还组织了一次由七位初始标注员参与的测试标注环节，以检验并完善标注指南。根据反馈，我们在指南中加入了更多示例，以提高清晰度并减少歧义。标注员根据自身时间安排参与各个环节，确保每个环节都有偏见、歧视和种族主义方面的专家参与。

The annotators were asked to assign a label 0 (not biased), a label 1 (biased), a label 2 (unsure), or a label 3 (to be excluded from the dataset). Sentences labeled as 2 (unsure) were later re-annotated to ensure precision. Annotators were encouraged to discuss challenging cases collaboratively, fostering consistency and alignment across the labeled dataset.
标注者被要求给句子分配标签：0（ 无偏见 ）、1（ 有偏见 ）、2（ 不确定 ）或 3（从数据集中排除 ）。被标记为 2（ 不确定 ）的句子随后会被重新标注，以确保准确性。标注者被鼓励协作讨论疑难案例，从而提高标注数据集的一致性和规范性。

The annotation sessions proceeded as follows. In the first session, seven annotators labeled a random sample of 1,800 instances. In the second session, eight annotators, including three from the first session, labeled 1,547 randomly selected instances. The final session focused on increasing the diversity of terms, annotating 1,007 instances containing terms not yet present in the labeled dataset.
标注过程如下。在第一阶段，七位标注员对随机抽取的1800个实例进行了标注。在第二阶段，八位标注员（其中三位来自第一阶段）对随机抽取的1547个实例进行了标注。最后一阶段的重点是增加术语的多样性，标注了1007个包含尚未出现在已标注数据集中的术语的实例。

Annotators reported that the task was challenging, particularly due to the nuanced nature of the definitions and due to unfamiliarity with the task. However, refinements to the guidelines and the inclusion of additional examples after playtesting made the process more manageable.
标注员反映这项任务颇具挑战性，尤其因为定义的细微差别以及他们对任务本身的不熟悉。然而，经过测试后，指南的完善和更多示例的加入使整个过程变得更加易于管理。

For RQ2, we evaluate model generalization by training in two distinct regimes:

6. Limitations
6. 局限性

While this study provides valuable insights, it is important to acknowledge certain limitations that offer avenues for future research. The data collection methodology presented here is adaptable to other languages. However, it requires the availability of government data for querying, which may vary across different regions. Additionally, our approach relies on a predefined seed list of bias terms, which presents two key considerations. First, the seed list may not be exhaustive, potentially omitting some forms of bias from the resulting dataset. Nonetheless, this challenge persists even without a seed list, as perceptions and manifestations of bias evolve over time. Second, the development and consensus on such a word list are essential for consistent application. We consider this limitation to be relatively minor, as the seed list used can serve as a foundation for creating comprehensive bias term lists in other languages through (automated) translation. Overall, these limitations highlight opportunities to enhance the methodology and extend its applicability to diverse linguistic and cultural contexts, thereby strengthening the robustness and inclusivity of bias detection efforts.
尽管本研究提供了宝贵的见解，但必须承认其存在一些局限性，这些局限性也为未来的研究提供了方向。本文提出的数据收集方法可以应用于其他语言。然而，它需要政府数据的可用性，而不同地区的政府数据可用性可能有所不同。此外，我们的方法依赖于预先定义的偏见词汇种子列表，这带来了两个关键问题。首先，该种子列表可能并不完整，最终数据集可能会遗漏某些形式的偏见。然而，即使没有种子列表，这一挑战依然存在，因为人们对偏见的认知和表现形式会随着时间推移而演变。其次，此类词汇列表的制定和共识对于其一致应用至关重要。我们认为这一局限性相对较小，因为所使用的种子列表可以作为基础，通过（自动）翻译在其他语言中创建全面的偏见词汇列表。总而言之，这些局限性凸显了改进方法并将其适用范围扩展到不同语言和文化背景的机会，从而增强偏见检测工作的稳健性和包容性。

8. Conclusion
8. 结论

In this study, we introduce the DGDB, a novel dataset comprising 3,747 annotated sentences sourced from the Dutch House of Representatives. This dataset was utilized to train and evaluate four variations of BERT-based models, demonstrating its effectiveness in identifying linguistic bias and providing insights into language variations associated with bias.
本研究引入了 DGDB 数据集，这是一个包含 3747 条标注句子的全新数据集，这些句子来源于荷兰众议院。我们利用该数据集训练并评估了四种基于 BERT 的模型，结果表明该数据集能够有效识别语言偏见，并深入揭示与偏见相关的语言差异。

Our results indicate that government documents are a viable source for bias detection data, underscoring the complexity of the bias detection task. The fine-tuned BERT-based models achieved strong performance in the in-domain regime, highlighting the importance of specialized, labeled datasets for bias detection, especially when leveraging LMs. Although performance declined in the out-of-domain regime, models still surpassed the random baseline, suggesting that while bias detection is achievable, enhancing generalization remains a challenge. Additionally, our investigation into resampling strategies revealed that undersampling the majority class improves model performance, emphasizing the value of balanced, high-quality datasets over larger, imbalanced ones.
我们的研究结果表明，政府文件是偏见检测数据的有效来源，这也凸显了偏见检测任务的复杂性。经过微调的基于 BERT 的模型在领域内表现出色，强调了专门标注的数据集对于偏见检测的重要性，尤其是在利用语言模型（LM）时。尽管在领域外性能有所下降，但模型仍然优于随机基线，这表明偏见检测虽然可行，但提高泛化能力仍然是一个挑战。此外，我们对重采样策略的研究表明，对多数类进行欠采样可以提高模型性能，强调了平衡的高质量数据集比规模更大、不平衡的数据集更有价值。

The DGDB dataset and the trained models presented in this work hold potential for reducing linguistic bias in governmental communications by serving as tools for bias monitoring and correction. By implementing such bias detection mechanisms, government bodies can promote more equitable and unbiased policy-making and governance.
本文提出的 DGDB 数据集和训练模型有望通过作为偏见监测和纠正工具，减少政府沟通中的语言偏见。通过实施此类偏见检测机制，政府机构可以促进更加公平公正的政策制定和治理。

Future work should focus on expanding DGDB to encompass a broader range of languages and cultural contexts, thereby enhancing the generalizability of bias detection models. Additionally, incorporating more labels and integrating uncertainty quantification frameworks can further improve model reliability and user trust. Combining these models with interactive classification systems can also facilitate real-time bias detection and mitigation in governmental documents (Angelopoulos et al., 2024; den Hengst et al., 2024).
未来的工作应着重扩展 DGDB，使其涵盖更广泛的语言和文化背景，从而增强偏见检测模型的泛化能力。此外，纳入更多标签并整合不确定性量化框架可以进一步提高模型的可靠性和用户信任度。将这些模型与交互式分类系统相结合，还可以促进政府文件中偏见的实时检测和缓解（Angelopoulos et al, 2024; den Hengst et al, 2024）。
